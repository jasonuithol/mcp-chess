Srdja Matovic [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Srdja Matovic**,  
a German born, Montenegrin computer scientist, software developer, and former member of the Internet Technologies Group at the [Hamburg University of Applied Sciences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamburg_University_of_Applied_Sciences) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As computer chess programmer, Srdja is author of the **Zeta** family of chess engines, which are [Zeta Dva](/Zeta_Dva "Zeta Dva"), a conventional engine written in plain [C](/C "C"), [Zeta](/Zeta "Zeta"), written in [OpenCL](/OpenCL "OpenCL"), a language suited for [GPUs](/GPU "GPU"), and the [6502](/6502 "6502") [retro program](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Retro_style) [Zeta Vintage](/index.php?title=Zeta_Vintage&action=edit&redlink=1 "Zeta Vintage (page does not exist)") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
