The **12th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from April 16 to 18, and 21 to 23, [1997](/Timeline#1997 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).Winner with a 100% score was [Yona Kosashvili](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yona_Kosashvili), followed by [Yasser Seirawan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yasser_Seirawan) and [Johan van Mil](http://www.chessvibes.com/reports/im-johan-van-mil-passed-away) with 5½/6 each.Best programs were [Kallisto](/Kallisto "Kallisto") and [Rebel](/Rebel "Rebel") [[2]](#cite_note-2) with 4½.
