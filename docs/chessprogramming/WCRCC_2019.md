The **Thirteenth Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship**, December 7/8, [2019](/Timeline#2019 "Timeline").
The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4) to play on-line via [ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)").
[DeusX](/Deus_X "Deus X") won the playoff versus [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo").
