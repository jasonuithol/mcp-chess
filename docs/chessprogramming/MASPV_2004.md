The **Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye** (MASPV) **2004** was held from May 28 to 30, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline"), at Kék Duna boat house [[1]](#cite_note-1), [Budapest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budapest), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary), near by the banks of [Danube](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Danube).
