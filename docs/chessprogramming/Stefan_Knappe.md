**Stefan Knappe**,  
a German economist, club chess player [[2]](#cite_note-2) and avocational computer chess programmer.
He is author of the chess program [Matador](/Matador "Matador"), which also participated as *Lokalmatador* [[3]](#cite_note-3) at the [BELCT 2001](/BELCT_2001 "BELCT 2001") in [Berlin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin).
Along with [Dieter Bürßner](/Dieter_B%C3%BCr%C3%9Fner "Dieter Bürßner") et al., Stefan was member of the *ChessCoach - Team*, developing chess training software for [Palm](/index.php?title=Palm&action=edit&redlink=1 "Palm (page does not exist)") and [Pocket-PC](/index.php?title=Pocket-PC&action=edit&redlink=1 "Pocket-PC (page does not exist)")
[[4]](#cite_note-4),
also affiliated with the *Inside Chess* project focusing on [web development](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Web_development) [[5]](#cite_note-5).
