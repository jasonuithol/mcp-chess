**Stephen Joseph J. Smith**,  
an American computer scientist and lead programmer [[2]](#cite_note-2) at *Great Game Products*, the makers of '*Bridge Baron*, the 1997 [world champion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_bridge#World_Computer-Bridge_Championship) of [computer bridge](/index.php?title=Bridge&action=edit&redlink=1 "Bridge (page does not exist)"), where he along with [Dana S. Nau](/Dana_S._Nau "Dana S. Nau") and [Thomas A. Throop](/Thomas_A._Throop "Thomas A. Throop") developed the [AI-planning](/Planning "Planning") techniques [[3]](#cite_note-3).
He holds a Ph.D. in 1997 from [University of Maryland, College Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Maryland,_College_Park) on search and planning applied to Bridge [[4]](#cite_note-4), and co-authored several papers on that topic as well on [forward pruning](/Pruning "Pruning") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
