The **CSVN 46th Programmers Tournament**, the [Theo van der Storm](/Theo_van_der_Storm "Theo van der Storm") Cup [[1]](#cite_note-1), took place at May 17-18, [2014](/Timeline#2014 "Timeline"), [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden).
