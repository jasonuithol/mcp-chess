# Alexander Pokrovski

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Alexander_Pokrovski

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Alexander Pokrovski**

**Alexander Pokrovski**, (Aleksandr Pokrovsky [[1]](#cite_note-1)),  
a Russian chess programmer, and author of the [PC](/IBM_PC "IBM PC") chess program [Algir](/Algir "Algir"), which participated at the [First Soviet Computer-Chess Championship 1988](/First_Soviet_Computer-Chess_Championship_1988 "First Soviet Computer-Chess Championship 1988") in [Ulan-Ude](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ulan-Ude) [[2]](#cite_note-2) and the [First International Chess-Computer Tournament in the USSR 1989](/First_International_Chess-Computer_Tournament_in_the_USSR_1989 "First International Chess-Computer Tournament in the USSR 1989") in [Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow) [[3]](#cite_note-3) .
