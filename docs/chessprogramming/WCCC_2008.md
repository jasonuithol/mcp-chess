The **Sixteenth World Computer Chess Championship** took place from September 28 to October 5, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"), Beijing Golden Century Golf Club, [Qinglonghu Township](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_township-level_divisions_of_Beijing#Fangshan_District), [Fangshan District](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fangshan_District), [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing), [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China), in conjunction with the [13th Computer Olympiad](/13th_Computer_Olympiad "13th Computer Olympiad") and the [6th International Conference on Computers and Games](/CG_2008 "CG 2008"). After the [disqualification](/World_Computer_Chess_Championship#RybkaDisqualification "World Computer Chess Championship") of [Rybka](/Rybka "Rybka") in June 2011, [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") is World Computer Chess Champion 2008 [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
