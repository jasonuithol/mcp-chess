The **Third Stockholm Micro Computer Chess Tournament** was held November 23-29, [1981](/Timeline#1981 "Timeline") in [Stockholm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockholm), Sweden, organized by the Swedish [ICCA](/ICCA "ICCA"), forerunner of the [SSDF](/SSDF "SSDF").
