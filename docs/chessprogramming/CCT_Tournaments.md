# CCT Tournaments

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/CCT_Tournaments

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* CCT Tournaments**

The Programmer's **Computer Chess Tournament** CCT are Online Computer Chess Tournaments played via the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") (ICC) [[1]](#cite_note-1) , and since 2010 via the [Free Internet Chess Server](/index.php?title=Free_Internet_Chess_Server&action=edit&redlink=1 "Free Internet Chess Server (page does not exist)") (FICS) [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
