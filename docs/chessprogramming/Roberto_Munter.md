Roberto Munter [[1]](#cite_note-about-1)

**Roberto Munter**,  
an Italien industrial consultant and computer chess programmer. He is author of the [Ippolit](/Ippolit "Ippolit") and [IvanHoe](/IvanHoe "IvanHoe") derivatives [Deep Saros](/Deep_Saros "Deep Saros"), [Elektro](/Elektro "Elektro")
[[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3) and [OldSnake](/index.php?title=OldSnake&action=edit&redlink=1 "OldSnake (page does not exist)")
[[4]](#cite_note-4),
and primary author of [Vitruvius](/Vitruvius "Vitruvius") [[1]](#cite_note-about-1).
