The **1993 QMW Uniform-Platform Computer Chess Championship** was held August 2-6, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline") at [Queen Mary and Westfield College](/Queen_Mary,_University_of_London "Queen Mary, University of London") of [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), initiated and organized by [Don Beal](/Don_Beal "Don Beal"). All games were played automatically.
