Vladan Vučković [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Vladan V. Vučković**,  
a Serbian computer scientist at the Faculty of Electronic Engineering, Computer Department, [University of Niš](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Ni%C5%A1). As computer chess programmer Vladan Vučković is author of the chess programs [Axon](/Axon "Axon") and [Achilles](/Achilles "Achilles") [[2]](#cite_note-2), supported by [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author") [Đorđe Vidanović](/%C4%90or%C4%91e_Vidanovi%C4%87 "Đorđe Vidanović").
