The **10th International Computers and Games Conference** was held on July 9 and 10 [[1]](#cite_note-1), [2018](/Timeline#2018 "Timeline"), College of Public Affairs, San-Shia Campus, [National Taiwan University](/National_Taiwan_University "National Taiwan University"), [New Taipei City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Taipei_City), [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan), in conjunction with the [21st Computer Olympiad](/index.php?title=21st_Computer_Olympiad&action=edit&redlink=1 "21st Computer Olympiad (page does not exist)").
