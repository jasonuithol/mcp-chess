The **13th Dutch Computer Chess Championship** took place November 6-7 and 13-14, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline") in [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
