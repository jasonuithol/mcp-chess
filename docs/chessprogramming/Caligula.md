Caligula [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Caligula**,  
a chess program by [Gerardo Castaño Recio](/Gerardo_Casta%C3%B1o_Recio "Gerardo Castaño Recio") and successor of [Toledo 2000](/Toledo_2000 "Toledo 2000").
Caligula played a strong [6th Spanish Open Computer Chess Championship 1999](/SCCC_1999 "SCCC 1999") and became Spanish Champion and runner-up behind [Chess Tiger](/Chess_Tiger "Chess Tiger"), winning from Tiger [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [Diep](/Diep "Diep"), but losing from its countrymen programs [Eugen](/Eugen "Eugen") and [Ruy Lopez](/Ruy_Lopez "Ruy Lopez").
