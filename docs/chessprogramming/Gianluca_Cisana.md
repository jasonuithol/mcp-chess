Gianluca Cisana [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Gianluca Cisana**,  
an Italian photographer and freelance programmer [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author of the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") compliant chess engine [BremboCE](/BremboCE "BremboCE"). The chess engine's name is related to the river [Brembo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brembo_%28river%29) in the [Province of Bergamo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Province_of_Bergamo), situated near Gianluca's home [[3]](#cite_note-3).
Gianluca further hosts a chess [tactics](/Tactics "Tactics") training site [[4]](#cite_note-4), providing the *Ideachess* [Android](/Android "Android") app [[5]](#cite_note-5).
