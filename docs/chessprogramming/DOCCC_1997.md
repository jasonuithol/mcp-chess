The **17th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place in [Alphen aan den Rijn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alphen_aan_den_Rijn). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, November 22-23 and 29-30, [1997](/Timeline#1997 "Timeline").
