The **Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye** (MASPV) **2009** was held from August 29 to 30, [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline"), hosted by the [University of Sopron](/University_of_Sopron "University of Sopron"), [Sopron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sopron), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary). Double round robin.
