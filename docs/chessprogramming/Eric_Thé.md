Eric Thé [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Eric Thé**,  
a Canadian musician, [multi-instrumentalist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multi-instrumentalist), music producer [[2]](#cite_note-2), teacher, and computer scientist with a Master's degree in 1992 from [McGill University](/McGill_University "McGill University") under advisor [Monroe Newborn](/Monroe_Newborn "Monroe Newborn").
His thesis covers [move ordering](/Move_Ordering "Move Ordering") concerning the efficiency of [alpha-beta](/Alpha-Beta "Alpha-Beta") search, using his thesis chess program [Fixafan](/Fixafan "Fixafan") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
