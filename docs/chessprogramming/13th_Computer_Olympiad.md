The venue of the Championships and [Conference](/CG_2008 "CG 2008") [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Thirteenth International Computer Games Championship**, due to the [2008 Summer Olympics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_Summer_Olympics) the Computer Olympiad was officially renamed for this occassion, took place from September 28 to October 5, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"), at Beijing Golden Century Golf Club, [Qinglonghu Township](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_township-level_divisions_of_Beijing#Fangshan_District), [Fangshan District](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fangshan_District), [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing), [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China), in conjunction with the [16th World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_2008 "WCCC 2008") and the [6th International Conference on Computers and Games](/CG_2008 "CG 2008"). The organization was in the hands of [Xinhe Xu](/Xinhe_Xu "Xinhe Xu") supported by local staff and the approved [ICGA](/ICGA "ICGA") team of [Jaap van den Herik](/Jaap_van_den_Herik "Jaap van den Herik"), [Mark Winands](/Mark_Winands "Mark Winands"), and [Joke Hellemons](/Johanna_Hellemons "Johanna Hellemons") [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
