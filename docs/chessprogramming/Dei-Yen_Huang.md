**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Dei-Yen Huang**

**Dei-Yen Huang**,  
a Taiwanese computer scientist, while affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and [Information Engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_engineering), [National Chiao Tung University](/National_Chiao_Tung_University "National Chiao Tung University"), [Hsinchu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hsinchu), he joined [I-Chen Wu's](/I-Chen_Wu "I-Chen Wu") work on [Connect6](/Connect6 "Connect6") in 2004 [[1]](#cite_note-1), as presented at [Advances in Computer Games 11](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_11 "Advances in Computer Games 11") in 2005 [[2]](#cite_note-2)
