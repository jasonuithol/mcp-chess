The **5th Polish Computer Chess Championship**, September 16 - 17, [2006](/Timeline#2006 "Timeline"), [Łódź](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA), [Poland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland). Round robin. [Butcher](/Butcher "Butcher") won the open category, [Matacz](/Matacz "Matacz") was best program of the uniform category played on computers provided by the organization.
