**Maximilian Lupke**,  
a German computer scientist, software developer and mathematician with B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in mathematics from [Humboldt University of Berlin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humboldt_University_of_Berlin) in 2015 and 2019 respectively [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is self-employed [PHP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PHP), [Haskell](/index.php?title=Haskell&action=edit&redlink=1 "Haskell (page does not exist)") and [Rust](/Rust "Rust") developer, and author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Asymptote](/Asymptote "Asymptote"),
