**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Sebastian Böhme**

**Sebastian Böhme**,  
a German chess and [correspondence chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Correspondence_chess) player, and [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author"), most notably as member of the [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") team [[1]](#cite_note-1). Recently he worked with [Richard Pijl](/Richard_Pijl "Richard Pijl") on the [opening book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") of [The Baron](/The_Baron "The Baron"), competing the [DOCCC 2010](/DOCCC_2010 "DOCCC 2010") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [ICT 2013](/ICT_2013 "ICT 2013").
