# Soei Tan

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Soei_Tan

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Soei Tan**

**Soei T. Tan**,  
an Indonesian computer scientist and independent IT and services professional [[1]](#cite_note-1). While working at the [University of Edinburgh](/University_of_Edinburgh "University of Edinburgh") [[2]](#cite_note-2) in the 70s, his research interests covered [knowledge representation](/Knowledge "Knowledge") of very simple chess [endgames](/Endgame "Endgame") like [KPK](/KPK "KPK"), and [Pawn Structure](/Pawn_Structure "Pawn Structure").
