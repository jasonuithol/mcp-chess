# Sacha Droste

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Sacha_Droste

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Sacha Droste**

**Sacha Droste**,  
a German computer scientist and software architect. As former member of the *Knowledge Engineering Group* headed by [Johannes Fürnkranz](/Johannes_F%C3%BCrnkranz "Johannes Fürnkranz") at [TU Darmstadt](/Darmstadt_University_of_Technology "Darmstadt University of Technology"), he defended his master thesis
on supervised [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") and [temporal difference learning](/Temporal_Difference_Learning "Temporal Difference Learning") in chess and the [chess variants](/Chess#Variants "Chess") [Crazyhouse](/Crazyhouse "Crazyhouse"), [Suicide Chess](/Losing_Chess "Losing Chess") and [Atomic Chess](/Atomic_Chess "Atomic Chess"), using [Sunsetter](/Sunsetter "Sunsetter") by [Georg von Zimmermann](/Georg_von_Zimmermann "Georg von Zimmermann") as test-bed of his experiments [[1]](#cite_note-1).
The matter is to [learn](/Learning "Learning") [piece values](/Point_Value "Point Value") and [piece-square values](/Piece-Square_Tables "Piece-Square Tables").
