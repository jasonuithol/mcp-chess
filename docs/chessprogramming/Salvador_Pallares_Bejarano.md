Salvador Pallares [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Salvador Pallares Bejarano**,  
a Spanish entrepreneur, business and technology consultant, who holds a Bachelor degree in business studies from [University of Seville](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Seville) in 1997 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As a chess player [[3]](#cite_note-3)
and programmer, he started chess programming in 2001, developing his first private engine **Littleclara**, superseded by his [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compatible chess engine [Clarabit](/Clarabit "Clarabit") [[4]](#cite_note-4), which started to play tournaments in 2007, and was published in 2008 [[5]](#cite_note-5).
