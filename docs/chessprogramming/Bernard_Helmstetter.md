Bernard Helmstetter [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Bernard Helmstetter**,  
a French computer scientist, game researcher and programmer at [Paris 8 University](/University_of_Paris#8 "University of Paris"), a [5 Dan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_rank) [Go](/Go "Go") player [[2]](#cite_note-2), and along with [Bruno Bouzy](/Bruno_Bouzy "Bruno Bouzy") and [Tristan Cazenave](/Tristan_Cazenave "Tristan Cazenave") one of the pioneers in [Monte-Carlo Tree Search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") [[3]](#cite_note-3) and its application in [Computer Go](/Go "Go") [[4]](#cite_note-4). Bernard Helmstetter is author of the [Lines of Action](/Lines_of_Action "Lines of Action") program [BING](http://www.grappa.univ-lille3.fr/icga/program.php?id=110) (Bing Is Not Gnuchess), which re-utilize large parts of code from [GNU Chess](/GNU_Chess "GNU Chess"), and the Go program [Oleg](http://www.grappa.univ-lille3.fr/icga/program.php?id=143).
