**Alder**,  
an early chess program by [Peter Kouwenhoven](/Peter_Kouwenhoven "Peter Kouwenhoven"). In 1978, Alder ran on a [PDP-11/45](/PDP-11 "PDP-11") of the [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University") to play a small match versus [IGM](/IGM "IGM") via telephone [[2]](#cite_note-2).
