The twelfth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**12th IPCCC**) took place February 19-23, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
