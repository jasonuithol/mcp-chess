# Chessica

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Chessica

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* Chessica**

**Chessica**,  
a commercial [Windows](/Windows "Windows") chess program distributed by [TASC](/TASC "TASC") in 1995 as part of their [TASC Chess System](/index.php?title=TASC_Chess_System&action=edit&redlink=1 "TASC Chess System (page does not exist)") with the [TASC SmartBoard](/TASC_SmartBoard "TASC SmartBoard") and *Interactive Chess Tutorials*, but also as standalone [Windows](/Windows "Windows") program. The engine is written by [Frans Morsch](/Frans_Morsch "Frans Morsch"), and based on the [DOS](/MS-DOS "MS-DOS") [Fritz 3.1](/Fritz "Fritz") program [[1]](#cite_note-1) sold by [ChessBase](/ChessBase "ChessBase"), which was the winner of the [WCCC 1995](/WCCC_1995 "WCCC 1995"). The functional [GUI](/GUI "GUI") was developed by TASC lead developer [Marc Derksen](/Marc_Derksen "Marc Derksen") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), supported by Dutch chess player and chess [database](/Databases "Databases") and GUI developer [Jessica Harmsen](/Jessica_Harmsen "Jessica Harmsen") [[4]](#cite_note-4). Chessica played three [Aegon Tournaments](/Aegon_Tournaments "Aegon Tournaments"), in [1995](/Aegon_1995 "Aegon 1995"), [1996](/Aegon_1996 "Aegon 1996") and [1997](/Aegon_1997 "Aegon 1997"), and further competed at the [DOCCC 1999](/DOCCC_1999 "DOCCC 1999") along with its brother [Quest](/Quest "Quest") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
