Bas Hamstra [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Bas Hamstra**,  
a Dutch software developer, and as computer chess programmer, author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and later [UCI](/UCI "UCI") [[2]](#cite_note-2) compliant chess engine [Tao](/Tao "Tao"). He was also involved in the early development of [Bookbuilder](/index.php?title=Bookbuilder&action=edit&redlink=1 "Bookbuilder (page does not exist)").
