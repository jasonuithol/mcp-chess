# CCCCISC 2008

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/CCCCISC_2008

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries](/Computer_Chess_Championship_of_CIS_Countries "Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries") \* 1st CCCCISC 2008**

The **1st Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries** [[1]](#cite_note-1) took place from February 29 to March 2, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"), at [Russian State Social University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_State_Social_University) in [Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow), [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia) [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
