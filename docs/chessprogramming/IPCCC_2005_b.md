The Fifteenth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**15th IPCCC**) took place December 27-30, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline") in the [Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum](/Heinz_Nixdorf_MuseumsForum "Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany). It was organized by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University") and was the first time held in conjunction with the human chess tournament [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
