**Petunia**,  
an experimental chess program applying a [parallel search](/Parallel_Search "Parallel Search") based on the serial [Genesis 2](/Genesis_NL "Genesis NL"), both developed by [Huib-Jan Imbens](/Huib-Jan_Imbens "Huib-Jan Imbens") and [Eric van Riet Paap](/Eric_van_Riet_Paap "Eric van Riet Paap"). Petunia played a strong [DOCCC 1993](/DOCCC_1993 "DOCCC 1993") with 7/11, 5th place from 24 [[2]](#cite_note-2), where it ran on a board with three [Intel](/Intel "Intel") [i860](/I860 "I860") processors at 40 MHz, controlled by a [486 PC](/X86 "X86").
