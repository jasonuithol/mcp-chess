**Andrea Manzo**,  
an Italian computer scientist and [correspondence chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Correspondence_chess) player, IM of the [ICCF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Correspondence_Chess_Federation) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author of the [Stockfish](/Stockfish "Stockfish") derivative [ShashChess](/ShashChess "ShashChess"), and co-author of [BrainLearn](/index.php?title=BrainLearn&action=edit&redlink=1 "BrainLearn (page does not exist)") based on [Brainfish](/Brainfish "Brainfish"), and was member of the [Vitruvius](/Vitruvius "Vitruvius") team around [Roberto Munter](/Roberto_Munter "Roberto Munter"), as [book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author") responsible for its [opening book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
