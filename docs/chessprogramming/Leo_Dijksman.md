Leo Dijksman & [László Szalai](/L%C3%A1szl%C3%B3_Szalai "László Szalai"), [ICT 2001](/ICT_2001 "ICT 2001") [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Leo Dijksman**, (died on February 18, 2015)  
was a Dutch computer chess expert, tester and promoter of [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") engines, and organizer of one of the highest quality engine competitions ever for many years, [WBEC Ridderkerk](/WBEC "WBEC") or *WinBoard Engine Competition* [[2]](#cite_note-2), also hosting and maintaining the associated chessengine news and tournament site [[3]](#cite_note-3). Leo Dijksman died in February 2015 at age 61 after battling cancer for almost two years [[4]](#cite_note-4) [[5]](#cite_note-5).
