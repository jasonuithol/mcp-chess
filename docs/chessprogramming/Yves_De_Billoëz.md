Yves De Billoëz [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Yves De Billoëz**,  
a Belgian [ICT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_and_communications_technology) architect, manager and consultant with expertise in [computer security](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_security), [cryptography](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cryptography) and [banking operations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bank) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is CTO and Co-Founder of *eSureCoin.com*, [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo), providing banks and financial institutions the ability to offer [Bitcoin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bitcoin) purchase and sale services [[3]](#cite_note-3).
As avocational computer chess programmer, Yves De Billoëz is author of the [open source engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Belofte](/Belofte "Belofte").
