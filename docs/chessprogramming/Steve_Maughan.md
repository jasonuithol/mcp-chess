**Steve Maughan**,  
a British [management consultant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Management_consulting) residing in Florida [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Steve Maughan is computer chess and [Othello](/Othello "Othello") programmer, and author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compatible chess engines [Monarch](/Monarch "Monarch") [[3]](#cite_note-3) and [Maverick](/Maverick "Maverick"), and the Othello or Reversi program *Edge Reversi* [[4]](#cite_note-4).
In March 2013, Steve started a [blog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blog) on the development of his chess engine Maverick [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6).
