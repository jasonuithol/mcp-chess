# ICT 2012

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/ICT_2012

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [International CSVN Tournament](/International_CSVN_Tournament "International CSVN Tournament") \* ICT 2012**

**[< Prev](/ICT_2011 "ICT 2011") [Next >](/ICT_2013 "ICT 2013")**

The **ICT 12** took place from May 12-13, [2012](/Timeline#2012 "Timeline"), [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). [Rybka](/Rybka "Rybka") won, running on a 100 core [cluster](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_cluster) consisting of 1 16 core [Sandy Bridge E Xeon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sandy_Bridge-E) and 7 12 core [Westmere Xeons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulftown) [[1]](#cite_note-1), runner-up [Critter](/Critter "Critter") ran on an [AMD](/AMD "AMD") [Phenom II X6](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AMD_Phenom_II_X6_1605T) @ 3.7GHz [[2]](#cite_note-2).
