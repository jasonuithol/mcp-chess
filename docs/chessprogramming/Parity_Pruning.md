**Parity Pruning**,   
an asymmetric consideration in various forward pruning decisions based on the [parity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parity_%28mathematics%29) of the [ply distance](/Ply "Ply") to the [root](/Root "Root"). The idea is to prune more aggressively on even plies when [side to move](/Side_to_move "Side to move") is the computer player, but less aggressively for the odd plies.
