**Morgoth**,  
a chess program by [Matt Craighead](/Matt_Craighead "Matt Craighead") written in [C](/C "C") as a complete rewrite of Craighead's former program [Neptune](/Neptune "Neptune") in 1995 [[2]](#cite_note-2) , where he initially had some issues with [quiescence search](/Quiescence_Search "Quiescence Search") and [move ordering](/Move_Ordering "Move Ordering"), and never competed in official tournaments.
