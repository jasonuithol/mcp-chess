**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Matteo D'Annibale**

**Matteo D'Annibale**,  
an Italian software developer and chess programmer.
He is primary author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [BlackMamba](/BlackMamba "BlackMamba") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
Along with [Roberto Munter](/Roberto_Munter "Roberto Munter"), he is further co-author of the private chess engine [Balor](/index.php?title=Balor&action=edit&redlink=1 "Balor (page does not exist)") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
