# Livingston Chess960 Computer World Championship

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Livingston_Chess960_Computer_World_Championship

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* Livingston Chess960 Computer World Championship**

The **Livingston** [[1]](#cite_note-1) [Chess960](/Chess960 "Chess960") **Computer World Championship** was held within the scope of the [Chess Classic](http://www.chesstigers.de/ccm9.php?lang=1) [Mainz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mainz), Germany, from 2005 until 2009, organized by [Hans-Walter Schmitt](/index.php?title=Hans-Walter_Schmitt&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hans-Walter Schmitt (page does not exist)") and his Chess Tigers [[2]](#cite_note-2).
