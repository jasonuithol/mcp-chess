**Dariusz Czechowski**,  
a Polish computer chess programmer [[2]](#cite_note-2) and author of [Darmenios](/Darmenios "Darmenios") [[3]](#cite_note-3), participant of the [WCCC 2010](/WCCC_2010 "WCCC 2010") and [WCSC 2010](/WCSC_2010 "WCSC 2010") in [Kanazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa,_Ishikawa), and winner of the [PCCC 2011](/PCCC_2011 "PCCC 2011") .
