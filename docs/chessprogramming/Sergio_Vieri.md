**Sergio Vieri**,  
an Indonesian computer scientist affiliated with the [National University of Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_University_of_Singapore) and [Google](/index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1 "Google (page does not exist)") [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore).
At the [International Olympiad in Informatics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Olympiad_in_Informatics), he won Bronze and Silver in 2016 and 2017 respectively [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and is now preparing and testing problems for selection and training for the Indonesian team [[3]](#cite_note-3).
Sergio Vieri is further successful chess trainer of [Leela Chess](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero").
Relaxing the "Zero" paradigm of pure [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") by [supervised learning](/Supervised_Learning "Supervised Learning") techniques, he is famous for his strong networks [[4]](#cite_note-4).
More recently, his training tools and methods also gave [Stockfish NNUE](/Stockfish_NNUE "Stockfish NNUE") a kick with an increase in [playing strength](/Playing_Strength "Playing Strength") hardly thought possible before [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6).
