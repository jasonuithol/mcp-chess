Orange County Convention Center [[1]](#cite_note-1)

[ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Nineteenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC), [Orlando](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orlando%2C_Florida), [Florida](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florida), USA, November 13-15, [1988](/Timeline#1988 "Timeline"). The tournament was held in conjunction with the [ACM](/ACM "ACM")/[IEEE](/IEEE "IEEE") [Supercomputing Conference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ACM/IEEE_Supercomputing_Conference) with a workshop *New Technologies for Chess* in the [Orange County Convention Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orange_County_Convention_Center) [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
