The **2nd Polish Computer Chess Championship**, August 29-31, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline"), [Łódź](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA), [Poland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland). Round robin. [Tytan](/Tytan "Tytan") won the open and simultaneously the uniform category played on [Intel](/Intel "Intel") [Celeron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Celeron) 1.4 GHz computers provided by the organization.
