**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Jochen Peussner**

**Jochen Peussner**,  
a German computer scientist and software developer, chess player, and as computer chess programmer author of the chess program [Neurologic](/Neurologic "Neurologic"), and more recently, the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [DisasterArea](/DisasterArea "DisasterArea") [[1]](#cite_note-1)..
