Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/WCRCC_2007

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship](/ACCA_World_Computer_Rapid_Chess_Championship "ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship") \* WCRCC 2007**

**[Next >](/WCRCC_2008 "WCRCC 2008")**

The **First Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship** was held July 21-22, [2007](/Timeline#2007 "Timeline"). The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/28+1), played on-line over the internet ([ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)")). [Bruce Wright](/Bruce_Wright "Bruce Wright"), co-author of [Duchess](/Duchess "Duchess") (2nd place [WCCC 1977](/WCCC_1977 "WCCC 1977") & 3rd place [WCCC 1980](/WCCC_1980 "WCCC 1980")), served as guest commentator [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
