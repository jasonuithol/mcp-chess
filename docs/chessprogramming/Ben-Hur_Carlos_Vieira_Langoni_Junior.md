**Ben-Hur Carlos Vieira Langoni Júnior**,  
a Brazilian computer scientist and software engineer holding a [Bachelor's degree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bachelor%27s_degree) in computer science from [Universidade Salgado de Oliveira](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universidade_Salgado_de_Oliveira), [São Gonçalo, Rio de Janeiro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Gon%C3%A7alo,_Rio_de_Janeiro) [[2]](#cite_note-2). As computer chess programmer, Ben-Hur Carlos Vieira Langoni Júnio is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [RedQueen](/RedQueen "RedQueen") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
