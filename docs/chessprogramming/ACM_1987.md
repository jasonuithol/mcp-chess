[ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Eighteenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC), [Dallas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dallas), [Texas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas), USA, October 25-27, [1987](/Timeline#1987 "Timeline"). The tournament was held in conjunction with the ACM Fall Joint Computer Conference in the [Dallas Infomart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Infomart) [[2]](#cite_note-2) along with a Computer Chess Workshop [[3]](#cite_note-3).
