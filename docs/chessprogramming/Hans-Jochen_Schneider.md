**Hans-Jochen Schneider**,  
a German mathematician, computer scientist and early chess programmer. Along with [Kurt Fischer](/Kurt_Fischer "Kurt Fischer"), Hans-Jochen Schneider is author of the first German chess program [Fischer-Schneider](/Fischer-Schneider "Fischer-Schneider"), written in 1965 while affiliated with the [University of Stuttgart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Stuttgart) [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
