Tom Luif [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Tom Luif**, (died on May 02, 2009) [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3)  
was a Dutch computer chess aficionado and collector of [dedicated chess computers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers"). He maintained the **Overtom Chess Computer Museum** [[4]](#cite_note-4),
introducing and describing many dedicated chess computers by almost all [manufacturers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers#Companies "Dedicated Chess Computers") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
