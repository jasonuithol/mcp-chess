The **Third World Microcomputer Chess Championship** took place from October 13 to 19, [1983](/Timeline#1983 "Timeline") at the SZÁMOK Computer Education and Information Centre, [Budapest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budapest), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary), as a special activity of the 3rd Symposium on Microprocessor and Microcomputer Applications. Tournament Organizer and Director was [Dr. László Lindner](/L%C3%A1szl%C3%B3_Lindner "László Lindner"), the [ICCA](/ICCA "ICCA") was represented by Past President [Benjamin Mittman](/Ben_Mittman "Ben Mittman"). Up to three computers per team were allowed, with the restriction those entries couldn't play each other in the seven round [Swiss-system tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament). [Elite Auto Sensory](/Elite "Elite") by [Dan](/Dan_Spracklen "Dan Spracklen") and [Kathe Spracklen](/Kathe_Spracklen "Kathe Spracklen"), supported by [Ron Nelson](/Ron_Nelson "Ron Nelson") and [Boris Baczynskyj](/Boris_Baczynskyj "Boris Baczynskyj") became champion with 6/7, while their entry [Sensory 9](/Sensory_9 "Sensory 9") won the prize of the best commercial available computer, competing with the [East German](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Germany) [Chess-Master](/Chess-Master "Chess-Master").

## Round 5

In round 5, [Prestige Challenger](/Chess_Challenger "Chess Challenger"), operated by [Dan Spracklen](/Dan_Spracklen "Dan Spracklen"), played [Mephisto X](/Mephisto_(H) "Mephisto (H)") operated by [Ossi Weiner](/Ossi_Weiner "Ossi Weiner") and [Thomas Nitsche](/Thomas_Nitsche "Thomas Nitsche"), as reported by [Frederic Friedel](/Frederic_Friedel "Frederic Friedel") [[8]](#cite_note-8) . At move 40, Mephisto had a big advantage, but started to miscalculate and after 75...Qc7, Weiner could hardly wait for the imminent adjournment and adjudication by the master, and had some arguments with [Fidelity Electronics](/Fidelity_Electronics "Fidelity Electronics") representative [Peter Reckwitz](/Peter_Reckwitz "Peter Reckwitz") [[9]](#cite_note-9) :
