**Mike Watters**,  
a British computer chess expert from [Milton Keynes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milton_Keynes), [England](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/England) [[1]](#cite_note-1). Mike is enthusiast and collector of [dedicated chess computers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers") and hosts the excellent *Chess Computer UK* sites [[2]](#cite_note-2).
