**Mark Watson Chess**, (Mark Watson)  
an early chess program written in 1977/78 by [Mark Watson](/Mark_Watson "Mark Watson") in [Basic](/Basic "Basic") for the [Apple II](/Apple_II "Apple II"), distributed by [Apple](/index.php?title=Apple&action=edit&redlink=1 "Apple (page does not exist)") on their demo cassette tape [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2). Mark Watson Chess participated at the [Second West Coast Computer Faire Microcomputer Chess Tournament](/MCCT_1978 "MCCT 1978") in March 1978 in the class C section for Basic programs [[3]](#cite_note-3).
