**Ulrich Türke**,  
a German physicist, who graduated from [University of Dortmund](/University_of_Dortmund "University of Dortmund") in 1985 [[2]](#cite_note-2). As former avocational computer chess programmer, Ulrich Türke is author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") compatible chess engine [Comet](/Comet "Comet").
