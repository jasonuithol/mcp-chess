**Aron Petkovski**,  
a Macedonian-American computer science major at [The University of Texas at Dallas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Texas_at_Dallas), and author of the [open source](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [UCI](/UCI "UCI") chess engine [Integral](/index.php?title=Integral&action=edit&redlink=1 "Integral (page does not exist)"). He is the co-founder and hosting provider of the [chess.aronpetkovski.com](https://chess.aronpetkovski.com) [OpenBench](/OpenBench "OpenBench") instance, facilitating the testing of engines including [Obsidian](/index.php?title=Obsidian&action=edit&redlink=1 "Obsidian (page does not exist)"), [PlentyChess](/index.php?title=PlentyChess&action=edit&redlink=1 "PlentyChess (page does not exist)"), [Clarity](/index.php?title=Clarity&action=edit&redlink=1 "Clarity (page does not exist)"), [Spaghet](/index.php?title=Spaghet&action=edit&redlink=1 "Spaghet (page does not exist)"), [Serendipity](/index.php?title=Serendipity&action=edit&redlink=1 "Serendipity (page does not exist)"), [Quanticade](/index.php?title=Quanticade&action=edit&redlink=1 "Quanticade (page does not exist)"), and [Elixir](/index.php?title=Elixir&action=edit&redlink=1 "Elixir (page does not exist)"). His contributions include  [dynamic improving](/Improving#Dynamic_Improving "Improving") and research in HCE  [evaluation](/Evaluation "Evaluation") functions with large parameter counts.
