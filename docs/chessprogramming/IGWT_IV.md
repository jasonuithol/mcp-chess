The **IGWT IV**,
the International [Gsei](/G_6 "G 6") Web Tournament IV edition, was held in February and March [2017](/Timeline#2017 "Timeline"). Eight Italian engines played the final steps versus eight guest engines - both groups required qualification tournaments. Again, [Andscacs](/Andscacs "Andscacs") by [Daniel José Queraltó](/Daniel_Jos%C3%A9_Queralt%C3%B3 "Daniel José Queraltó") and [Chiron](/Chiron "Chiron") by [Ubaldo Andrea Farina](/Ubaldo_Andrea_Farina "Ubaldo Andrea Farina") won their matches in the round of sixteen, the quarterfinal and semifinal to play the hard-fought final. This edition, Andscacs could take revenge from last years defeat, and became the lucky but deserved winner [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
