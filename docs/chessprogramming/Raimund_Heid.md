Raimund Heid [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Raimund Heid**,  
a German computer scientist and software developer with a [diplom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplom) degree in CS from [University of Hamburg](/University_of_Hamburg "University of Hamburg"). As avocational computer chess programmer, to avoid unlearning programming as project manager [[2]](#cite_note-2), he is author of the [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Protector](/Protector "Protector").
