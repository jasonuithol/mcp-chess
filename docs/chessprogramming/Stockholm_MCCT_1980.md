The **Second Stockholm Micro Computer Chess Tournament** was held in December [1980](/Timeline#1980 "Timeline") in [Stockholm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockholm), Sweden, organized by the Swedish [ICCA](/ICCA "ICCA"), forerunner of the [SSDF](/SSDF "SSDF").
