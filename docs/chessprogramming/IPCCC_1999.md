The eighth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**8th IPCCC**) took place from February 3-7, [1999](/Timeline#1999 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
