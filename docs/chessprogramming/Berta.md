Dicke Berta [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Berta**,  
a private chess engine [[2]](#cite_note-2) written in [C](/C "C")/[C++](/Cpp "Cpp") by [Felix Schmenger](/Felix_Schmenger "Felix Schmenger"). Berta regularly plays online at the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") [[3]](#cite_note-3), and has participated at various [online tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches#online "Tournaments and Matches"), such as [CCT tournaments](/CCT_Tournaments "CCT Tournaments") and [ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championships](/ACCA_World_Computer_Rapid_Chess_Championship "ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship"), most recently the [WCRCC 2018](/WCRCC_2018 "WCRCC 2018").
