**Robert J. (Bob) Pawlak**,  
an American electrical and electronics engineer, Ph.D. from [Columbus State Community College](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbus_State_Community_College) in 1983 [[2]](#cite_note-2), and freelance chess and computer chess writer for [Chess Life](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chess_Life), who maintained the *chessreview.com* [[3]](#cite_note-3) and the *ChessAssistance.com* sites, the latter archived by [ChessOK](/ChessOK "ChessOK") [[4]](#cite_note-4), and further authored tutorials and lessons for [Chess Assistant](/Chess_Assistant "Chess Assistant").
