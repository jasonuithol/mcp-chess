Hilton and Towers Hotel [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The [ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Fifteenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) took place from October 7-9, [1984](/Timeline#1984 "Timeline") at the Imperial Ballroom of the [Hilton San Francisco and Tower Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hilton_San_Francisco_%26_Towers), [San Francisco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Francisco%2C_California), [California](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California), USA.
