The **CCT1** was organized by Steve McRiley, played via the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") (ICC) [[1]](#cite_note-1), January 29-30 and February 5-6, [2000](/Timeline#2000 "Timeline"), 8 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 75min 10sec increment.
