Bishop's movements in chess [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Bishop**,  
the [sliding piece](/Sliding_Pieces "Sliding Pieces") moving along the [diagonals](/Diagonals "Diagonals") and [anti-diagonals](/Anti-Diagonals "Anti-Diagonals"). As a consequence of its movement, each bishop always remains on either the white or black [squares](/Squares "Squares"), and so it is also common to refer to them as light-squared or dark-squared bishops.
