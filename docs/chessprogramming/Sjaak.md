**Sjaak** [[1]](#cite_note-1), (TrojanKnight)  
a private chess, [Antichess](/Losing_Chess "Losing Chess"), and later [Atomic Chess](/Atomic_Chess "Atomic Chess") playing program by [Ronald de Man](/Ronald_de_Man "Ronald de Man"), registered under the handle *TrojanKnight* [[2]](#cite_note-2) at [FICS](/index.php?title=FICS&action=edit&redlink=1 "FICS (page does not exist)") [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
Sjaak has a [parallel SMP search](/Parallel_Search "Parallel Search") [[5]](#cite_note-5), and was test bed of Ronald de Man's [Syzygy Bases](/Syzygy_Bases "Syzygy Bases") [[6]](#cite_note-6).
