The **I.G.T. 2018** (International [Gsei](/G_6 "G 6") Tournament) was held on October 27 and 28, [2018](/Timeline#2018 "Timeline") in [Borgo Virgilio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Borgo_Virgilio), [Mantua](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mantua), [Lombardy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lombardy).
During the tournament, a two game man vs machine match between [FIDE master](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIDE_titles#FIDE_Master_(FM)) Valerio Luciani [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [AdaChess](/AdaChess "AdaChess") took place, both games won by AdaChess [[3]](#cite_note-3).
