**University of Ljubljana**,   
[Ljubljana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ljubljana), [Slovenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenia), is the oldest, largest, and internationally best ranked university in Slovenia, being among the first 500 or the first 3% of the world's best universities according to the [ARWU](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_Ranking_of_World_Universities) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
