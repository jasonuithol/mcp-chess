# DOCCC 2001

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2001

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2001**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2000 "DOCCC 2000") [Next >](/DOCCC_2002 "DOCCC 2002")**

The **21st** Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, October 27-28 and November 3-4, [2001](/Timeline#2001 "Timeline"). [Chess Tiger](/Chess_Tiger "Chess Tiger") was the winner with 9/11, since [Rebel](/Rebel "Rebel") also with 9/11 had less sum of opponent scores, also due to playing [EEC](/index.php?title=EEC&action=edit&redlink=1 "EEC (page does not exist)") in the last round. A clear symptom the swiss system was a bit degenerated with eleven rounds for sixteen participants, despite most were happy playing so many games.
