# ECCC 1979

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/ECCC_1979

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [European Computer Chess Championship](/European_Computer_Chess_Championship "European Computer Chess Championship") \* ECCC 1979**

**[< Prev](/ECCC_1976 "ECCC 1976")**

The **Second European Computer Chess Championship** took place from September 25-27, [1979](/Timeline#1979 "Timeline"), at the [Wembley Conference Centre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wembley_Conference_Centre), [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom). The tournament, sanctioned by the [ICCA](/ICCA "ICCA"), was first planned to be held in conjunction with the *Fourth International Congress of Cybernetics and Systems* in [Amsterdam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam) [[1]](#cite_note-1) during the week of August 21, 1978, organized by [Barend Swets](/Barend_Swets "Barend Swets") [[2]](#cite_note-2), but was displaced due to lack of sponsors [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
