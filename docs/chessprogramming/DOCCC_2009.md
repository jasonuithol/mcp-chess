The **29th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place October 16-18, [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline"), at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). With 9 participants an 8 rounds robin tournament was played, 90 minutes per player for the whole game.
