Jonatan Pettersson [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Jonatan Pettersson**,  
a Swedish computer scientist and [Java](/Java "Java") developer. He is author of the chess engine [Mediocre](/Mediocre "Mediocre"), written in Java [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and maintains the *Mediocre Chess blog*, covering the development of his engine and various topics on chess programming, also recently announcing he is working on a new [bitboard](/Bitboards "Bitboards") based Java engine [[3]](#cite_note-3).
