**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* [Rebel](/Rebel "Rebel") \* ProDeo**

**ProDeo**, (Pro Deo)  
a free version of the former commercial Rebel chess program series by [Ed Schröder](/Ed_Schroder "Ed Schroder") with a complete rewritten personality setting in self-explainable natural language, making it one of the most tunable chess engines. ProDeo is [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant and runs under [Windows](/Windows "Windows") with various [GUIs](/GUI "GUI"), and incorporates an open source [EOC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Encyclopaedia_of_Chess_Openings) [database](/Databases "Databases"). ProDeo played the [DOCCC 2004](/DOCCC_2004 "DOCCC 2004"), [ICT 2005](/ICT_2005 "ICT 2005"), [DOCCC 2005](/DOCCC_2005 "DOCCC 2005"), [DOCCC 2011](/DOCCC_2011 "DOCCC 2011"), and the [ICT 2012](/ICT_2012 "ICT 2012").
