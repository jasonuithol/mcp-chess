**Ratih Dewanti**,  
an Indonesian mathematician at [Gunadarma University](http://id.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universitas_Gunadarma), [Depok](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depok), [Jawa Barat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Java), Ph.D. in 2010 [[1]](#cite_note-1).
Her research interests include [numerical analysis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Numerical_Analysis), [topology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topology), [differential equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Differential_equation), [finance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finance) and [business economics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Business_economics) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
During the [WMCCC 1996](/WMCCC_1996 "WMCCC 1996") at the Gunadarma University Campus in [Jakarta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jakarta), she was member of the organization and PR committee, and author of the tournament report published in the [ICCA Journal](/ICGA_Journal "ICGA Journal") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
