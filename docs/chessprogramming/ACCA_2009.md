on [ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)"), 6 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 45min 10sec increment.
