Johanes Suhardjo [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Johanes Suhardjo**,  
an Indonesian born computer scientist, consultant, analyst, and assistant director at *College of Engineering*, [University of Notre Dame](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Notre_Dame), [Notre Dame, Indiana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Notre_Dame,_Indiana)
[[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") compatible [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Parrot](/Parrot "Parrot") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
