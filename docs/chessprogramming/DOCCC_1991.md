The **11th Dutch Computer Chess Championship 1991** took place in October [1991](/Timeline#1991 "Timeline") at Academic Computer Center Utrecht (ACCU), [Utrecht University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_University), [Utrecht](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_%28city%29), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
