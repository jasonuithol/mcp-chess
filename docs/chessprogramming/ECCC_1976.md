# ECCC 1976

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/ECCC_1976

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [European Computer Chess Championship](/European_Computer_Chess_Championship "European Computer Chess Championship") \* ECCC 1976**

**[Next >](/ECCC_1979 "ECCC 1979")**

The **First European Computer Chess Championship** took place August 9-11, [1976](/Timeline#1976 "Timeline") at the [Vrije Universiteit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vrije_Universiteit), [Amsterdam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). Eight (nine?) programs participated, first four to qualify for the [WCCC 1977](/WCCC_1977 "WCCC 1977") in [Toronto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toronto) [[1]](#cite_note-1). The three round Swiss tournament was won by [Master](/Master "Master") with [Orwell](/Orwell "Orwell") and [Tell](/Tell "Tell") tying for second place [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
