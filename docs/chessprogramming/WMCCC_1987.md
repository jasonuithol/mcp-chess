The **Seventh World Microcomputer Chess Championship** took place from September 14 to 20, [1987](/Timeline#1987 "Timeline"), [Rome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rome), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy). Both the Manufacturers' group (Commercial Event) and the Programmers' group (Software Event) were won by [Richard Lang](/Richard_Lang "Richard Lang") with [Mephisto Roma](/Mephisto_Roma "Mephisto Roma") and [Psion](/Psion "Psion") respectively, followed by [David Levy](/David_Levy "David Levy") and [Mark Taylor](/Mark_Taylor "Mark Taylor") with [CXG Sphinx](/CXG_Sphinx "CXG Sphinx") and [Cyrus 68K](/Cyrus_68K "Cyrus 68K"), more closely than the results suggested [[2]](#cite_note-2) . Best amateur was [Gyula Horváth](/Gyula_Horv%C3%A1th "Gyula Horváth") with [Pandix](/Pandix "Pandix").
