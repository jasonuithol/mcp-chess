**The third Chess Programmers Tournament**,
was organized in March [2010](/Timeline#2010 "Timeline") by [Richard Pijl](/Richard_Pijl "Richard Pijl"). It took place in [De Bouwhoeve](http://www.debouwhoeve.be/), [Hoogstraten-Wortel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoogstraten), [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium). Eleven participants, six round swiss, time-control was 90 minutes per side and game.
