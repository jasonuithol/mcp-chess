**Dragon by Komodo Chess**, (KomodoDragon, Komodo Dragon)  
a version of Komodo which combines an [NNUE](/NNUE "NNUE") based [evaluation](/Evaluation "Evaluation") with Komodo's [search](/Search "Search"),
either classical [alpha-beta](/Alpha-Beta "Alpha-Beta") as well as [MCTS](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search").
Dragon was announced on November 02, 2020, and released as **Dragon by Komodo Chess** on November 10, 2020.
[Strength](/Playing_Strength "Playing Strength") improvements for both search modes were given in the 150 (4 [threads](/Thread "Thread")) to 190 (one thread) Elo range [[2]](#cite_note-2).

Dragon is the work of [Larry Kaufman](/Larry_Kaufman "Larry Kaufman") and [Mark Lefler](/Mark_Lefler "Mark Lefler"), who superseded original Komodo author [Don Dailey](/Don_Dailey "Don Dailey") after his death on November 22, 2013,
further supported by [Dmitry Pervov](/Dmitry_Pervov "Dmitry Pervov") and [Dietrich Kappe](/Dietrich_Kappe "Dietrich Kappe") concerning the NNUE inference code and training data. The [neural network](/Neural_Networks "Neural Networks") was trained [supervised](/Supervised_Learning "Supervised Learning") by chess [Grandmaster](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Grandmaster) Larry Kaufman, along with [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") considering billions of positions and evaluations from Komodo games.
[Chess.com](/index.php?title=Chess.com&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chess.com (page does not exist)") supported the project financially [[3]](#cite_note-3).

Dragon 3.3 is expected to be the final version of Dragon after [Mark Lefler](/Mark_Lefler "Mark Lefler") and [Larry Kaufman](/Larry_Kaufman "Larry Kaufman") retire from running Komodo Chess, turn it over to [Chess.com](/index.php?title=Chess.com&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chess.com (page does not exist)")[[4]](#cite_note-4), and remain as consultants through 2025.[[5]](#cite_note-5)

# Tournament Play

Dragon by Komodo Chess, at [TCEC](/TCEC "TCEC") called **KomodoDragon**, played the [Premier Division](/TCEC_Season_20#Premier "TCEC Season 20") of [TCEC Season 20](/TCEC_Season_20 "TCEC Season 20"), and became strong third behind [LCZero](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero") and [Stockfish](/Stockfish "Stockfish") in 2020/2021. KomodoDragon became third again at [TCEC Season 21](/TCEC_Season_21 "TCEC Season 21"), winning the [Infrafinal](/TCEC_Season_21#Infra "TCEC Season 21"), and became runner up behind Stockfish at [TCEC Season 22](/TCEC_Season_22 "TCEC Season 22") in 2022.
