Hilton Anatole [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The [ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Seventeenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC), took place from November 2-6, [1986](/Timeline#1986 "Timeline"), [Anatole Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hilton_Anatole), [Dallas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dallas), [Texas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas), USA. Simultaneously, in the [Dallas Infomart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Infomart), the [6th WMCCC](/WMCCC_1986 "WMCCC 1986") took place with a compatible time schedule for all those participating both events.
