# Basil Falcinelli

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Basil_Falcinelli

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Basil Falcinelli**

**Basil Falcinelli**,  
a programmer in the [internet security](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_security),
[Internet privacy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_privacy) and [cryptocurrency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cryptocurrency) [open source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_model) community [[1]](#cite_note-1) affiliated with *ReQrypt.org* [[2]](#cite_note-2), also involved in computer chess.
He introduced and published a [Linux](/Linux "Linux") port of the [Gull 3](/Gull "Gull") chess engine [[3]](#cite_note-3),
which subsequently evolved to [LazyGull](/Gull#LazyGull "Gull") [[4]](#cite_note-4), further incorporating his [Fathom](/Syzygy_Bases#Fathom "Syzygy Bases") [Syzygy tablebase](/Syzygy_Bases "Syzygy Bases") probing tool [[5]](#cite_note-5).
