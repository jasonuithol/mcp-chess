The **16th World Microcomputer Chess Championship** was combined within the [9th WCCC](/WCCC_1999 "WCCC 1999") for all participants playing on a microcomputer with one core. It took place from June 14 to 19, [1999](/Timeline#1999 "Timeline") at [Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum](/Heinz_Nixdorf_MuseumsForum "Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany), in conjunction with the [Advances in Computer Games 9](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_9 "Advances in Computer Games 9") conference. [Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder") was the winner of both tournaments simultaneously..
