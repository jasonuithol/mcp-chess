The **31th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place October 14-16, [2011](/Timeline#2011 "Timeline"), [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). Eight Participants played seven rounds. The [CSVN announcement](/ICGA_Investigations#CSVN "ICGA Investigations") in August 2011 [[1]](#cite_note-1) against the [ICGA decision](/ICGA_Investigations#RybkaDecision "ICGA Investigations") concerning [Rybka](/Rybka "Rybka"), caused several former participants to write an open letter of protest towards the [CSVN](/CSVN "CSVN") to don't enter their tournaments under the current direction [[2]](#cite_note-2).
