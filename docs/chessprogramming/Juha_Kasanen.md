**Juha Kasanen**, (1944 - 1988 [[2]](#cite_note-2))  
was a Finnish mathematician and computer scientist, chess player and [chess composer](/Category:Chess_Composer "Category:Chess Composer") [[3]](#cite_note-3), in the 70s and early 80s affiliated with the computer centre of the [University of Helsinki](/University_of_Helsinki "University of Helsinki") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
Along with [Mika Korhonen](/Mika_Korhonen "Mika Korhonen") and [Timo Saari](/Timo_Saari "Timo Saari"), he was member of the team developing the mainframe chess program [Shy](/Shy "Shy"), which played [WCCC 1983](/WCCC_1983 "WCCC 1983") in [New York](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City).
