The **Ninth World Microcomputer Chess Championship** took place from September 9 to 16, [1989](/Timeline#1989 "Timeline"), [Portorož](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portoro%C5%BE), [Slovenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenia) (in 1989 the [Socialist Republic of Slovenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socialist_Republic_of_Slovenia), a [constituent country](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constituent_country) of the [Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socialist_Federal_Republic_of_Yugoslavia)), in the Ball Room of the [Palace Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kempinski_Palace_Portoro%C5%BE). This was the same room that the 15 year old [Bobby Fischer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bobby_Fischer) played his first international tournament, the Portorož [Interzonal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interzonal) in 1958 [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4) .

# Excursion

[![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/ce/Skocjanske_jame.jpg/330px-Skocjanske_jame.jpg)](/File:Skocjanske_jame.jpg)

[Škocjan Cave entrance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%A0kocjan_Caves) [[11]](#cite_note-11)

The WMCCC excursion took place on Wednesday, September 13, visiting the [Škocjan Caves](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%A0kocjan_Caves) and the [Lipica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lipica,_Se%C5%BEana) Stud Farm. In consideration of the upcoming incident, not all participants were attending [[12]](#cite_note-12).

# Disqualification

The German entry [Quickstep](/Quickstep "Quickstep") by [Klaus Dieter Langer](/Klaus_Dieter_Langer "Klaus Dieter Langer") appeared to be a [Clone](/Category:Clone "Category:Clone") of [Mephisto Almeria](/Mephisto_Almeria "Mephisto Almeria"). After a protest by [Hegener & Glaser](/Hegener_%26_Glaser "Hegener & Glaser"), and several meetings of the Tournament Directors with Mr. Langer, presenting the Mephisto evidence, Mr. Langer was not able or willing to provide any evidence Quickstep was original. After consultation with ICCA President [David Levy](/David_Levy "David Levy"), Quickstep and Mr. Langer were disqualified from the tournament [[13]](#cite_note-13) [[14]](#cite_note-14).
