The **4th Chess Computer Cup**, November 7 and 8, [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline"), [Carugate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carugate), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy). Six rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), timecontrol 60 minutes + 10 seconds increment per move. The event was organized by Accademia Scacchistica Le Due Torri [[1]](#cite_note-1) , [Bologna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bologna), the Department of Computer Science – [University of Bologna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Bologna), and the [Associazione Italiana di Calcolo Automatico](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Associazione_Italiana_per_l%27Informatica_ed_il_Calcolo_Automatico) (AICA) [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
