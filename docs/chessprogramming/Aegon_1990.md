# Aegon 1990

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Aegon_1990

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Aegon Tournaments](/Aegon_Tournaments "Aegon Tournaments") \* Aegon 1990**

**[< Prev](/Aegon_1989 "Aegon 1989") [Next >](/Aegon_1991 "Aegon 1991")**

The **5th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from May 8 to 10, and 13 to 15, [1990](/Timeline#1990 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). The one and only time, a computer won an Aegon tournament was [HiTech](/HiTech "HiTech") with 5 out of 6. Its record was matched by [Jeroen Piket](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeroen_Piket) and Edgar Blokhuis, both of whom also had 5 points, but inferior tie-break points.
