**21st World Computer Chess Championship 2015**,
from June 29, to July 03, [2015](/Timeline#2015 "Timeline"), Snellius Building [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands), in conjunction with the [WCSC 2015](/WCSC_2015 "WCSC 2015"), [Advances in Computer Games 14](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_14 "Advances in Computer Games 14") [[4]](#cite_note-4), and the [18th Computer Olympiad](/18th_Computer_Olympiad "18th Computer Olympiad"). [Jonny](/Jonny "Jonny"), by [Johannes Zwanzger](/Johannes_Zwanzger "Johannes Zwanzger"), brought home the gold on the 2400 core cluster, thanks to upset victories against [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") in round 5 and [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") in round 9. Komodo is second and third place is shared by Hiarcs and [Protector](/Protector "Protector") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
