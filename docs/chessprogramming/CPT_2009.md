**The second Chess Programmers Tournament**,
was organized in February [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline") by [Richard Pijl](/Richard_Pijl "Richard Pijl"). It took place in [De Bouwhoeve](http://www.debouwhoeve.be/), [Hoogstraten-Wortel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoogstraten), [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium). Twelve participants, six round swiss, time-control was 90 minutes per side and game. [Pocket Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder") was added to make the field even, operated by [Tessa Pijl](/Tessa_Pijl "Tessa Pijl").
