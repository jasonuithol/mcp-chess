Kevin O’Connell [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Kevin John O’Connell**,  
an Irish chess player, a [FIDE Master](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIDE_titles#FIDE_Master_(FM)), an international arbiter and a FIDE senior trainer [[2]](#cite_note-2), chess consultant [[3]](#cite_note-3), columnist and writer, author of various chess books [[4]](#cite_note-4) and newspaper columns and a computer chess business person. In 1979, along with his business partner [David Levy](/David_Levy "David Levy") founder of [Philidor Software](/Philidor_Software "Philidor Software") and in 1981 of [Intelligent Software](/Intelligent_Software "Intelligent Software") [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6), developing and trading [dedicated chess computers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers") [[7]](#cite_note-7), chess software for [home computers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Home_computer), as well as chess display systems [[8]](#cite_note-8).

# Programs

O’Connell is mentioned as co-author [[9]](#cite_note-9) of following chess programs and computers, competing at ICCA-tournaments:

- [Philidor](/Philidor "Philidor")
- [Cyrus 68K](/Cyrus_68K "Cyrus 68K")
- [SciSys X](/SciSys_X "SciSys X")
