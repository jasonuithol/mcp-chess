**Booot**,  
an [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess program](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") written in [Delphi 6](/Delphi "Delphi") by [Alex Morozov](/Alex_Morozov "Alex Morozov"). Booot determines [sliding piece attacks](/Sliding_Piece_Attacks "Sliding Piece Attacks") with [rotated bitboards](/Rotated_Bitboards "Rotated Bitboards") and applies [PVS](/Principal_Variation_Search "Principal Variation Search") with all common search improvements such as [null move pruning](/Null_Move_Pruning "Null Move Pruning"), [late move reductions](/Late_Move_Reductions "Late Move Reductions") and [IID](/Internal_Iterative_Deepening "Internal Iterative Deepening"). Booot **6.1**, released in October 2016, comes with [lazy SMP](/Lazy_SMP "Lazy SMP") and a completely redesigned [evaluation function](/Evaluation_Function "Evaluation Function") [[2]](#cite_note-2).

# Tournament Play

Booot played the [1st Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries](/CCCCISC_2008 "CCCCISC 2008"), the [CCT9](/CCT9 "CCT9") and [CCT10](/CCT10 "CCT10"), and the [CPT 2010](/CPT_2010 "CPT 2010") and [CPT 2011](/CPT_2011 "CPT 2011"), the [WCCC 2011](/WCCC_2011 "WCCC 2011"), the [ICT 2013](/ICT_2013 "ICT 2013"), the [IGT 2015](/IGT_2015 "IGT 2015"), where it became runner-up behind [Pedone](/Pedone "Pedone"), and the [IGT 2016](/IGT_2016 "IGT 2016"), again second place behind [Chiron](/Chiron "Chiron"). Booot played the [WCCC 2018](/WCCC_2018 "WCCC 2018"), and was close to win the [WCSC 2018](/WCSC_2018 "WCSC 2018"), where it lost the play-off versus [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo"). Since [TCEC Season 10](/TCEC_Season_10 "TCEC Season 10") in 2017, Booot regularly participates at [TCEC](/TCEC "TCEC").

# Code snippets

This is how Booot generates bishop attacks with rotated bitboard lookups in v4.15.1 [[4]](#cite_note-4)

```
Function BishopsMove(from: integer) : bitboard;
var
    indx : integer;
    temp :bitboard;
  begin
    indx:=(AllDh1 shr Dsh1[from]) and MaskDh1[from];
    temp:=RBDh1[from,indx];
    indx:=(AllDa1 shr Dsa1[from]) and MaskDa1[from];
    temp:=temp or RBDa1[from,indx];
    Result:=temp;
  end;
```

...and inlined in v5.1.0 [[5]](#cite_note-5)

```
Function BishopMovesBB(Square : Tsquare;var Board:Tboard):TBitBoard;inline;
 begin
  result:=RotatedBh1[square,((Board.AllBh1 shr ShiftBh1[square]) and MaskBh1[square])]
       or RotatedBa1[square,((Board.AllBa1 shr ShiftBa1[square]) and MaskBa1[square])];
 end;
```
