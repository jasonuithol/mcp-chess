David B. Weller [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**David B. Weller**,  
an American electrical engineer and software developer with interests in [Robot Games](/Robots "Robots") [[2]](#cite_note-2), [Solar Energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_energy) [[3]](#cite_note-3), and chess programming.
David is author of the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") compliant chess engines [GES](/GES "GES") [[4]](#cite_note-4) and [Xpdnt](/Xpdnt "Xpdnt") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
