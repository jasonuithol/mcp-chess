Cascade Atrium, Opryland Hotel [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The [ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Eleventh North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) was held from October 26 to 28, [1980](/Timeline#1980 "Timeline") at the [Opryland Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaylord_Opryland_Resort_%26_Convention_Center), [Nashville](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nashville%2C_Tennessee), [Tennessee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tennessee), adjacent to the famous [Grand Ole Opry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Ole_Opry) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
