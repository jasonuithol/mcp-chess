Karl-Heinz Milaster [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Karl-Heinz Milaster**,  
a German computer chess programmer, author of [ChessBrain](/Chess_Brain "Chess Brain"), a chess computer with an own chess co-processor [[2]](#cite_note-2), which played the 12th [WMCCC 1993](/WMCCC_1993 "WMCCC 1993") in Munich [[3]](#cite_note-3), and author of the commercial chess program [Schach!](/index.php?title=Schach!&action=edit&redlink=1 "Schach! (page does not exist)"), distributed 1996 by the German book and software publisher [Data Becker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_Becker) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
