**Philipp Lenk**,  
a German software engineer, programming enthusiast and educator at [Humboldt University of Berlin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humboldt_University_of_Berlin).
He designed and taught lessons and tutorials and developed exercises for numerous courses including to ones on [C++](/Cpp "Cpp") programming, programming principles, [algorithms](/Algorithms "Algorithms") and data structures,
[operating system principles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operating_system) and [compiler construction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiler#Compiler_construction) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author the [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [paulchen332](/Paulchen332 "Paulchen332"), written in [C++ 17](/Cpp#17 "Cpp").
