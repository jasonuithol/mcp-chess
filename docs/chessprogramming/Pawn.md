**Pawn**, (♙)  
the smallest and initially weakest chess man, restricted to move only one empty square forward, or to control and capture one square diagonally forward. A pawn on its original second (White) or seventh (Black) rank may push two empty squares forward, but may immediately captured [en passant](/En_passant "En passant") if that [double push](/Pawn_Push#DoublePush "Pawn Push") crosses a square conrolled by an opponent pawn. A pawn arriving to its final rank [promotes](/Promotions "Promotions") - most often to a [queen](/Queen "Queen"), rarely to a [rook](/Rook "Rook"), [bishop](/Bishop "Bishop") or [knight](/Knight "Knight").
