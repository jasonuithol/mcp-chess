# Roman Antonczyk

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Roman_Antonczyk

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Roman Antonczyk**

**Roman Antonczyk**,  
an Austrian [[1]](#cite_note-1) computer scientist, in the 90s affiliated with the [Vienna University of Technology](/Vienna_University_of_Technology "Vienna University of Technology") [[2]](#cite_note-2), later with [Siemens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siemens) Austria [[3]](#cite_note-3).
As a computer chess programmer, he is author of the chess program [YChess](/YChess "YChess") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
