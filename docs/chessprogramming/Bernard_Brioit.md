**Bernard Brioit**,  
a French computer chess programmer, and author of the chess program [BBchess](/BBchess "BBchess"), written in [Pascal](/Pascal "Pascal").

# Interview

Quote from an Interview with Bernard Brioit by [Frank Quisinsky](/Frank_Quisinsky "Frank Quisinsky"), May 31, 2002 [[2]](#cite_note-2):

```
I am 55 years old and I develop BBchess for more than 10 years. With Christian Barreteau (AnMon) I began and we to have several years long together operated. Bruno Lucas (Dragon) came then to it and our co-operation was and is still fruitful.
```
