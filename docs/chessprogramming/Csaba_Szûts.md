Csaba Szûts [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Csaba Szûts**,  
a Hungarian chess player and [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author") of the chess programs [Pandix](/Pandix "Pandix") by primary author [Gyula Horváth](/Gyula_Horv%C3%A1th "Gyula Horváth") [[2]](#cite_note-2),
also involved in the commercial release of Pandix ChessFriend [[3]](#cite_note-3), and more recently, [Crux](/Crux "Crux") by primary author [László Szalai](/L%C3%A1szl%C3%B3_Szalai "László Szalai") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
