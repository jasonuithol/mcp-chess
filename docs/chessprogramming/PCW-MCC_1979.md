The **Second PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship** was held November 1-3, [1979](/Timeline#1979 "Timeline"), as part of the [Personal Computer World](/Personal_Computer_World "Personal Computer World") exhibition, [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom).

# Battle for Survival

[Max](/Max "Max") versus [Wizard](/Wizard "Wizard") was reported in [Personal Computing, Vol. 4, No. 6](/Personal_Computing#4_6 "Personal Computing") with remarks given by [Morris Miller](/index.php?title=Morris_Miller&action=edit&redlink=1 "Morris Miller (page does not exist)"), "A comedy of errors in which the readers can supply their own question marks, exclamation points, asterisks and stars" [[4]](#cite_note-4)

```
[Event "PCW-MCC 1979"]
[Site "London"]
[Date "1979.11.02"]
[Round "4"]
[White "Max"]
[Black "Wizard"]

1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nf6 3.Nxe5 Nxe4 4.Qe2 Nd6 5.Nc6+ Be7 6.Nxd8 Kxd8 7.d4

f5 14.Qxg7 Ne4 15.Qxh7 Ng5 16.Qxf5+ Ne6 17.Qxd5+ Bd6 18.Qxd6# 1-0
```
