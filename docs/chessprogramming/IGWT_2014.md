The **IGWT 2014**, the International [Gsei](/G_6 "G 6") Web Tournament II edition, took place in May/June [2014](/Timeline#2014 "Timeline") [[1]](#cite_note-1). After various qualification steps, 8 Italian and 8 guest engines played the [knockout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single-elimination_tournament), won by title holder [Chiron](/Chiron "Chiron") in the final versus [Gaviota](/Gaviota "Gaviota").
