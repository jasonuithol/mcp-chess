The venue of the Championships and Conference [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Sixth International Conference on Computers and Games** was held from September 29 to October 1, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline") at Beijing Golden Century Golf Club, [Qinglonghu Township](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_township-level_divisions_of_Beijing#Fangshan_District), [Fangshan District](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fangshan_District), [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing), [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China), in conjunction with the [13th Computer Olympiad](/13th_Computer_Olympiad "13th Computer Olympiad") and the [16th World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_2008 "WCCC 2008") .
