Martin Giepmans [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Martin Giepmans**,  
was a Dutch computer chess programmer, and author of the chess engines [SpiderChess](/SpiderChess "SpiderChess") and [Anatoli](/Anatoli "Anatoli"), both written in [Delphi](/Delphi "Delphi"). Martin died from [cancer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cancer) in October 2009 [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3) .
