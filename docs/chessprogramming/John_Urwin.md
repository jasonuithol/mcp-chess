**John Urwin**,  
an American sales and marketing manager, and consultant of [Electric power conversion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_power_conversion) technologies.
As former systems programmer and supervisor at [Cromenco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cromemco) with interests in chess and computer chess, he worked with [David Kittinger](/David_Kittinger "David Kittinger") on [MyChess](/MyChess "MyChess"), and became its [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
