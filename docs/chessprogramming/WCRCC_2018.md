The **Twelfth Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship**, October 20/21, [2018](/Timeline#2018 "Timeline") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4) to play on-line via [ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)"). [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") defended its title after a Playoff versus [Chiron](/Chiron "Chiron") and [Rofchade](/Rofchade "Rofchade").
