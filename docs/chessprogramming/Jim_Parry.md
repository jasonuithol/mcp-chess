**Jim Parry**,  
a Canadian computer scientist and early computer chess programmer when he was undergraduate student at [University of Waterloo](/University_of_Waterloo "University of Waterloo") in the mid 70s. Along with [Ron Hansen](/Ron_Hansen "Ron Hansen"), [Russell Crook](/Russell_Crook "Russell Crook"), and [Gary Calnek](/Gary_Calnek "Gary Calnek") [[2]](#cite_note-2), he was co-author of the successful mainframe program [Ribbit](/Ribbit "Ribbit") [[3]](#cite_note-3), competing the [WCCC 1974](/WCCC_1974 "WCCC 1974") and winner of the [ACM 1974](/ACM_1974 "ACM 1974").
