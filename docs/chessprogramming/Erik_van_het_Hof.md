Erik van het Hof [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Erik van het Hof**,  
a Dutch computer scientist and software engineer with a M.Sc. degree from [University of Twente](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Twente) in 1998 [[2]](#cite_note-2), and
affiliations in [Fort Lauderdale](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fort_Lauderdale,_Florida), [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague) and [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
Along with [Hermen Reitsma](/Hermen_Reitsma "Hermen Reitsma"), Erik van het Hof is co-author of the chess engines [BugChess aka QueenMaxima](/BugChess_NL "BugChess NL") [[4]](#cite_note-4) and [Maxima2](/Maxima2 "Maxima2") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
