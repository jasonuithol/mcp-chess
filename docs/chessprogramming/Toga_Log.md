**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* [Toga](/Toga "Toga") \* Toga Log**

**Toga Log**,  
a Toga derivative by Josef D., capable of [logging](/Logging "Logging") details of [evaluation](/Evaluation "Evaluation") in [HTML](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTML) format,
useful as a natural language description of reasonably strong evaluation function. Early versions of [Rodent](/Rodent "Rodent") used the [Toga Log User Manual](#UserManual) as a specification, and the first major divergence had to be made regarding [king safety](/King_Safety "King Safety") [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
