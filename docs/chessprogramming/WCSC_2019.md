**8th World Chess Software Championship 2019**,  
on August 11 and 12, [2019](/Timeline#2019 "Timeline"), in [The Venetian Macau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Venetian_Macao), [Macau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macau), [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China) in conjunction with the [WCCC 2019](/WCCC_2019 "WCCC 2019"), the [22nd Computer Olympiad](/index.php?title=22nd_Computer_Olympiad&action=edit&redlink=1 "22nd Computer Olympiad (page does not exist)") and the [Advances in Computer Games 16](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_16 "Advances in Computer Games 16") conference, all organized by the [ICGA](/ICGA "ICGA"), alongside with the [28th IJCAI conference](/Conferences#IJCAI2019 "Conferences") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Six participants played a [double round robin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Round-robin_tournament) with a time control of 45 minutes per side plus 15 seconds increment per move.
Common hardware was a [Levono](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lenovo) [Windos 10](/Windows "Windows") system with a [Pentium Silver](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goldmont_Plus) [x86-64](/X86-64 "X86-64") processor at 1.1 GHz [[3]](#cite_note-3). [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") was able to defend its title.
