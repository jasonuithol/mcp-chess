**Péter Horváth**,[[2]](#cite_note-2)  
a Hungarian software developer and computer chess programmer. He started chess programming in 1991 - his first chess program was called [Robin](/Robin "Robin") running under [MS-DOS](/MS-DOS "MS-DOS") [[3]](#cite_note-3). Robin was superseded by the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [Asterisk](/Asterisk "Asterisk") in 2002 [[4]](#cite_note-4). His UCI engine [Hussar](/Hussar "Hussar") appeared in in 2009.
