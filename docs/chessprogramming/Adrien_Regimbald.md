**Adrien M. Regimbald**,  
a Canadian computer engineer, chess player, organizer of chess tournaments throughout [Alberta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alberta), and as computer chess programmer author of the [open source chess program](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Faile](/Faile "Faile") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
