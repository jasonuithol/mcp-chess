The **Eleventh Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship**, June 24/25, [2017](/Timeline#2017 "Timeline") [[1]](#cite_note-1) . The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4) to play on-line via [ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)"). [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") defended its title.
