**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Niklas Fiekas**

**Niklas Fiekas**,  
a German chess player and aficionado, open source software developer [[1]](#cite_note-1), and computer scientist affiliated with [Clausthal University of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clausthal_University_of_Technology) [[2]](#cite_note-2) . He is primary author of [python-chess](/Python-chess "Python-chess"), a chess library written in [Python](/Python "Python") featuring [move generation](/Move_Generation "Move Generation") and validation, [PGN](/Portable_Game_Notation "Portable Game Notation") reading [[3]](#cite_note-3) and [opening book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") reading.
