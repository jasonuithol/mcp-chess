The first **Livingston** [[1]](#cite_note-1) [Chess960](/Chess960 "Chess960") **Computer World Championship** was held within the scope of the [Chess Classic](http://www.chesstigers.de/ccm9.php?lang=1) [Mainz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mainz), Germany, August 9-14, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline"), organized by [Hans-Walter Schmitt](/index.php?title=Hans-Walter_Schmitt&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hans-Walter Schmitt (page does not exist)") and his Chess Tigers [[2]](#cite_note-2).
