The **CCT6** was organized by [Volker Richey](/index.php?title=Volker_Richey&action=edit&redlink=1 "Volker Richey (page does not exist)"), played via the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") (ICC) [[1]](#cite_note-1) from January 31 till February 1, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline"), 9 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 45min 10sec increment.
