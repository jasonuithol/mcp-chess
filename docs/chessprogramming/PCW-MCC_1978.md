**[Next >](/PCW-MCC_1979 "PCW-MCC 1979")**

The **First PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship** was held September 1978 as part of the [Personal Computer World](/Personal_Computer_World "Personal Computer World") exhibition, West Center Hotel, [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom).
