**Norbert Esser**, (Norbert Eßer)  
a German chess player [[2]](#cite_note-2), and former avocational computer chess programmer. Along with [Ralf Dörr](/Ralf_D%C3%B6rr "Ralf Dörr"), he is co-author of the private chess program [Nexus](/Nexus "Nexus").
