**Aamir Syed**,  
an [Indian American](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_American) game designer. Along with his father [Omar Syed](/Omar_Syed "Omar Syed"), Aamir Syed designed the game of [Arimaa](/Arimaa "Arimaa") when he was four years old. He was eponym of the game ("Arimaa" is "Aamir" spelled backwards plus an initial "a") [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
