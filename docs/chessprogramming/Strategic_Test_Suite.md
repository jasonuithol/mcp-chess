**Strategic Test Suite**, (STS)  
a series of themed test suites by [Dann Corbit](/Dann_Corbit "Dann Corbit") and [Swaminathan Natarajan](/Swaminathan_Natarajan "Swaminathan Natarajan"), designed to evaluate chess engine's long term understanding of [strategical](/Strategy "Strategy") and positional concepts. More recently, the positions were revised and converted by [Ferdinand Mosca](/Ferdinand_Mosca "Ferdinand Mosca") to be used by an analysis tool [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
