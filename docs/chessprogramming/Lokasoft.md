# Lokasoft

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Lokasoft

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Organizations](/Organizations "Organizations") \* Lokasoft**

**Lokasoft**,  
a Dutch chess software developing and trading company directed by [Lex Loep](/Lex_Loep "Lex Loep"), established since 1992 [[1]](#cite_note-1).
In 2002 Lokasoft took over the activities of [Schröder BV](/Ed_Schroder "Ed Schroder") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
