**Hrafn Eiríksson**,  
an Icelandic computer scientist and software engineer. He holds a B.Sc. and M.Sc. from [Reykjavík University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reykjav%C3%ADk_University) in 2011 and 2014 respectively [[2]](#cite_note-2).
For his B.Sc. thesis, he re-investigated [Multi-Cut pruning](/Multi-Cut "Multi-Cut") in [game-tree search](/Search "Search") under supervision of [Yngvi Björnsson](/Yngvi_Bj%C3%B6rnsson "Yngvi Björnsson")
[[3]](#cite_note-3).
He proposed an enhancement to apply Multi-cut if a [transposition table](/Transposition_Table "Transposition Table") probe indicates a [beta-cutoff](/Beta-Cutoff "Beta-Cutoff") without sufficient [draft](/Depth "Depth") stored.
The experiments as elaborated in his thesis were conducted with his own [open source engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Ziggy](/Ziggy_IS "Ziggy IS"), written in [Java](/Java "Java") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
