The **18th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, November 21-22 and 28-29, [1998](/Timeline#1998 "Timeline").
