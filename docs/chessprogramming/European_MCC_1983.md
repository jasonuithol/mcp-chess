# European MCC 1983

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/European_MCC_1983

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [European Microcomputer Chess Championship](/European_Microcomputer_Chess_Championship "European Microcomputer Chess Championship") \* 4th European MCC 1983**

**[< Prev](/European_MCC_1982 "European MCC 1982")**

The inofficial **4th European Microcomputer Chess Championship** was held September 28 to October 2, [1983](/Timeline#1983 "Timeline") at the [Barbican Centre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbican_Centre) in [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom) as part of the [Personal Computer World Show](/Personal_Computer_World "Personal Computer World"), also known as the 6th [PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship](/PCW_Microcomputer_Chess_Championship "PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship"). [John Nunn](/John_Nunn "John Nunn") served as tournament director [[1]](#cite_note-1).
