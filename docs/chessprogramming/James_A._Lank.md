**James A. Lank**  
an American chess player and former chess programmer. In the early 80s, along with [Lloyd L. Lank](/Lloyd_L._Lank "Lloyd L. Lank"), he developed the [mainframe chess program](/Category:Mainframe "Category:Mainframe") [Cube](/Cube "Cube"), which ran on a [Cray-1](/Cray-1 "Cray-1"), and participated as Cube **2.0** at the [ACM 1980](/ACM_1980 "ACM 1980") [[1]](#cite_note-1), and as Cube **2.1** the [ACM 1981](/ACM_1981 "ACM 1981") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
