The **Nineteenth World Computer Chess Championship** took place from November 19 to 23, [2011](/Timeline#2011 "Timeline"), [Tilburg University](/Tilburg_University "Tilburg University"), [Tilburg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tilburg) [[2]](#cite_note-2) , [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands) in conjunction with the [2nd World Chess Software Championship](/WCSC_2011 "WCSC 2011"), the [16th Computer Olympiad](/16th_Computer_Olympiad "16th Computer Olympiad") and the [Advances in Computer Games 13](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_13 "Advances in Computer Games 13") conference.
