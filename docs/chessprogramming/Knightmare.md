**Knightmare**,  
[Mike Leany's](/Mike_Leany "Mike Leany") first experimental prototypes in chess programming.
Knightmare ran under [Windows](/Windows "Windows") and had minimal support for the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") protocol [[1]](#cite_note-1).
