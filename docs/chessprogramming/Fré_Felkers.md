**Fré Felkers**,  
a Dutch computer chess programmer and author of chess program [Delta](/Delta "Delta"), competing at eleven [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championships](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") from [1985](/DOCCC_1985 "DOCCC 1985") until [1998](/DOCCC_1998 "DOCCC 1998"), the [WMCCC 1990](/WMCCC_1990 "WMCCC 1990") in [Lyon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lyon) [[2]](#cite_note-2) and the [IPCCC 1991](/IPCCC_1991 "IPCCC 1991").
