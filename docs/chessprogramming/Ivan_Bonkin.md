**Ivan Bonkin**, (Иван Бонькин)  
a Russian civil engineer from [Balashikha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balashikha) in [Moscow Oblast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow_Oblast), at times affiliated with the [Moscow State University](/Moscow_State_University "Moscow State University") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
As a computer chess programmer, Ivan Bonkin is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Bison](/Bison "Bison").
