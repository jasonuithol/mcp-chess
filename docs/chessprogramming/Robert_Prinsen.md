# Robert Prinsen

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Robert_Prinsen

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Robert Prinsen**

**Robert Prinsen**,  
a British programmer, who while affiliated with *InterScan Data Systems*, [Hounslow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hounslow), in the 70s [[1]](#cite_note-1), developed the early chess program [A16chs](/A16chs "A16chs") in only seven months
[[2]](#cite_note-2).
Competing at the [First World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_1974 "WCCC 1974"), 1974 in [Stockholm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockholm),
A16chs ran on an Alpha16 minicomputer [[3]](#cite_note-3).
