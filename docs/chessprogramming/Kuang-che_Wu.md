**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Kuang-che Wu**

**Kuang-che Wu**,  
a Taiwanese computer scientist and Chinese Chess programmer. He holds a B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, [National Taiwan University](/National_Taiwan_University "National Taiwan University"). His research interests include [endgame databases](/Endgame_Tablebases "Endgame Tablebases") and [search algorithms](/Search "Search") in [Computer Chinese Chess](/Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess"). Kuang-che Wu is co-author of the Chinese Chess programs [Contemplation](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=112) and [Elephant](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=113) [[1]](#cite_note-1).
