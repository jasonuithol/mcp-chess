**Perola Valfridsson**,  
a Swedish professional programmer specialized within [telecommunication](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Telecommunication) [[2]](#cite_note-2). As computer chess programmer, Perola is author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compatible chess engine [Ruffian](/Ruffian "Ruffian"), which appeared in the computer chess scene in 2002 with promising results [[3]](#cite_note-3). Ruffian won the [DOCCC 2003](/DOCCC_2003 "DOCCC 2003") [[4]](#cite_note-4), and the (unofficial) Swedish Championship in 2003. [MobyGames](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MobyGames) mentions Perola Valfridsson as author of [Kasparov Chessmate](/Kasparov_Chessmate "Kasparov Chessmate") [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6).
