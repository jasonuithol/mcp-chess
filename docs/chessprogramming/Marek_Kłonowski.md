**Marek Kłonowski**,  
a Polish software developer and computer chess programmer. He is author of the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") compliant chess engine [Excelsior](/Excelsior "Excelsior") [[2]](#cite_note-2), which played the [PCCC 2003](/PCCC_2003 "PCCC 2003"), and rewritten from [Delphi](/Delphi "Delphi") to [C#](/C_sharp "C sharp"), the [IOPCCC 2007](/IOPCCC_2007 "IOPCCC 2007").
