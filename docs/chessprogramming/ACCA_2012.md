# ACCA 2012

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/ACCA_2012

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [ACCA Americas' Computer Chess Championship](/ACCA_Americas%27_Computer_Chess_Championship "ACCA Americas' Computer Chess Championship") \* ACCA 2012**

**[< Prev](/ACCA_2011 "ACCA 2011")**

The **2012 Seventh Annual ACCA Americas' Computer Chess Championships** was played November 17 & 18, [2012](/Timeline#2012 "Timeline")
on [ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)"), Round Robin Standard, 45min 10sec increment. Six programs participated. Interestingly, the final ranking was in alphabetic order, the winner was [Arasan](/Arasan "Arasan") by [Jon Dart](/Jon_Dart "Jon Dart").
