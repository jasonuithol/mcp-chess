Atari ST [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Atari ST** was a personal computer released by [Atari Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atari_Corporation) in 1985 and commercially available until the early 90s. The "ST" officially stands for "Sixteen/Thirty-two" [[2]](#cite_note-2) , which referred to the [Motorola](/index.php?title=Motorola&action=edit&redlink=1 "Motorola (page does not exist)") [68000's](/68000 "68000") 16-bit and 32-bit external and internal buses.
