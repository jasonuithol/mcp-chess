# Michael Ehrig

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Michael_Ehrig

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Michael Ehrig**

**Michael Ehrig**,  
a German programmer and primary author of the chess engine [Greif](/Greif "Greif").
Greif played the [WMCCC 1993](/WMCCC_1993 "WMCCC 1993") in Munich [[1]](#cite_note-1), the [Aegon 1994](/Aegon_1994 "Aegon 1994"), and the [IPCCC 1994](/IPCCC_1994 "IPCCC 1994").
At the private [Clodra](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clodra) chess tournament organized by [Rainer Serfling](/Rainer_Serfling "Rainer Serfling") in November 1994,
Greif was recognized as a [clone](/Category:Clone "Category:Clone") of [Chrilly Donninger's](/Chrilly_Donninger "Chrilly Donninger") program [Nimzo 2](/Nimzo "Nimzo") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
