Carmelo Calzerano[[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Carmelo Calzerano**,  
an Italian senior software engineer graduated from [University of Pisa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Pisa) in 1998.
As computer chess programmer and founding member of the [Gruppo Scacchi e Informatica](/G_6 "G 6"), he is author of the chess engine [Leila](/Leila "Leila") [[2]](#cite_note-2), which won the [1st Campionato Italiano per Programmi Scacchistici](/CIPS_2001 "CIPS 2001") 2001 in [Pisa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pisa).
