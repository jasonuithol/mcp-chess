# Carlos Pesce

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Carlos_Pesce

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Carlos Pesce**

**Carlos Pesce**,  
an Argentinian chess player [[1]](#cite_note-1), computer chess expert and [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author").
Carlos worked with various chess program authors not only on their [book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book"), notably with [Vincent Diepeveen](/Vincent_Diepeveen "Vincent Diepeveen") on [Diep](/Diep "Diep") [[2]](#cite_note-2), with [Richard Pijl](/Richard_Pijl "Richard Pijl") on [The Baron](/The_Baron "The Baron") [[3]](#cite_note-3),
with [Dieter Bürßner](/Dieter_B%C3%BCr%C3%9Fner "Dieter Bürßner") on [Yace](/Yace "Yace") [[4]](#cite_note-4), with [Tim Foden](/Tim_Foden "Tim Foden") on [Green Light Chess](/Green_Light_Chess "Green Light Chess") [[5]](#cite_note-5), with [Gian-Carlo Pascutto](/Gian-Carlo_Pascutto "Gian-Carlo Pascutto") on [Deep Sjeng](/Deep_Sjeng "Deep Sjeng"), with [Sergei Markoff](/Sergei_Markoff "Sergei Markoff") on [SmarThink](/SmarThink "SmarThink") [[6]](#cite_note-6), with [Igor Korshunov](/Igor_Korshunov "Igor Korshunov") on [WildCat](/WildCat "WildCat") [[7]](#cite_note-7), with [Carmelo Calzerano](/Carmelo_Calzerano "Carmelo Calzerano") on [Leila](/Leila "Leila"), and with [Miguel A. Ballicora](/Miguel_A._Ballicora "Miguel A. Ballicora") on [Gaviota](/Gaviota "Gaviota") [[8]](#cite_note-8), to name a few.
Carlos was also involved in providing opening books for the [Arena](/Arena "Arena") [GUI](/GUI "GUI").
