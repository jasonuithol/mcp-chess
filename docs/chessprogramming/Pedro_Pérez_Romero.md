# Pedro Pérez Romero

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Pedro_Pérez_Romero

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Pedro Pérez Romero**

**Pedro Pérez Romero**,  
a Spanish [[1]](#cite_note-1)
computer engineer and software developer, at times residing in France while affiliated with [Airbus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As a chess player and [endgame](/Endgame "Endgame") enthusiast, fascinated from [endgame tablebases](/Endgame_Tablebases "Endgame Tablebases"), in particular [Nalimov tablebases](/Nalimov_Tablebases "Nalimov Tablebases"), Pedro Pérez Romero developed the chess endgame tablebase generator [FinalGen](/FinalGen "FinalGen") in his spare time.
