The **2005 Australasian National Computer Chess Championship** (NC3) was held on July 17, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline"), as part of the [Australian National University](/Australian_National_University "Australian National University") Chess Festival [[1]](#cite_note-1) , [Red Hill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Hill,_Australian_Capital_Territory), [Canberra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canberra), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia). Free chess server hosted by [Andrew Tridgell](/Andrew_Tridgell "Andrew Tridgell"), organized by [Shaun Press](/Shaun_Press "Shaun Press") [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
