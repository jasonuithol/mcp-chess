**Frank Schneider**,  
a German computer scientist and chess programmer. Along with co-author [Kai Skibbe](/Kai_Skibbe "Kai Skibbe"), Frank is primary author of the chess engines [GromitChess](/Gromit "Gromit"), [Anaconda](/Anaconda "Anaconda") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [PocketGrandmaster](/PocketGrandmaster "PocketGrandmaster") for [Windows](/Windows "Windows") [Pocket PCs](/index.php?title=Pocket_PC&action=edit&redlink=1 "Pocket PC (page does not exist)") and [mobiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Mobile) [[3]](#cite_note-3). He is further author of [Ginkgo](/Ginkgo "Ginkgo"), debutant at the [WCCC 2015](/WCCC_2015 "WCCC 2015") in [Leiden](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), and author of [Fritz 17](/Fritz "Fritz"), released in November 2019.
