**Jonathan Kreuzer**,  
an American computer scientist and games programmer with focus on [adventure games](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adventure_game), currently senior engineer at [thatgamecompany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thatgamecompany).
Along with *Josh Hess*, Jonathan Kreuzer maintains the *3DKingdoms* site offering games, as well as a [Checkers](/Checkers "Checkers") [Bitboards](/Bitboards "Bitboards") tutorial [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Jonathan Kreuzer is author of the chess programs [Sam](/index.php?title=Sam&action=edit&redlink=1 "Sam (page does not exist)") [[3]](#cite_note-3), and [Slow Chess](/Slow_Chess "Slow Chess"), the latter supporting the [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") protocols, as well featuring an own [GUI](/GUI "GUI") [[4]](#cite_note-4), the [Checkers](/Checkers "Checkers") program *Gui Checkers* [[5]](#cite_note-5), and the [Othello](/Othello "Othello") program *Pointy Stone 3* [[6]](#cite_note-6).
