David Forthoffer [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**David Forthoffer**,  
an American computer scientist, chess player, [national master](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chess_title#Master) [[2]](#cite_note-2), and early [Endgame Tablebases](/Endgame_Tablebases "Endgame Tablebases") researcher.
Along with primary author [Sito Dekker](/Sito_Dekker "Sito Dekker") and [Lars Rasmussen](/Lars_Rasmussen "Lars Rasmussen") he contributed to the automated database generation program [Retro](/index.php?title=Retro&action=edit&redlink=1 "Retro (page does not exist)"), considering both [en passant](/En_passant "En passant") and [castling](/Castling "Castling") [[3]](#cite_note-3), which revealed five discrepancies in KRKB [[4]](#cite_note-4) from the numbers published by [Jaap van den Herik](/Jaap_van_den_Herik "Jaap van den Herik") and [Bob Herschberg](/Bob_Herschberg "Bob Herschberg"), et al.[[5]](#cite_note-5).
