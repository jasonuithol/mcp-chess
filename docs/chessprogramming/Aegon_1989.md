Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Aegon_1989

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Aegon Tournaments](/Aegon_Tournaments "Aegon Tournaments") \* Aegon 1989**

**[< Prev](/Aegon_1988 "Aegon 1988") [Next >](/Aegon_1990 "Aegon 1990")**

The **4th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place in Spring [1989](/Timeline#1989 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). This year, only five rounds were played over a period of three weeks. The humans won by **57½** to **23** [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
