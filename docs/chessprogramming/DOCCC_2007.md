# DOCCC 2007

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2007

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2007**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2006 "DOCCC 2006") [Next >](/DOCCC_2008 "DOCCC 2008")**

The **27th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place October 26 to 28, [2007](/Timeline#2007 "Timeline"), at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was a nine round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 90 minutes per player for the whole game.
