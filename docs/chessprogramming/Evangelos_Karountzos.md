Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Evangelos_Karountzos

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Evangelos Karountzos**

**Evangelos Karountzos**,  
a Greece computer scientist affiliated with [University of Groningen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen) and currently working on [requirements traceability](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Requirements_traceability).
Along with [Zacharias Georgiou](/Zacharias_Georgiou "Zacharias Georgiou"), [Yaroslav Shkarupa](/Yaroslav_Shkarupa "Yaroslav Shkarupa") and [Matthia Sabatelli](/Matthia_Sabatelli "Matthia Sabatelli"), he worked on a project dealing with [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") in simple [chess endgames](/Endgame "Endgame") such as [KRK](/KRK "KRK") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
