**TCEC Season 2**,  
the second TCEC season directed and organized by [Martin Thoresen](/Martin_Thoresen "Martin Thoresen"), held from February 11, 2011 until April 21, 2011.
[Houdini 1.5a](/Houdini "Houdini") defended its title and won the [nTCEC Season 2 Superfinal](#Superfinal) with a margin of **22-18** versus [Rybka 4.1](/Rybka "Rybka").
