The [ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Thirteenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) was held from October, 24 to 26, [1982](/Timeline#1982 "Timeline") at the [Dallas Hilton Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dallas_Hilton), [Dallas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dallas%2C_Texas), [Texas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas), USA [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
