[Rembrandt](/Category:Rembrandt "Category:Rembrandt") - [Pallas Athena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pallas_Athene_(painting)) [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Pallas**,  
a chess program by [Erik Leenders](/Erik_Leenders "Erik Leenders") for the [Atari ST](/Atari_ST "Atari ST").
Pallas participated at four [Dutch Computer Chess Championships](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship"), the [DOCCC 1987](/DOCCC_1987 "DOCCC 1987"), [DOCCC 1988](/DOCCC_1988 "DOCCC 1988"), [DOCCC 1989](/DOCCC_1989 "DOCCC 1989") and finally at the [DOCCC 1993](/DOCCC_1993 "DOCCC 1993").
