# Aegon 1995

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Aegon_1995

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Aegon Tournaments](/Aegon_Tournaments "Aegon Tournaments") \* Aegon 1995**

**[< Prev](/Aegon_1994 "Aegon 1994") [Next >](/Aegon_1996 "Aegon 1996")**

The **10th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from April 26 to 28, and May 1 to 3, [1995](/Timeline#1995 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). After five rounds, [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") had 100% so far even winning from [Yasser Seirawan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yasser_Seirawan), but lost the final round from [John van der Wiel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_van_der_Wiel), who then scored 5½ out of 6 and won the tournament. [MChess Pro](/MChess "MChess") won versus [Larry Christiansen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Larry_Christiansen), [Roberto Cifuentes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roberto_Cifuentes) and [Zsuzsa Polgar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susan_Polgar), to make [Marty Hirsch](/Marty_Hirsch "Marty Hirsch") and by his German [distributor](/Martin_Stamer "Martin Stamer") a bit euphoric [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
