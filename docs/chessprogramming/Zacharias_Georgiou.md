Zacharias Georgiou [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Zacharias Georgiou**,  
a Greek Cypriot computer scientist and Ph.D. student at [University of Cyprus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Cyprus), where he already received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees,
researching in the area of [data analytics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Analytics) in [fog computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fog_computing) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As exchange student at the [University of Groningen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen), he worked on chess - the project work along with [Evangelos Karountzos](/Evangelos_Karountzos "Evangelos Karountzos"), [Yaroslav Shkarupa](/Yaroslav_Shkarupa "Yaroslav Shkarupa") and [Matthia Sabatelli](/Matthia_Sabatelli "Matthia Sabatelli") dealt with [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") in simple [chess endgames](/Endgame "Endgame") such as [KRK](/KRK "KRK") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
