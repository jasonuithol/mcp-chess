**Jaap (J.J.) van Oosterwijk Bruyn** (February 01, 1924 - July 2003),  
was a Dutch mathematician, [actuarial scientist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Actuarial_science), and [gerontologists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gerontology). He was a master chess player [[2]](#cite_note-2) , and in the 80s the first chairman of the [Dutch Computer Chess Federation](/CSVN "CSVN"), as well of the [5th Advances in Computer Chess Comference](/Advances_in_Computer_Chess_5 "Advances in Computer Chess 5") [[3]](#cite_note-3). He also wrote various reports on tournaments and matches in [Computerschaak](/Computerschaak "Computerschaak") [[4]](#cite_note-4) .
