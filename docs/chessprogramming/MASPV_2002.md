The **Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye** (MASPV) **2002** was held from April 19 to 21, [2002](/Timeline#2002 "Timeline"), hosted by the [University of Sopron](/University_of_Sopron "University of Sopron"), [Sopron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sopron), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary).
