The **Sakkprogramok Versenye 2008** [[1]](#cite_note-1) was held from August 30 to 31, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline") in [Budapest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budapest). According to the [MASPV 2009](/MASPV_2009 "MASPV 2009") site [[2]](#cite_note-2), seven programs participated in double round robin with 12 games per player, and the official winner was [Merlin](/Merlin_(HU) "Merlin (HU)"), due the noncompetitive attendance of actual winner [Pandix](/Pandix "Pandix").
