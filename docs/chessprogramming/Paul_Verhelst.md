# Paul Verhelst

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Paul_Verhelst

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Paul Verhelst**

**Paul Verhelst**,  
a Dutch computer chess expert, who published and maintained interesting chess [[1]](#cite_note-1) and [chess programming](/Programming "Programming") sites [[2]](#cite_note-2).
[Ed Schröder](/Ed_Schroder "Ed Schroder") once mentioned the [0x88](/0x88 "0x88") [square difference](/0x88#SquareRelations "0x88") lookup as Paul Verhelst way [[3]](#cite_note-3), which was used and described by [Paul Wiereyn](/Paul_Wiereyn "Paul Wiereyn") [[4]](#cite_note-4) and, according to [David Kittinger](/David_Kittinger "David Kittinger"), was originally attributed to [Mikhail Botvinnik](/Mikhail_Botvinnik "Mikhail Botvinnik") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
