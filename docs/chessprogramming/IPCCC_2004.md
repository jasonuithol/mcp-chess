The thirteenth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**13th IPCCC**) took place February 11-15, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
