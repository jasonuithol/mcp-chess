Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/PCCC_2004

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Polish Computer Chess Championship](/Polish_Computer_Chess_Championship "Polish Computer Chess Championship") \* PCCC 2004**

**[< Prev](/PCCC_2003 "PCCC 2003") [Next >](/PCCC_2005 "PCCC 2005")**

The **3rd Polish Computer Chess Championship**, September 16-19, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline"), [Łódź](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA), [Poland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland). Round robin. [Rzeźnik](/Butcher "Butcher") won the open category after a playoff versus [Matacz](/Matacz "Matacz"), which also was the best program of the uniform category played on [Intel](/Intel "Intel") [Celeron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Celeron) 2.0GHz computers provided by the organization.
