**Alexander Lyashuk**,  
a Belarusian computer scientist and software engineer at [Google](/index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1 "Google (page does not exist)") Switzerland. He graduated in 2005 from [Belarusian State University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarusian_State_University) in [Minsk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minsk).
Alexander Lyashuk is primary developer of [Leela Chess Zero](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero"), in particular the rewritten [Lc0](/Leela_Chess_Zero#Lc0 "Leela Chess Zero") engine
[[2]](#cite_note-2)
[[3]](#cite_note-3), which replaced the initial **LCZero**, running up to ten times faster on [Nvidia](/Nvidia "Nvidia") [GPUs](/GPU "GPU") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
