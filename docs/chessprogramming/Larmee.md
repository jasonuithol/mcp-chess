l'Armée historique [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Larmee**,  
a chess program by [Frans van Gool](/Frans_van_Gool "Frans van Gool") for the [Atari ST](/Atari_ST "Atari ST"). Larmee participated at the [DOCCC 1987](/DOCCC_1987 "DOCCC 1987") on a Atari 1040 ST and gained 3 points out of 7 rounds.
