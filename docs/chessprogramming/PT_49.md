Denksportcentrum Leiden [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **CSVN 49th Programmers Tournament**, took place on October 17-18, [2015](/Timeline#2015 "Timeline"), [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). [Jonny](/Jonny "Jonny") won on resistance points tied with [Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder"), both 6/7, followed by [GridProtector](/GridProtector "GridProtector") and [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") with 5/7.
