The second International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**2nd IPCCC**) took place from October 1-3, [1992](/Timeline#1992 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
