[Porta Nigra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Porta_Nigra) in [Trier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trier), Frank's hometown [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**FCT1**,
a rating list maintained and hosted by [Frank Quisinsky](/Frank_Quisinsky "Frank Quisinsky"), considering [UCI](/Category:UCI "Category:UCI") and [Winboard engines](/Category:WinBoard "Category:WinBoard") running under [Windows 7 Professional SP1](/Windows "Windows") [x64](/X86-64 "X86-64"), 45 minutes per side and game [[2]](#cite_note-2). FCT1 started in May 2014 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
