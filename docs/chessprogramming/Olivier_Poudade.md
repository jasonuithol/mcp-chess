# Olivier Poudade

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Olivier_Poudade

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Olivier Poudade**

**Olivier Poudade**, (Baudsurfer/[Red Sector Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tristar_and_Red_Sector_Incorporated))  
a French [hacker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_%28programmer_subculture%29)
[[1]](#cite_note-1)
and [assembly](/Assembly "Assembly") programmer with focus on [x86](/X86 "X86"). He is member of the [Red Sector Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tristar_and_Red_Sector_Incorporated) (RSI) coding group
[[2]](#cite_note-2) and leads RSI since 2008 as historic original co-founder [[3]](#cite_note-3).
More recently, he is author of the none FIDE chess compliant minimal chess engines [BootChess](/BootChess "BootChess"), released in January 2015 [[4]](#cite_note-4), and [ChessLin](/ChessLin "ChessLin"), published in Spring 2016
[[5]](#cite_note-5),
which even fits in 256 bytes.
