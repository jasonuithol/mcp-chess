Johanna Hellemons [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Johanna (Joke) Hellemons**,  
a Dutch scientist, scientific colleague of [Jaap van den Herik](/Jaap_van_den_Herik "Jaap van den Herik"), and representative of the [ICGA](/ICGA "ICGA") - until August 2008 at the [Maastricht University](/Maastricht_University "Maastricht University"), then at the *Tilburg Centre for Creative Computing* (TiCC) of the [Tilburg University](/Tilburg_University "Tilburg University") [[2]](#cite_note-2), and since 2014 at *Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science* (LIACS), [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), where Johanna Hellemons will continue her work as Editorial Manager of the [ICGA Journal](/ICGA_Journal "ICGA Journal") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
