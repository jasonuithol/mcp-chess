# Jost Triller

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Jost_Triller

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Jost Triller**

**Jost Triller**,  
a German software developer and computer chess programmer, based in Berlin.
He is author of various [open source chess engines](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") available at [GitHub](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GitHub) and [GitLab](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GitLab).
His engine [Hactar](/Hactar "Hactar") was written in [Rust](/Rust "Rust") [[1]](#cite_note-1), [squared-chess](/Squared-chess "Squared-chess") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [Googleplex Starthinker](/Googleplex_Starthinker "Googleplex Starthinker") in [C++](/Cpp "Cpp") [[3]](#cite_note-3),
and the most recent [Nalwald](/Nalwald "Nalwald") written in the [Nim programming language](/Nim_(Programming_Language) "Nim (Programming Language)") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
