**Evgeniĭ Dmitrievich Cherevik**, (Евгений Дмитриевич Черевик)  
a Russian economist with focus on [economic planning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_planning) and expertise in [planned economy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planned_economy). During the early 90s, he was collaborator of [Mikhail Botvinnik](/Mikhail_Botvinnik "Mikhail Botvinnik"), [Vasily Vladimirov](/Vasily_Vladimirov "Vasily Vladimirov") and [Vitaly Vygodsky](/Vitaly_Vygodsky "Vitaly Vygodsky") in working on [Chess Computer Sapiens](/CC_Sapiens "CC Sapiens") and its economic counterpart *Economical Computer Sapiens* [[1]](#cite_note-1).
