**Stephen F. (Steve) Becker**,  
an American attorney, physicist and professor emeritus at [Bucknell University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bucknell_University), [Lewisburg, Pennsylvania](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lewisburg,_Pennsylvania),
holding a law degree and a Ph.D. in physics. During the mid 70s, while already affiliated with Bucknell, he developed the chess program [Sortie](/Sortie "Sortie"), which participated at the [ACM 1975](/ACM_1975 "ACM 1975") in [Minneapolis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minneapolis), [Minnesota](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
