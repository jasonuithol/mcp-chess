The **Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye** (MASPV) **2003** was held from April 18 to 20, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline"), hosted by the [University of Sopron](/University_of_Sopron "University of Sopron"), [Sopron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sopron), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary).
