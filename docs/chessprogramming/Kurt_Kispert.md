Kurt Kispert [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Kurt Kispert**, (November 25, 1959 – August 14, 2020)  
was an Austrian collector of [dedicated chess computers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers") and since 2001, along with [Alwin Gruber](/index.php?title=Alwin_Gruber&action=edit&redlink=1 "Alwin Gruber (page does not exist)"), organizer of the *D.A.CH-Oldie-Tournaments* in [Kaufbeuren](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kaufbeuren) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He further hosted and maintained *Kurt´s Schachcomputer Homepage* [[3]](#cite_note-3),
which was and is an inspiration for many computer chess lovers.
