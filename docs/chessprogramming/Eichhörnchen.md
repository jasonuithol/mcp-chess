Eichhörnchen [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Eichhörnchen**, (Eichhoernchen, белка, belka)  
a private chess engine [[2]](#cite_note-2) written by [Wieland Belka](/Wieland_Belka "Wieland Belka").
Eichhörnchen played the [First Italian Open Chess Software Cup](/IOCSC_2010 "IOCSC 2010"), 2010 in [Carugate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carugate) and the [WCRCC 2019](/WCRCC_2019 "WCRCC 2019").
It further played [Olivier Deville's](/Olivier_Deville "Olivier Deville") [OpenWar](/ChessWar#OpenWar "ChessWar") and [ChessWar](/ChessWar "ChessWar") tournaments [[3]](#cite_note-3).
