**Kim Kåhre**,  
a Finnish high school student at [Kuninkaantie High School](https://fi.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuninkaantien_lukio), [Espoo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Espoo), and in 2019 member of its chess team [[1]](#cite_note-1).
As computer chess programmer, along with [Finn Eggers](/Finn_Eggers "Finn Eggers") and [Eugenio Bruno](/index.php?title=Eugenio_Bruno&action=edit&redlink=1 "Eugenio Bruno (page does not exist)"), Kim Kåhre is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") chess engine [Koivisto](/Koivisto "Koivisto") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
