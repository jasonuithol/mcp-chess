**Paolo Ciancarini**,  
an Italian computer scientist and professor at the [University of Bologna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Bologna). His research interests covers [distributed search](/Parallel_Search "Parallel Search"),
computer chess and [KriegSpiel](/KriegSpiel "KriegSpiel"). Along with [Gian Piero Favini](/index.php?title=Gian_Piero_Favini&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gian Piero Favini (page does not exist)"), Paolo Ciancarini is co-author of the KriegSpiel program and two times Gold Medal winner [Darkboard](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=242), written in [Java](/Java "Java") [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
