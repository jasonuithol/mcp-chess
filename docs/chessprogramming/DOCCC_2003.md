Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2003

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2003**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2002 "DOCCC 2002") [Next >](/DOCCC_2004 "DOCCC 2004")**

The **23rd Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, October 18-19 and 25-26, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline").
