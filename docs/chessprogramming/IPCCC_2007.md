Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/IPCCC_2007

The 17th International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**17th IPCCC**) took place from December 27-30, [2007](/Timeline#2007 "Timeline"), in the [Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum](/Heinz_Nixdorf_MuseumsForum "Heinz Nixdorf MuseumsForum"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany). It was organized by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University") and was held in conjunction with the human chess tournament *Paderborner Schachtuerken-Cup* [[1]](#cite_note-1) .
