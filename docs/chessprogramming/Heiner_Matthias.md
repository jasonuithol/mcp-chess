**Heiner Matthias**,  
a German chess player and [Fide Master](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIDE_Master).
While affiliated with the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), Heiner was the [opening book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") author and chess consultant of the "university" chess programs [Zugzwang](/Zugzwang_(Program) "Zugzwang (Program)") and [P.ConNerS](/P.ConNerS "P.ConNerS") [[2]](#cite_note-2). Heiner Matthias further played the [Aegon 1997](/Aegon_1997 "Aegon 1997") for the carbon side, losing from [CilkChess](/CilkChess "CilkChess") and [Kallisto](/Kallisto "Kallisto"), winning versus [Rajah](/Rajah "Rajah"), [Capture](/Capture_(program) "Capture (program)") and [Ferret](/Ferret "Ferret"), and drawing [HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS").
