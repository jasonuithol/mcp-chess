Casino Graz [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Eighth Computer Olympiad** took place from November 23 to 27, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline"), at Casineum of Casino Graz, in [Graz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graz), [European Capital of Culture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Capital_of_Culture) in 2003, [Austria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria), in conjunction with the [11th World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_2003 "WCCC 2003") and the [10th Advances in Computer Games Conference](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_10 "Advances in Computer Games 10") which were both held nearby at the [Castle Hill, Dom im Berg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schlo%C3%9Fberg_(Graz)). The 9th Computer Olympiad was organized by [Mark Winands](/Mark_Winands "Mark Winands"), and [Jonathan Schaeffer](/Jonathan_Schaeffer "Jonathan Schaeffer") served as tournament director [[2]](#cite_note-2).
