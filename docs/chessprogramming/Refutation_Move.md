# Refutation Move

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Refutation_Move

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Chess](/Chess "Chess") \* [Moves](/Moves "Moves") \* Refutation Move**

*The **Refutation Move** is a [Move Ordering](/Move_Ordering "Move Ordering") related issue.*

A **Refutation Move** in the context of [search algorithms](/Search "Search") like [Alpha-Beta](/Alpha-Beta "Alpha-Beta") or [Principal Variation Search](/Principal_Variation_Search "Principal Variation Search") is a move which [fails high](/Fail-High "Fail-High") at [Cut-nodes](/Node_Types#CUT "Node Types"). It is not necessarily the [best move](/Best_Move "Best Move"), but good enough to refute opponents previous move. Since it is the best move found so far, in the context of [transposition table](/Transposition_Table "Transposition Table"), it is often flippant mentioned as best move as well.
