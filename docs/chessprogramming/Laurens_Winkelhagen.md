Laurens Winkelhagen [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Laurens Winkelhagen**,  
a Dutch computer scientist and senior software engineer. He holds a M.Sc. in [artificial intelligence](/Artificial_Intelligence "Artificial Intelligence") from [Utrecht University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_University) in 2006.
As computer chess programmer, Laurens Winkelhagen is author of the chess engines [JanWillem](/JanWillem "JanWillem"), released in 2004, and [FrankWalter](/FrankWalter "FrankWalter"), first released in 2009 [[2]](#cite_note-2), revised in 2018 [[3]](#cite_note-3), and published as [open source](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") in February 2019 [[4]](#cite_note-4).
