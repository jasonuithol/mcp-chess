**Fauzi Akram Dabat**,  
a Jordanian OTB chess player, [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author"), [correspondence chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Correspondence_chess) player, certified National Chess Arbiter, [Stockfish](/Stockfish "Stockfish") active developer, experienced chess coach, and computer chess aficionado.

# OTB chess

He got his first rating in 2007 with a rating of 1957, reaching a peak rating of 2006 in the year 2015.[[2]](#cite_note-2)

# Arbitration

Fauzi Akram Dabat holds the official title of National Chess Arbiter. In addition to his long-standing experience as a player, and is actively involved in officiating chess events.

# Opening Books

In 2004, he started to create [opening books](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") for different [GUI](/GUI "GUI") and formats, focusing mainly on the [abk-Format](/ABK "ABK") of the [Arena](/Arena "Arena") GUI, and [CTG-Format](/CTG "CTG") [[3]](#cite_note-3).

In August 2015, [Stockfish 6](/Stockfish "Stockfish") by [Marco Costalba](/Marco_Costalba "Marco Costalba"), [Joona Kiiski](/Joona_Kiiski "Joona Kiiski") and [Tord Romstad](/Tord_Romstad "Tord Romstad") topped of the [SSDF](/SSDF "SSDF") ratinglist using the "Aggressive 1.3 by Fauzi.abk" opening book by Fauzi Akram Dabat [[4]](#cite_note-4) [[5]](#cite_note-5).

Fauzi's books proceeded to achieve impressive results also in the Sedat Chess Opening Books Tournaments. [SCCT Book 56 Super](https://sites.google.com/site/computerschess/scct-cs-56-super), [SCCT book Super League 5](https://sites.google.com/site/computerschess/scct-book-nn-cs-5).

# Correspondence chess

Fauzi, Jordan's strongest correspondence chess player, began competing in ICCF in 2019, earning the CCM title in 2021 and the IM title in 2025, reaching a peak rating of 2519.[[6]](#cite_note-6)

He is also the current three-time champion of the IECC Chess Championship, having won the title in the 2022/2023, 2023/2024, and 2024/2025 seasons. *Click on "Championship Tournament" to view the champion of each season.*[[7]](#cite_note-7)

# Coaching and Training

Fauzi is a highly experienced chess coach with more than 15 years of training activity. He has trained numerous players both individually and in group settings, ranging from juniors to advanced competitive players. Several of his trainees have achieved major rating improvements, with multiple players surpassing the 2000 FIDE rating barrier. His coaching focuses on opening preparation, middlegame planning, endgame technique, tournament psychology, and practical over-the-board performance.

# Stockfish Development Contribution

Fauzi embarked on his journey as a [Stockfish](/Stockfish "Stockfish") developer in 2016, when he ran his first test on Fishtest.[[8]](#cite_note-8) Since then, he has become one of the project’s most active and prolific contributors, with over 9,000 tests conducted and more than 220 commits merged into the engine, thanks to his significant contributions and expertise. [[9]](#cite_note-9) [[10]](#cite_note-10)

Fauzi also launched a dedicated YouTube channel: [ChessTubeTree](https://www.youtube.com/@chesstubetree), covering everything related to chess—from opening theory and game analysis to creative content and AI-assisted visual storytelling.
