**Paweł Liskowski**,  
a Polish computer scientist and assistant professor at the Institute of Computing Science,
[Poznań University of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pozna%C5%84_University_of_Technology).
His research interest include various subfields of [artificial intelligence](/Artificial_Intelligence "Artificial Intelligence"),
such as [evolutionary computation](/Genetic_Programming#EvolutionaryComputation "Genetic Programming"), [genetic programming](/Genetic_Programming "Genetic Programming"),
[deep learning](/Deep_Learning "Deep Learning"), [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning"), [machine learning](/Learning "Learning") and [pattern recognition](/Pattern_Recognition "Pattern Recognition")
[[2]](#cite_note-2). His 2012 master's thesis focuses on comparison and analysis of methods that [learn](/Learning "Learning") the [Othello](/Othello "Othello") position evaluation [[3]](#cite_note-3).
