The fourth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**4th IPCCC**) took place in October, [1994](/Timeline#1994 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
