| **iota**, (Iota)  a minimalist [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") by [Daniel White](/Daniel_White "Daniel White"), written in [C](/C "C"), supporting the full rules of chess and a subset of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") protocol [[1]](#cite_note-1). The source code consists of about 1788 characters, not counting comments or white spaces [[2]](#cite_note-2) . It accepts [en passant](/En_passant "En passant"), [minor promotions](/Promotions "Promotions") and [castling](/Castling "Castling") - but will never castle itself [[3]](#cite_note-3). Iota performs [alpha-beta](/Alpha-Beta "Alpha-Beta"), and considers [material](/Material "Material") and [center bonus](/Center_Distance "Center Distance") for pawns, knights and bishops in [evaluation](/Evaluation "Evaluation") [[4]](#cite_note-4). | Ιώτα [[5]](#cite_note-5) |
