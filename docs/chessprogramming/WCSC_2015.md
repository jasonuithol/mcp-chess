**4th World Chess Software Championship 2015**,
from July 04 to July 05, [2015](/Timeline#2015 "Timeline"), Snellius Building [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands), in conjunction with the [WCCC 2015](/WCCC_2015 "WCCC 2015"), [Advances in Computer Games 14](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_14 "Advances in Computer Games 14") [[4]](#cite_note-4), and the [18th Computer Olympiad](/18th_Computer_Olympiad "18th Computer Olympiad"). Hardware for all programs was [Intel](/Intel "Intel") i7-3930K 3.4GHz 4 cores, 16 GiB [RAM](/Memory#RAM "Memory"). Timecontrol was game in 45 minutes per side plus increment of 15 seconds per move. [Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder") by [Stefan Meyer-Kahlen](/Stefan_Meyer-Kahlen "Stefan Meyer-Kahlen") won its 19th title after winning the blitz play-off (5 minutes, 5 seconds) versus [Ginkgo](/Ginkgo "Ginkgo") with 1½ - ½. Both programs achieved 5/7 from the round robin and drew both games from the regular play-off. [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") and [Protector](/Protector "Protector") share third place.
