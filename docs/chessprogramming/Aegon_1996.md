The **11th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from April 10 to 12, and 15 to 17, [1996](/Timeline#1996 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands). Winner with a 100% score was [Yasser Seirawan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yasser_Seirawan), best program was [Quest](/Quest "Quest") with 4½/6.
