MSU main Building in early autumn [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Lomonosov Moscow State University**,   
[Moscow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow), [Russia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia), previously known as **Lomonosov University** or MSU (университет Ломоносова, Universitét Lomonósova; МГУ, MGU), is the oldest and largest university in Russia, founded in 1755, the university was renamed in honor of its founder, [Mikhail Lomonosov](/Mathematician#MLomonosov "Mathematician"), in 1940 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
