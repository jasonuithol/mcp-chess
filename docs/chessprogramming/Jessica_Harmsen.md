Jessica Harmsen [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Jessica Harmsen**,  
a Dutch chess player, [Woman International Master](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIDE_titles#Woman_International_Master_.28WIM.29) and chess [GUI](/GUI "GUI") and chess [database](/Databases "Databases") developer. Jessica played three [Aegon Tournaments](/Aegon_Tournaments "Aegon Tournaments"), [1993](/Aegon_1993 "Aegon 1993"), [1994](/Aegon_1994 "Aegon 1994") and [1995](/Aegon_1995 "Aegon 1995"), and was involved in the [Windows](/Windows "Windows") GUIs and additional chess database development for [Chessica](/Chessica "Chessica") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [Chessmaster 6000 and 7000](/Chessmaster "Chessmaster") [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
