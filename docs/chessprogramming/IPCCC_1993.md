The third International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**3rd IPCCC**) took place from October 1-3, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
