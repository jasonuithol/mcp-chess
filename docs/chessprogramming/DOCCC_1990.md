The **10th Dutch Computer Chess Championship 1990** took place on October 6-7, 13-14, [1990](/Timeline#1990 "Timeline"), at Academic Computer Center Utrecht (ACCU), [Utrecht University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_University), [Utrecht](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_%28city%29), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
