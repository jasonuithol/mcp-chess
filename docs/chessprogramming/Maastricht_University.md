# Important UM Dates

- Until 1996 the University of Limburg in Maastricht [[2]](#cite_note-2)
- In 1996 name change to 'Universiteit Maastricht' [[3]](#cite_note-3)
- 11 January 2008: Name change to 'Maastricht University'
- Until 2008 associated with the Editorial Board of the [ICGA Journal](/ICGA_Journal "ICGA Journal") [[4]](#cite_note-4).
