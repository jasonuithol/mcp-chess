Kasparov Chessmate [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Kasparov Chessmate**,  
a chess program released in 2003 as [Windows](/Windows "Windows"), [Mac OS](/Mac_OS "Mac OS") and [Palm OS](/index.php?title=Palm_OS&action=edit&redlink=1 "Palm OS (page does not exist)") version with [Garry Kasparov](/Garry_Kasparov "Garry Kasparov") co-credited as game designer [[2]](#cite_note-2).
[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page) mentions [The Learning Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Learning_Company) as developer, and [Jamdat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jamdat) as publisher, [MobyGames](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MobyGames) refers [Hexacto Games Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lemonade_Tycoon) and [Mindscape Asia Pacific Pty Ltd.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mindscape) [[3]](#cite_note-3), and credits [Perola Valfridsson](/Perola_Valfridsson "Perola Valfridsson") as author of Kasparov Chessmate [[4]](#cite_note-4). The Kasparov Chessmate chess AI is based on the [Ruffian](/Ruffian "Ruffian") engine [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6).
