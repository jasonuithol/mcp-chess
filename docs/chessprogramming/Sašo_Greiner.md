Sašo Greiner [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Sašo Greiner**,  
a Slovenian computer scientist at *Computer Architecture and Languages Laboratory* [[2]](#cite_note-2), Institute of Computer Science at [University of Maribor](/University_of_Maribor "University of Maribor").
His research interests include [programming languages](/Languages "Languages"), [compiler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiler) and [computer architectures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_architecture), [virtual machines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_machine), and [evolutionary computation](/Genetic_Programming#EvolutionaryComputation "Genetic Programming"), in particular [differential evolution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Differential_evolution) as applied in [automated tuning](/Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") of chess engines.
