**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* Pschach**

**Pschach**,  
a private chess program by [Peter Sucker](/Peter_Sucker "Peter Sucker"), written in [Pascal](/Pascal "Pascal") [[1]](#cite_note-1).
Pschach played the [WMCCC 1993](/WMCCC_1993 "WMCCC 1993") in [Munich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Munich), and finished last with a draw versus [Diogenes](/Diogenes "Diogenes").
