**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Denis Grafen**

**Denis Grafen**,  
a German senior system engineer [[1]](#cite_note-1)
and pretended author of [Deep<9>](/Deep9 "Deep9"), which was a [clone](/Category:Clone "Category:Clone") of [Pepito](/Pepito "Pepito") by [Carlos del Cacho](/Carlos_del_Cacho "Carlos del Cacho") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3). Deep<9> played the [CSS](/Computerschach_und_Spiele "Computerschach und Spiele") Online-Masters in 2002/2003 [[4]](#cite_note-4),
and was initially registered to play the [IPCCC 2003](/IPCCC_2003 "IPCCC 2003") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
