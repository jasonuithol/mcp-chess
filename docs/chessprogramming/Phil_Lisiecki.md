**Philip Andrew (Phil) Lisiecki**,  
an American computer scientist and chief architect at [Akamai Technologies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akamai_Technologies) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
While affiliated with [Massachusetts Institute of Technology](/Massachusetts_Institute_of_Technology "Massachusetts Institute of Technology") during the late 90s, he was member of the team in developing the [CilkChess](/CilkChess "CilkChess") massive parallel computer chess program [[4]](#cite_note-4) .
