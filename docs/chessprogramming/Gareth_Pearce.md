**Gareth Pearce**, (Tilps)  
an Australian physicist, computer scientist and software engineer at [Google](/index.php?title=Google&action=edit&redlink=1 "Google (page does not exist)") Australia. He holds a B.Sc. in physics and computer science from [University of Sydney](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Sydney) in 2002.
As contributor [[1]](#cite_note-1) and administrator of the [Leela Chess Zero](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero") project, he is LCZero's lead training coordinator [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Actually he works on back-porting [Ceres'](/Ceres "Ceres") [MCTS](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") algorithmic improvements to [Lc0](/Leela_Chess_Zero#Lc0 "Leela Chess Zero"), such as the parallel multiple gathering [[3]](#cite_note-3).
