The **Fifth Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship** was held July 23-24, [2011](/Timeline#2011 "Timeline"). The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4), played on-line over the internet ([ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)")).
