Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2010

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2010**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2009 "DOCCC 2009") [Next >](/DOCCC_2011 "DOCCC 2011")**

The **30th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place November 26-28, [2010](/Timeline#2010 "Timeline"), [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). Twenty Participants played a nine round [Swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss_system_tournament), 90 minutes per game and side.
