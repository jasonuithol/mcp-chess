**Gerhard Mehlsam**,  
an Austrian computer scientist. He defended his Ph.D. at [Vienna University of Technology](/Vienna_University_of_Technology "Vienna University of Technology") in 1989 [[1]](#cite_note-1),
and continued his research along with [Hermann Kaindl](/Hermann_Kaindl "Hermann Kaindl") and [Wilhelm Barth](/Wilhelm_Barth "Wilhelm Barth") on *Feature Construction during Tree Learning*,
which addresses the “problem of new terms” in the context of [learning decision trees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decision_tree_learning) using the approach based on [Ross Quinlan's](/Ross_Quinlan "Ross Quinlan") [Iterative Dichotomiser 3](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ID3_algorithm) (ID3) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
