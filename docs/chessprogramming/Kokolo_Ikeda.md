Kokolo Ikeda [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Kokolo Ikeda**,  
a Japanese computer scientist, games researcher and associate professor at [Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology](/JAIST "JAIST"). He holds a M.Sc. and Ph.D from [Tokyo Institute of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo_Institute_of_Technology) in 2000 and 2003 respectively. His research interests include [game](/Games "Games") [informatics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Informatics), [Monte-Carlo tree search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search"), [evolutionary algorithm](/Genetic_Programming#EvolutionaryAlgorithms "Genetic Programming"), [machine learning](/Learning "Learning"), and [agent-based](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agent-based_model) [simulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simulation).

# Ikeda Laboratory

Kokolo Ikeda is head of the **Ikeda Laboratory** at JAIST [[2]](#cite_note-2) . Their goal is not only to make strong AI players for various games, but "realistic", "enjoyable" and/or "educational" agents. In addition, [genetic algorithm](/Genetic_Programming#GeneticAlgorithm "Genetic Programming"), [multi-objective optimization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multi-objective_optimization) and [multi-agent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multi-agent_system) simulation are intensively studied.

# Nomitan

As computer [Go](/Go "Go") programmer, Kokolo Ikeda is co-author of the Go playing program [Nomitan](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/program.php?id=625), an [UCT](/UCT "UCT") based original program using an [evaluation function](/index.php?title=Evaluation_function&action=edit&redlink=1 "Evaluation function (page does not exist)") [optimized](/Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") to game records of [professional players](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Go_professional). Nomitan won three times Bronze at [Yokohama 2013](/17th_Computer_Olympiad#Go "17th Computer Olympiad"), in [9x9](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/tournament.php?id=267), [13x13](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/tournament.php?id=272) and [19x19](https://www.game-ai-forum.org/icga-tournaments/tournament.php?id=273) Go [[4]](#cite_note-4).
