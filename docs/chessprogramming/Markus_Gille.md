Markus Gille [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Markus Gille**,  
a German chess programmer and chess player [[2]](#cite_note-2), along with [Ernst A. Heinz](/Ernst_A._Heinz "Ernst A. Heinz") and [Peter Gillgasch](/Peter_Gillgasch "Peter Gillgasch"), author of the computer chess program [DarkThought](/DarkThought "DarkThought"). Beside programming on DarkThought, Markus was also responsible of its [opening book](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") - and represented and operated DarkThought at various tournaments.
