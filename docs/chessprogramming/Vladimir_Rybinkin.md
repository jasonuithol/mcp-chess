**Vladimir Rybinkin**,  
a Russian computer scientist with expertise in [databases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Database), affiliated with the WSEAS (World Scientific and Engineering Academy and Society) [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
As former computer chess programmer, Vladimir Rybinkin is primary author of the chess program [Mirage](/Mirage "Mirage"), supported by [Yuri Shpeer](/Yuri_Shpeer "Yuri Shpeer").
