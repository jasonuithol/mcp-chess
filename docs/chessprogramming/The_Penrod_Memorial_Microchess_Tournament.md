# The Penrod Memorial Microchess Tournament

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/The_Penrod_Memorial_Microchess_Tournament

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [American Microcomputer Chess Tournaments](/American_Microcomputer_Chess_Tournaments "American Microcomputer Chess Tournaments") \* The Penrod Memorial Microchess Tournament**

The **Penrod Memorial Microchess Tournament**, held early [1979](/Timeline#1979 "Timeline"), in remembrance to [Douglas Penrod](/Douglas_Penrod "Douglas Penrod"), editor of the [Computer Chess Newsletter](/ICGA_Journal#ComputerChessNewsletter "ICGA Journal"), who implemented the [First Microcomputer Chess Tournament](/MCCT_1978 "MCCT 1978") at the Second [West Coast Computer Faire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Coast_Computer_Faire), [San Jose, California](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Jose,_California) [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2), and who died in November 1978.
