**Luca-Mihnea Metehau**, (Luca Metehău)  
a Romanian youth chess player, [10th grade](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tenth_grade) student at the [Tudor Vianu National College of Computer Science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tudor_Vianu_National_College_of_Computer_Science), participant of national and international Junior [Olympiads in Informatics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Olympiad_in_Informatics), and bronze medal winner of the Junior Balkan Olympiad in Informatics in 2019 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Combining his both passions, Luca Metehau is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Clover](/Clover "Clover"), first released in April 2021 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
