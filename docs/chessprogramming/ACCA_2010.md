The **2010 Fifth Annual ACCA Americas' Computer Chess Championships** was played November 6 & 7, [2010](/Timeline#2010 "Timeline"), on [Free Internet Chess Server](/index.php?title=Free_Internet_Chess_Server&action=edit&redlink=1 "Free Internet Chess Server (page does not exist)") (FICS), 6 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 45min 10sec increment.
