**Yoshikuni Sato**,  
a Japanese computer scientist at Graduate School of Systems and Information Engineering, Center for Computational Sciences, [University of Tsukuba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tsukuba). His research interests includes [Monte-Carlo Tree Search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") applied to [Shogi](/Shogi "Shogi"). His paper *A Shogi Program based on Monte-Carlo Tree Search* [[1]](#cite_note-1) won the 2010 [ICGA Journal Award](/ICGA_Journal#JournalAward "ICGA Journal").
