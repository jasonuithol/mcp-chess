The **2nd Spanish Open Computer Chess Championship** was held on October 29 to November 01, [1994](/Timeline#1994 "Timeline"), at [Polytechnic University of Valencia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polytechnic_University_of_Valencia), [Valencia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valencia), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain).
