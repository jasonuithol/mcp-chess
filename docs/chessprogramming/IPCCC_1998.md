The seventh International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**7th IPCCC**) took place from February 11-15, [1998](/Timeline#1998 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
