Tobias Joppen [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Tobias Joppen**,  
a German computer scientist affiliated with [TU Darmstadt](/Darmstadt_University_of_Technology "Darmstadt University of Technology") and scientific staff in the [knowledge](/Knowledge "Knowledge") engineering group headed by [Johannes Fürnkranz](/Johannes_F%C3%BCrnkranz "Johannes Fürnkranz") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
His research interests include [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning"), [general game playing](/General_Game_Playing "General Game Playing") and [Monte-Carlo tree search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
