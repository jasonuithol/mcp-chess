The **1994 QMW Uniform-Platform Computer Chess Championship** was held August 1-8, [1994](/Timeline#1994 "Timeline"), at [Queen Mary and Westfield College](/Queen_Mary,_University_of_London "Queen Mary, University of London") of [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), organized by [Don Beal](/Don_Beal "Don Beal"). All games were played automatically.
