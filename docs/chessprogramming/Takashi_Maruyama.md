**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Takashi Maruyama**

**Takashi Maruyama**,  
a Japanese computer scientist and [Shogi](/Shogi "Shogi") programmer. He is co-author of the Shogi engine [Gekisashi](/index.php?title=Gekisashi&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gekisashi (page does not exist)") [[1]](#cite_note-1), which won the Gold medal at the [15th Computer Olympiad, Shogi](/15th_Computer_Olympiad#Shogi "15th Computer Olympiad") 2010 in [Kanazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa,_Ishikawa).
