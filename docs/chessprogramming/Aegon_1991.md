The **6th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from May 22 to 24, and 27 to 29, [1991](/Timeline#1991 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
