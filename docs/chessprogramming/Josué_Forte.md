Josué Forte [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Josué Forte**,  
a Brazilian computer scientist, computer chess programmer and author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compatible chess engine [Matheus](/Matheus "Matheus"),
which already had its tournament debut at [Don Beal's](/Don_Beal "Don Beal") [Uniform-Platform CCC 1994](/UPCCC_1994 "UPCCC 1994") [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and was first published as [WinBoard engine](/Category:WinBoard "Category:WinBoard") in 2002 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
