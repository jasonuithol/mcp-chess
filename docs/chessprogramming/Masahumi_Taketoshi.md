**Masahumi Taketoshi**, (Masafumi Taketoshi [[1]](#cite_note-1))  
a Japanese computer scientist, in 2002 affiliated with Departments of Computer Science, [Shizuoka University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shizuoka_University), [Hamamatsu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamamatsu,_Shizuoka). As [Shogi](/Shogi "Shogi") programmer he is co-author of [Tacos](/index.php?title=Tacos&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tacos (page does not exist)") [[2]](#cite_note-2), the four times Gold medal winner at [Computer Olympiads](/Computer_Olympiad "Computer Olympiad").
