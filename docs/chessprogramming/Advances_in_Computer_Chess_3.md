The **Advances in Computer Chess 3** conference was held at [Imperial College](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_College_London), [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [UK](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom), on April 9-10, [1981](/Timeline#1981 "Timeline"). The conference was planned and organized by [Mike Clarke](/Mike_Clarke "Mike Clarke"), who especially welcomed [Mikhail Botvinnik](/Mikhail_Botvinnik "Mikhail Botvinnik") at the opening ceremony [[2]](#cite_note-2).

# Lectures

1. [Ivan Bratko](/Ivan_Bratko "Ivan Bratko"), [Matjaž Gams](/Matja%C5%BE_Gams "Matjaž Gams") (**1982**). *Error Analysis of the Minimax Principle*.
2. [Don Beal](/Don_Beal "Don Beal") (**1982**). *Benefits of minimax search*.
3. [Kevin Coplan](/Kevin_Coplan "Kevin Coplan") (**1982**). *A Special-Purpose Machine for an Improved Search Algorithm for Deep Chess Combinations*.
4. [Joe Condon](/Joe_Condon "Joe Condon"), [Ken Thompson](/Ken_Thompson "Ken Thompson") (**1982**). *Belle Chess Hardware*.
5. [Ken Thompson](/Ken_Thompson "Ken Thompson") (**1982**). *Computer Chess Strength*.
6. [Ivan Bratko](/Ivan_Bratko "Ivan Bratko"), [Danny Kopec](/Danny_Kopec "Danny Kopec") (**1982**). *A Test for Comparison of Human and Computer Performance in Chess*. [pdf](http://www.sci.brooklyn.cuny.edu/%7Ekopec/Publications/Publications/O_41_C.pdf)
7. [Max Bramer](/Max_Bramer "Max Bramer") (**1982**). *Machine-Aided Refinement of Correct Strategies for the Endgame in Chess.*
8. [Alen Shapiro](/Alen_Shapiro "Alen Shapiro"), [Tim Niblett](/Tim_Niblett "Tim Niblett") (**1982**). *Automatic Induction of Classification Rules for Chess End game.*
9. [Thomas Nitsche](/Thomas_Nitsche "Thomas Nitsche") (**1982**). *A Learning Chess Program.*
10. [Max Bramer](/Max_Bramer "Max Bramer"), [B. E. P. Alden](http://www.informatik.uni-trier.de/~ley/pers/hy/a/Alden:B=_E=_P=.html) (**1982**). *A Program for Solving Retrograde Analysis Chess Problems.*
11. [Donald Michie](/Donald_Michie "Donald Michie") (**1982**). *Information and Complexity in Chess*.
12. [Hermann Kaindl](/Hermann_Kaindl "Hermann Kaindl") (**1982**). *Positional Long-Range Planning in Computer Chess.*
13. [Mikhail Botvinnik](/Mikhail_Botvinnik "Mikhail Botvinnik") (**1982**). *Decision Making and Computers.*
