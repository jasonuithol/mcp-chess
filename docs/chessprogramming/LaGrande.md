# LaGrande

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/LaGrande

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* LaGrande**

**LaGrande**,  
a chess program by [Gabriele Müller](/Gabriele_M%C3%BCller "Gabriele Müller") as successor of her [Crafty](/Crafty "Crafty") [clone](/Category:Clone "Category:Clone") [Voyager](/Voyager "Voyager").
Voyager, LaGrande and its "sister" [LaPetite](/LaPetite "LaPetite") were promoted by [Frank Quisinsky](/Frank_Quisinsky "Frank Quisinsky"),
when he initially assumed Voyager an experimental private chess program by Gabriele Müller [[1]](#cite_note-1), even after it was exposed as clone in February 1999 [[2]](#cite_note-2), but later admitted the evidence of cloning [[3]](#cite_note-3). LaGrande was the "sister" of the third clone LaPetite, which was according to Quisinsky a much more aggressive and speculative player [[4]](#cite_note-4).
