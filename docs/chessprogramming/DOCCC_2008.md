**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2007 "DOCCC 2007") [Next >](/DOCCC_2009 "DOCCC 2009")**

The **28th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place November 14 to 16, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"), at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). With 10 participants a 9 rounds robin tournament was played, 90 minutes per player for the whole game.
