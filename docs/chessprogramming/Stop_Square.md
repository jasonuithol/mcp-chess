# Stop Square

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Stop_Square

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Chess](/Chess "Chess") \* [Squares](/Squares "Squares") \* Stop Square**

[![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8b/Stop_Defacing_Signs.jpg/250px-Stop_Defacing_Signs.jpg)](/File:Stop_Defacing_Signs.jpg)

Stop defacing Stop signs! [[1]](#cite_note-1)

In a terminology of [Hans Kmoch](/Hans_Kmoch "Hans Kmoch") [[2]](#cite_note-2) , a **stop square** of a [pawn](/Pawn "Pawn") is a square where it can arrive in one non-capturing move, a [pawn push](/Pawn_Push "Pawn Push"). **Telestops** are the further squares behind the stop on the pawn's [front-span](/Pawn_Spans "Pawn Spans"), the final one the [promotion square](/Promotion_Square "Promotion Square").
