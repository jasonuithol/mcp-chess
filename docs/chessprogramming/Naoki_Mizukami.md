**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Naoki Mizukami**

**Naoki Mizukami**,  
a Japanese computer scientist affiliated with the Graduate School of Engineering, [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo). His research interests include [Monte-Carlo tree search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search") and [UCT](/UCT "UCT") considering [opponent models](/Opponent_Model_Search "Opponent Model Search"), in particular applied to the game of [Mahjong](/index.php?title=Mahjong&action=edit&redlink=1 "Mahjong (page does not exist)") [[1]](#cite_note-1), and [deep learning](/Deep_Learning "Deep Learning").
