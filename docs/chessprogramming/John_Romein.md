**Johannes Willem (John) Romein** (born March 26, 1970),  
a Dutch computer scientist at [ASTRON](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ASTRON), the Netherlands Foundation for Research in [Astronomy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astronomy). Before, he was postdoctoral researcher working on parallel [algorithms](/Algorithms "Algorithms") in the [bioinformatics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bioinformatics) department at [Vrije Universiteit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vrije_Universiteit), [Amsterdam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam), and defended his Ph.D. on *Multigame - An Environment for Distributed Game-Tree Search* in 2001 under the supervision of [Henri Bal](/Henri_Bal "Henri Bal") [[1]](#cite_note-1). Multigame is a [language](/Languages "Languages") for the purpose of [general game playing](/General_Game_Playing "General Game Playing") using a [parallel search](/Parallel_Search "Parallel Search") and [distributed computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Distributed_computing). In 2002, along with [Henri Bal](/Henri_Bal "Henri Bal"), he solved the game of [Awari](/Awari "Awari") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
