[ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Fourteenth North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) was simultaneously the [Fourth Computer World Championship](/WCCC_1983 "WCCC 1983"). It took place from October 22 to 25, [1983](/Timeline#1983 "Timeline"), in the Royal Ballroom A in the [Sheraton Center Hotel](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sheraton_New_York_Hotel_and_Towers) [[2]](#cite_note-2), [New York](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City), [USA](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). [Cray Blitz](/Cray_Blitz "Cray Blitz") was the sole winner with 4½ out of 5.
