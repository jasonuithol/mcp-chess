Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/BlueFeverSoft

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* BlueFeverSoft**

**BlueFeverSoft**,  
a hobby programmer doing video series on [YouTube](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube) to explain to anyone curious about starting in [programming](/Programming "Programming") including Computer Chess in [C](/C "C") and [JavaScript](/JavaScript "JavaScript").
He enjoys all aspects of programming, and is constantly writing little games and utilities for himself [[1]](#cite_note-1).
His didactic C engine as developed in his video is called [Vice](/Vice "Vice") (Video Instructional Chess Engine) [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
