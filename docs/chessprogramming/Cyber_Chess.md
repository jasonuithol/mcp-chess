**Cyber Chess**,  
a commercial chess program for the [Acorn Archimedes](/Acorn_Archimedes "Acorn Archimedes") with [ARM-architecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_ARM_microarchitectures) and [RISC OS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RISC_OS), including a chess engine, animated [3D graphics](/3D_Graphics_Board "3D Graphics Board"), [sound effects](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sound_effect) and a full state-of-the-art [user interface](/GUI "GUI"), developed in 1992/93 by [William Tunstall-Pedoe](/William_Tunstall-Pedoe "William Tunstall-Pedoe"), and published by [The Fourth Dimension](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fourth_Dimension_%28company%29) [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3)..
