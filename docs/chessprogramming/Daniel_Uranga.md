# Daniel Uranga

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Daniel_Uranga

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Daniel Uranga**

**Daniel Uranga**,  
an Argentinian engineer and computer chess programmer.
He is author of the chess engine [RataAeroespacial](/index.php?title=RataAeroespacial&action=edit&redlink=1 "RataAeroespacial (page does not exist)") [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and more recently contributed to [Leela Chess Zero](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero"). He made numerous changes to both the binary and the training code
and helped [Albert Silver](/Albert_Silver "Albert Silver") with his efforts leading to [Fat Fritz](/Fat_Fritz "Fat Fritz") [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
