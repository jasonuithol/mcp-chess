The tenth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**10th IPCCC**) took place 20-25 February, [2001](/Timeline#2001 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
