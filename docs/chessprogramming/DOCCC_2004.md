Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2004

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2004**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2003 "DOCCC 2003") [Next >](/DOCCC_2005 "DOCCC 2005")**

The **24th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, October 16-17 and 23-24, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline").
