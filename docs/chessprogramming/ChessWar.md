**ChessWar**,  
an [Engine](/Engines "Engines") tournament site, hosted and administrated by [Olivier Deville](/Olivier_Deville "Olivier Deville"). The ChessWar tournaments took first place in 2003, abandoned in Sepetember 2012 [[1]](#cite_note-1). Further tournaments during that time were OpenWar and AEGT [[2]](#cite_note-2).
