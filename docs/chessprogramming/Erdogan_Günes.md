**Erdogan (Erdo) Günes**,  
a Turkish computer chess expert and [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author") residing in Germany.
Erdo cooked [opening books](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") for various programs, notably at the [WCCC 2010](/WCCC_2010 "WCCC 2010") in [Kanazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa,_Ishikawa) even as team member of two participating programs,
[Rondo](/Rondo "Rondo") by [Zach Wegner](/Zach_Wegner "Zach Wegner") and [Anthony Cozzie](/Anthony_Cozzie "Anthony Cozzie") as well as [Fridolin](/Fridolin "Fridolin") by [Christian Sommerfeld](/Christian_Sommerfeld "Christian Sommerfeld").
Further programs supported and operated by Erdogan were [Deep Sjeng](/Deep_Sjeng "Deep Sjeng") by [Gian-Carlo Pascutto](/Gian-Carlo_Pascutto "Gian-Carlo Pascutto") [[2]](#cite_note-2),
[HIARCS](/HIARCS "HIARCS") by [Mark Uniacke](/Mark_Uniacke "Mark Uniacke"), [Goliath](/Goliath "Goliath") by [Michael Borgstädt](/Michael_Borgst%C3%A4dt "Michael Borgstädt"), [Hydra](/Hydra "Hydra") by [Chrilly Donninger](/Chrilly_Donninger "Chrilly Donninger"), [Zappa](/Zappa "Zappa") by [Anthony Cozzie](/Anthony_Cozzie "Anthony Cozzie"),
[Falcon](/Falcon "Falcon") by [Eli David](/Eli_David "Eli David") [[3]](#cite_note-3),
and [Komodo](/Komodo "Komodo") by [Don Dailey](/Don_Dailey "Don Dailey"), [Mark Lefler](/Mark_Lefler "Mark Lefler"), and [Larry Kaufman](/Larry_Kaufman "Larry Kaufman").
His tremendous gambling and operating skills, specially in [Blitz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess), was a warrant for top places, titles and Blitz titles [[4]](#cite_note-4) at [World](/World_Computer_Chess_Championship "World Computer Chess Championship") [[5]](#cite_note-5) and [World Microcomputer Chess Championships](/World_Microcomputer_Chess_Championship "World Microcomputer Chess Championship").
Most recently, Erdo operated Komodo during the [WCCC 2016](/WCCC_2016 "WCCC 2016"), [WCCC 2017](/WCCC_2017 "WCCC 2017"), [WCCC 2018](/WCCC_2018 "WCCC 2018") and [WCCC 2019](/WCCC_2019 "WCCC 2019") winning four times the [Shannon Trophy](/Shannon_Trophy "Shannon Trophy"), not to mention WCCC Blitz titles and winning three times the [World Chess Software Championship](/World_Chess_Software_Championship "World Chess Software Championship") [WCSC 2016](/WCSC_2016 "WCSC 2016"), [WCSC 2018](/WCSC_2018 "WCSC 2018") and [WCSC 2019](/WCSC_2019 "WCSC 2019") with Komodo.

# Döner Gambit

Erdogan is famous for his formidable opening ideas and preparation. Well known is the [Döner](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D%C3%B6ner_kebab) Gambit, a [Blackmar-Diemer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blackmar-Diemer_Gambit) like opening, extensively analyzed by Günes, which usually occurs as following: 1. d4 d5 2. Nc3 Nf6 3. f3 with later e4 to encourage black to capture e4 and f3.
[Christopher Lutz](/Christopher_Lutz "Christopher Lutz") proved Döner Gambit quite harmless, if black exchanges queens and give back material [[6]](#cite_note-6) [[7]](#cite_note-7).
