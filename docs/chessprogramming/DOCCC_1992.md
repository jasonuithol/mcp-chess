The **12th Dutch Computer Chess Championship** took place on October 24, 25, 31, November 01, [1992](/Timeline#1992 "Timeline") at Academic Computer Center Utrecht (ACCU), [Utrecht University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_University), [Utrecht](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utrecht_%28city%29), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
