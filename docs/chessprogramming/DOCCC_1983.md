The **3rd Dutch Computer Chess Championship** took place August 19-21, and 26-28, [1983](/Timeline#1983 "Timeline"), at the Centraal Reken Instituut of [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).
