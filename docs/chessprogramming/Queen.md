Queen's movements in chess [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Queen**,  
a [sliding piece](/Sliding_Pieces "Sliding Pieces") moving along the [ranks](/Ranks "Ranks"), [files](/Files "Files"), [diagonals](/Diagonals "Diagonals") and [anti-diagonals](/Anti-Diagonals "Anti-Diagonals"), thus combining the movements of [Rook](/Rook "Rook") and [Bishop](/Bishop "Bishop") and therefor the most powerful piece in the game of chess.
Along with the Rook, the Queen is considered a [major or heavy piece](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glossary_of_chess#Major_piece).
