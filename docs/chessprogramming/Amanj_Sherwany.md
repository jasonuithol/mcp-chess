**Amanj Sherwany**,  
born in Kurdistan/Iraq, he is a computer scientist [[2]](#cite_note-2) and the author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") chess engine [Zahak](/Zahak "Zahak"), first released in February 2021 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
