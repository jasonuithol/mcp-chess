Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Dirk_Frickenschmidt

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Dirk Frickenschmidt**

**Dirk Frickenschmidt**,  
a German [evangelical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evangelical_Church_in_Germany#Name) [pastor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pastor#Protestantism) in [Unterbarmen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barmen), [Wuppertal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wuppertal) [[1]](#cite_note-1) [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), computer chess expert, author of a German computer chess book [[4]](#cite_note-4), and one of the founders of [CCC](/CCC "CCC") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
