[![](/images/thumb/e/e2/SamuelBakVariousOpenings.jpg/300px-SamuelBakVariousOpenings.jpg)](http://chgs.elevator.umn.edu/asset/viewAsset/57f3b6787d58ae5f74bf8ba9#57f3b6d77d58ae5574bf8bc0)

[Samuel Bak](/Category:Samuel_Bak "Category:Samuel Bak") - Various Openings [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Opening** is the first game phase from the [initial starting position](/Initial_Position "Initial Position") with the aim to [develop](/Development "Development") the [minor pieces](/Pieces "Pieces"), to [control the center](/Center_Control "Center Control") and depending on the [center](/Pawn_Center "Pawn Center") [pawn structure](/Pawn_Structure "Pawn Structure"), to prepare or achieve a safe square for the [king](/King "King"), most likely by [castling](/Castling "Castling").

Some programs apply special knowledge in their evaluation for this early game stage, most notably to encourage all minor pieces to develop, to prepare castling options, and to penalize too early [queen](/Queen "Queen") development, which either hinders own minor pieces, or to become target of opponent attacks with the consequence to eventually lose some [tempo](/Tempo "Tempo").
