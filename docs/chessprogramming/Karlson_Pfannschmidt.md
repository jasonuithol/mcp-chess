Karlson Pfannschmidt [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Karlson Pfannschmidt**, (Kiudee)  
a German computer scientist specialized in [machine learning](/Learning "Learning"), affiliated with the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University").
He designed and published tools for local and distributed [tuning](/Automated_Tuning "Automated Tuning") of chess engines using state-of-the-art [Bayesian optimization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayesian_optimization) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
His Bayesian implementation of a sequential model-based optimization, dubbed **Bayes-skopt** [[3]](#cite_note-3) was successfully applied to tune hyperparameters of [Leela Chess Zero](/Leela_Chess_Zero "Leela Chess Zero") and its relatives [[4]](#cite_note-4).
