**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Richard Snyder**

**Richard Snyder**,  
an American computer scientist and AI researcher at times affiliated with the [University of California, Santa Cruz](/University_of_California,_Santa_Cruz "University of California, Santa Cruz") (UCSC). He worked with [Robert Levinson](/Robert_Levinson "Robert Levinson") et al. on [machine learning](/Learning "Learning") and [knowledge representation](/Knowledge "Knowledge"), associative pattern retrieval, and [Morph](/Morph "Morph"), a system that learns to play chess from its experience only [[1]](#cite_note-1).
