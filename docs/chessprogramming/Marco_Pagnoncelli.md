**Marco Pagnoncelli**,  
an Italian chess player and computer chess programmer. He is son of [Gianpietro Pagnoncelli](https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gianpietro_Pagnoncelli), the president of the [Italian Chess Federation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_Chess_Federation) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As chess programmer, Marco Pagnoncelli is author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") compatible chess engine [CyberPagno](/CyberPagno "CyberPagno") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
After a break of several years, he worked on his engine again and released CyberPagno **2.2** in November 2014 [[4]](#cite_note-4).
