**Douglas Bagnall**,  
a New Zealand computer scientist, programmer, technologist and artist [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and author of the [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [p4wn](/P4wn "P4wn") written in [JavaScript](/JavaScript "JavaScript").
Recently, he worked on [author identification](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Author_profiling) using [recurrent neural networks](/Neural_Networks#RNNs "Neural Networks") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
