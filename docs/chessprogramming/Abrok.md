**Abrok**,  
a [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine by [Roman Korba](/Roman_Korba "Roman Korba") [[1]](#cite_note-1),
written in [C](/C "C")/[C++](/Cpp "Cpp"), and first released in June 2002 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
