The **CCT4** was organized by [Volker Richey](/index.php?title=Volker_Richey&action=edit&redlink=1 "Volker Richey (page does not exist)"), played via the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") (ICC) [[1]](#cite_note-1) , January 19-20 and January 26-27, [2002](/Timeline#2002 "Timeline"), 11 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 60min 10sec increment.
