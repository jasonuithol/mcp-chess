The **15th World Microcomputer Chess Championship** took place from October 25 to November 02, [1997](/Timeline#1997 "Timeline"), at [Paris Bourse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris_Bourse) in [Palais Brogniart](http://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palais_Brongniart), [Paris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris), [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France). [Junior](/Junior "Junior") by [Amir Ban](/Amir_Ban "Amir Ban") and [Shay Bushinsky](/Shay_Bushinsky "Shay Bushinsky") won its first title, 1½ ahead of [Virtual Chess](/Virtual_Chess "Virtual Chess"). The closing dinner took place in [Le 58 Tour Eiffel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eiffel_Tower#Restaurants), [Eiffel Tower](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eiffel_Tower).
