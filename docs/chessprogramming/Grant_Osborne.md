**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Grant Osborne**

**Grant Osborne**,  
a British computer chess programmer, and author of the chess program [AtlanChess](/AtlanChess "AtlanChess").
Grant Osborne was involved in finding dense [Magics](/Best_Magics_so_far "Best Magics so far") for [Magic Bitboards](/Magic_Bitboards "Magic Bitboards") [[1]](#cite_note-1),
and proposed [Magic Compression](/Kindergarten_Bitboards#MagicCompression "Kindergarten Bitboards") for [Kindergarten Bitboards](/Kindergarten_Bitboards "Kindergarten Bitboards") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
