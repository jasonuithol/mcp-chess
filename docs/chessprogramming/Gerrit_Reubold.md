**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Gerrit Reubold**

**Gerrit Reubold**,  
a German [lifeguard](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lifeguard) of the [German Life Saving Association DLRG](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/German_Life_Saving_Association) [[1]](#cite_note-1), chess player [[2]](#cite_note-2), and programmer [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
As computer chess programmer, Gerrit Reubold is author of the chess program the [Bringer](/Bringer "Bringer").
