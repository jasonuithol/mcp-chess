**Louis Kessler**,  
a Canadian [genealogist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Genealogy) [[1]](#cite_note-1)
[[2]](#cite_note-2), programmer and as former computer chess programmer author of the chess program [Brute Force](/Brute_Force_(Program) "Brute Force (Program)") which participated at the [8th](/ACM_1977 "ACM 1977") and [9th](/ACM_1978 "ACM 1978") [North American Computer Chess Championships](/ACM_North_American_Computer_Chess_Championship "ACM North American Computer Chess Championship") in 1977 and 1978 respectively.
