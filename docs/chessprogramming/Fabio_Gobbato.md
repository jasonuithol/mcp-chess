Fabio Gobbato [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Fabio Gobbato**,  
an Italian computer chess programmer, and author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [Pedone](/Pedone "Pedone"), which had it tournament debut at the [IGT 2013](/IGT_2013 "IGT 2013"), and already won the [IGT 2014](/IGT_2014 "IGT 2014") with 6/7!
He has further written the didactic open source chess engine [QBBEngine](/QBBEngine "QBBEngine") to demonstrate how to use [quad-bitboards](/Quad-Bitboards "Quad-Bitboards") [[2]](#cite_note-2),
and the tool *ParserCutechess*, which parses the output of [Cutechess-cli](/Cutechess-cli "Cutechess-cli") to create [match statistics](/Match_Statistics "Match Statistics") [[3]](#cite_note-3). In July 2021, Fabio Gobbato announced his chess engine [Revenge](/Revenge "Revenge") as commercial successor of Pedone [[4]](#cite_note-4).
