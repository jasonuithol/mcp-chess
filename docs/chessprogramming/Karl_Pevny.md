Karl Pevny [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Karl Pevny**, (1939 - 2013)   
was an Austrian computer chess programmer and author of the private chess program [Magellan](/Magellan "Magellan"), which played the [WMCCC 1993](/WMCCC_1993 "WMCCC 1993") in [Munich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Munich) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
