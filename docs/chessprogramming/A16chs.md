Alpha16 [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**A16chs**,  
a chess program developed by [Robert Prinsen](/Robert_Prinsen "Robert Prinsen") of *Interscan Data Systems, U.K.* [[2]](#cite_note-2) in seven months [[3]](#cite_note-3). A16chs participated at the [First World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_1974 "WCCC 1974"), 1974 in [Stockholm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockholm), where it ran on an Alpha16 minicomputer.
