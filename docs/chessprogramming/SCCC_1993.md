The **First Spanish Computer Chess Championship** was held on October 09-12, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline") in [Barcelona](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barcelona), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain). It was organized by the *Chessformat Magazine* with support of [ANACA](/ANACA "ANACA"), the Asociación Nacional de Aficionados a las Computadoras de Ajedrez. The competition was a complete success, so much so that it was decided to promote it into an annual event [[2]](#cite_note-2). The tournament was a round robin with seven participants, time control was 60 moves in 2 hours, rest in 30 minutes. [Betsabe](/Betsabe "Betsabe") by [Juan Benitez](/Juan_Benitez "Juan Benitez") won with a perfect 100% score.
