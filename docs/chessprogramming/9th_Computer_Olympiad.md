The **Ninth Computer Olympiad** took place from July 3 to 8, [2004](/Timeline#2004 "Timeline"), at the Psychology Building, [Bar-Ilan University](/Bar-Ilan_University "Bar-Ilan University"), [Ramat Gan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ramat_Gan), [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel), in conjunction with the [12th World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_2004 "WCCC 2004") and the [Computer and Games 2004](/CG_2004 "CG 2004") conference.
