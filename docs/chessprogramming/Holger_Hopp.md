Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Holger_Hopp

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Holger Hopp**

**Holger Hopp**,  
a German computer scientist who defended his diploma thesis in 1995 under [Peter Sanders](/Peter_Sanders "Peter Sanders") on the topic of [parallel search](/Parallel_Search "Parallel Search")
[[1]](#cite_note-1), and his Ph.D. thesis in 2001 on [cache](/Memory#Cache "Memory") behaviour of [optimizing compiler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optimizing_compiler) under [Walter F. Tichy](/Mathematician#WFTichy "Mathematician") [[2]](#cite_note-2), both degrees from [University of Karlsruhe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karlsruhe_Institute_of_Technology).
