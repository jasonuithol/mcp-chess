Adam Hair [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Adam Hair**,  
an American computer chess aficionado, notably working with [Miguel A. Ballicora](/Miguel_A._Ballicora "Miguel A. Ballicora") on testing [Gaviota](/Gaviota "Gaviota"), and member of [CCRL](/CCRL "CCRL"), the Computer Chess Rating Lists.
In January 2012, he published a survey of evidence from both perspectives concerning the [Rybka Controversy](/Rybka_Controversy "Rybka Controversy") and [ICGA Investigations](/ICGA_Investigations "ICGA Investigations"), at times as hosted by [Ed Schröder](/Ed_Schroder "Ed Schroder") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
Adam Hair served as moderator in [CCC](/CCC "CCC").
