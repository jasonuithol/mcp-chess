**Morgan Houppin**,  
a French software developer and student at [42 Computer Training School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/42_(school)) in [Lyon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lyon) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As computer chess programmer, Morgan Houppin is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") [Stash](/Stash "Stash") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
