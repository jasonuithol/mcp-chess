The **8th Aegon Man-Machine** tournament took place from May 5 to 7, and 10 to 12, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline"), at [Aegon Headquarters](/Aegon_Tournaments#headquarters "Aegon Tournaments") in [The Hague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague), [Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands).

## Quadruple pawns

As mentioned by [Marcel van Kervinck](/Marcel_van_Kervinck "Marcel van Kervinck") [[3]](#cite_note-3) , former [Draughts](/Draughts "Draughts") World Champion [Jannes van der Wal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jannes_van_der_Wal) arranged to get the seldom case of a [Quadruple pawn](/Double_and_Triple_(Bitboards)#Multiple "Double and Triple (Bitboards)") versus [HiTech](/HiTech "HiTech"), the machine by former [Correspondence chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Correspondence_chess) World Champion [Hans Berliner](/Hans_Berliner "Hans Berliner") performing [B\*](/B* "B*"):

```
[Event "8th AEGON man-mach"]
[Site "The Hague NED"]
[Date "1993.05.07"]
[Round "03"]
[White "Jannes van der Wal"]
[Black "B*HiTech"]
[Result "0-1"]

1.d4 d5 2.c3 Nf6 3.Bg5 Ne4 4.Bh4 g5 5.Bg3 h5 6.e3 h4 7.Be5 f6 8.f3 fxe5
9.fxe4 dxe4 10.Be2 Bg7 11.Bh5+ Kf8 12.d5 Nd7 13.Bg4 Nc5 14.Be2 e6
15.d6 cxd6 16.Nh3 d5 17.O-O+ Kg8 18.c4 Qb6 19.Bh5 Rh6 20.Qg4 Qxb2
21.Qxg5 Bd7 22.Nd2 Kh7 23.Nb3 Nxb3 24.axb3 Bf6 25.Rxf6 Rxf6
26.Qxf6 Qxa1+ 27.Kf2 Qb2+ 28.Be2 Kg8 29.Qg6+ Kf8 30.Qf6+ Kg8
31.Qg6+ Kf8 32.Qh7 d4 33.Ng5 dxe3+ 34.Kg1 Ke8 35.Qg8+ Ke7 36.Qf7+ 0-1
```
