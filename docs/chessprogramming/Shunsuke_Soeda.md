**Shunsuke Soeda**,  
a Japanese computer scientist affiliated with the Computing System Research Group, [University of Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Tokyo). He is co-author of the open source [Shogi](/Shogi "Shogi") program [GPS Shogi](/index.php?title=GPS_Shogi&action=edit&redlink=1 "GPS Shogi (page does not exist)") [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), available under [GPL](/Free_Software_Foundation#GPL "Free Software Foundation") version 2 or later. In April 2013, GPS Shogi, running on a computer cluster of 700 PCs in the University of Tokyo, [beat](/Shogi#GPSShogi "Shogi") Hiroyuki Miura, one of the Top-10 professional Shogi players [[4]](#cite_note-4) [[5]](#cite_note-5) [[6]](#cite_note-6).
