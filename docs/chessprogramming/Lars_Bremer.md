# Lars Bremer

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Lars_Bremer

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Lars Bremer**

**Lars Bremer**,  
a German software developer, journalist and writer with affinity to computer [Games](/Games "Games") and computer chess. Lars was at times editor-in-chief for the German online magazine [Computerschach und Spiele](/Computerschach_und_Spiele "Computerschach und Spiele"),
wrote for the German computer magazine [c't](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C%27t) [[1]](#cite_note-1)
and for [Walter Eigenmann's](/index.php?title=Walter_Eigenmann&action=edit&redlink=1 "Walter Eigenmann (page does not exist)") online magazine *Glarean* [[2]](#cite_note-2).
On his own German site [[3]](#cite_note-3), one finds articles from *Computerschach und Spiele* and some interesting games like Lars' own [Connect Four](/Connect_Four "Connect Four") program *Mustrum* [[4]](#cite_note-4),
as well as [Chrilly's](/Chrilly_Donninger "Chrilly Donninger") Corner [[5]](#cite_note-5).
