**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Tony Hedlund**

[![](/images/5/50/TonyHedlund.jpg)](https://www.iccf.com/player?id=450983)

Tony Hedlund [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Tony Hedlund**, (died early November 2019 [[2]](#cite_note-2))  
was a Swedish [correspondence chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Correspondence_chess) player, [International Correspondence Chess Grandmaster](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Correspondence_Chess_Grandmaster),
computer chess expert, long term tester and administrator of the [SSDF](/SSDF "SSDF") database of test games [[3]](#cite_note-3),
and author of many articles and game annotations in the Swedish computer chess magazine [PLY](/PLY_(Magazine) "PLY (Magazine)") and subsequently, in his [Computer Chess Newsletter](#CCN).
In 2003, Tony Hedlund served as moderator in [CCC](/CCC "CCC") [[4]](#cite_note-4) .
