The **First United States Open Computer Chess Championship** was held in [Mobile, Alabama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile,_Alabama), June 21-23, [1985](/Timeline#1985 "Timeline"). The rules allowed open hardware and multiple "team" entries.

# Team Order

The tournament was structured in such a way that manufacturers had to play their own machines against each other in some rounds. The rules clearly stated that the operators of each computer had the right to resign or offer a draw on behalf of their machines. Apparently the temptation to take advantage of the rules was too much for [Fidelity](/Fidelity_Electronics "Fidelity Electronics") to overcome [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
