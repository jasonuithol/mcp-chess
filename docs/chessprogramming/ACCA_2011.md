The **2011 Sixth Annual ACCA Americas' Computer Chess Championships** was played November 12 & 13, [2011](/Timeline#2011 "Timeline") on [HGM's chess server](/index.php?title=HGM%27s_Server&action=edit&redlink=1 "HGM's Server (page does not exist)"), 6 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 45min 10sec increment.
