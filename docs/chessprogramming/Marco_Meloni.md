Marco Meloni [[1]](#cite_note-LinkedIn-1)

**Marco Meloni**,  
an Italian chemical engineer, M.Sc. from [Sapienza University of Rome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sapienza_University_of_Rome) in 2009 [[1]](#cite_note-LinkedIn-1), and occasional freelancer for a publishing company,
creating articles, tutorial translations and IT projects in the [Linux](/Linux "Linux") and [Windows](/Windows "Windows") fields [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [Naraku](/Naraku "Naraku"), but stopped working on the engine after clone suspicions appeared,
admitting taking ideas from [Fruit](/Fruit "Fruit"), [Crafty](/Crafty "Crafty"), [Ippolit](/Ippolit "Ippolit") (bitboard infrastructure) and [Stockfish](/Stockfish "Stockfish") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
