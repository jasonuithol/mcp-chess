Hyatt Regency Indianapolis [[1]](#cite_note-1)

[ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **23rd North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) was held February 14-17, [1993](/Timeline#1993 "Timeline"), [Hyatt Regency Indianapolis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyatt_Regency_Indianapolis), [Indianapolis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indianapolis%2C_Indiana), [Indiana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indiana), USA. Throughout the tournament, [Robert Levinson](/Robert_Levinson "Robert Levinson") demonstrated his learning program [Morph](/Morph "Morph") [[2]](#cite_note-2). [Tony Marsland](/Tony_Marsland "Tony Marsland") served as moderator of a panel discussion entitled *Computer Chess: What Remains?* with [Robert Hyatt](/Robert_Hyatt "Robert Hyatt"), Robert Levinson, [Monroe Newborn](/Monroe_Newborn "Monroe Newborn") and [John Stanback](/John_Stanback "John Stanback") as members [[3]](#cite_note-3).
