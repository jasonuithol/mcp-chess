The **Tenth Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship**, July 16/17, [2016](/Timeline#2016 "Timeline") [[1]](#cite_note-1) . The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4), played on-line over the internet ([ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)")).
