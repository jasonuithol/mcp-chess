**Ken Cohen**,  
an American technologist, entrepreneur, businessman and manufacturer of [consumer electronics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Consumer_electronics) and [dedicated chess computers](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers"). In 1979 along with [Eric White](/Eric_White "Eric White") co-founder of the [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong) manufacturer *Cassia* (1979-1981) [[1]](#cite_note-1), followed in 1981 *White and Allcock* (1981-1984) [[2]](#cite_note-2), and in 1984, [Newcrest Technology](/Newcrest_Technology "Newcrest Technology") (1984-1991) aka *CXG Systems* .
