**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2005 "DOCCC 2005") [Next >](/DOCCC_2007 "DOCCC 2007")**

The **26th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place November 3 to 5, [2006](/Timeline#2006 "Timeline"), at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was a nine round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 90 minutes per player for the whole game.
