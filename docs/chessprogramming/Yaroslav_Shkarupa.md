**Yaroslav Shkarupa**,  
an Ukrainian computer scientist with a B.Sc. degree from [National University of Kyiv-Mohyla Academy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_University_of_Kyiv-Mohyla_Academy) in 2017 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
As exchange student at the [University of Groningen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Groningen), he worked on chess - the project work along with [Zacharias Georgiou](/Zacharias_Georgiou "Zacharias Georgiou"), [Evangelos Karountzos](/Evangelos_Karountzos "Evangelos Karountzos") and [Matthia Sabatelli](/Matthia_Sabatelli "Matthia Sabatelli") dealt with [reinforcement learning](/Reinforcement_Learning "Reinforcement Learning") in simple [chess endgames](/Endgame "Endgame") such as [KRK](/KRK "KRK") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
