**Aleksey (Alexey, Alex) Morozov**,  
an Ukrainian chess programmer and author of the chess program [Booot](/Booot "Booot"). Alex reported on the [1st Computer Chess Championship of CIS Countries](/CCCCISC_2008 "CCCCISC 2008") 2008 in Moscow [[2]](#cite_note-2), and is recently quite active in playing over the board as well as online tournaments with Booot.
