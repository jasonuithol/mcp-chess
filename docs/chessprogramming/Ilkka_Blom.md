**Ilkka Blom**,  
a Finnish [chess problem composer](/Category:Chess_Composer "Category:Chess Composer") and author of the commercial chess solving programs [Alybadix](/Alybadix "Alybadix") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and the [Excel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Excel) worksheet for solving 9 x 9 [Sudoku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudoku) grids, *Aly-Sudoku*. Alybadix is one of the oldest software (1980) programs for chess problems, compatible with the free [Windows](/Windows "Windows") [GUI](/GUI "GUI") [APwin](/APwin "APwin") by [Paul Wiereyn](/Paul_Wiereyn "Paul Wiereyn") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
