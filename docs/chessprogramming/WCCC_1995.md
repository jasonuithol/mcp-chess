The **Eighth World Computer Chess Championship** took place from May 25-30, [1995](/Timeline#1995 "Timeline"), Ho Sin Hang Engineering Building at the [Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_University_of_Hong_Kong), [Shatin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sha_Tin), [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong) - [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China).

[Fritz](/Fritz "Fritz") on a [90 MHz P5](/X86 "X86") won the title after a play-off versus [Star Socrates](/Star_Socrates "Star Socrates"), after already winning again [Deep Blue Prototype](/Deep_Blue "Deep Blue") in round five.
