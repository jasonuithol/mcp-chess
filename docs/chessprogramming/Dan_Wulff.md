Dan Wulff [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Dan Wulff Kristensen**,  
a Danish computer chess expert, [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author"), tester, operator and along with primary programmer [Steen Suurballe](/Steen_Suurballe "Steen Suurballe") member of the [Gandalf](/Gandalf "Gandalf") team. Dan operated Gandalf almost at all [IPCCCs](/IPCCC "IPCCC") from 1994 until 2007.
