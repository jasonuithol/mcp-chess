Lars Hjörth, [WMCCC 1984](/WMCCC_1984 "WMCCC 1984") [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Lars Hjörth**,  
a Swedish former professional computer chess programmer and along with [Ulf Rathsman](/Ulf_Rathsman "Ulf Rathsman") co-author of the chess programs [Plymate](/Plymate "Plymate") and [Y!](/Y! "Y!") (Why Not 88 and 89). At the [WMCCC 1984](/WMCCC_1984 "WMCCC 1984"), where Rathsman's program [Princhess](/Princhess "Princhess") became tied first, Lars Hjörth, affiliated with [Johan Enroth's](/Johan_Enroth "Johan Enroth") *Systemhuset* aka *System Integrierung* company [[2]](#cite_note-2), was already working with Rathsman on the [Conchess](/Conchess "Conchess") program, and operated the dedicated machines [[3]](#cite_note-3).
