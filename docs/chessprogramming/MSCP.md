**MSCP**,  
**M**arcel's **S**imple **C**hess **P**rogram by [Marcel van Kervinck](/Marcel_van_Kervinck "Marcel van Kervinck") [[1]](#cite_note-1), a small, simple, yet complete [open source chess engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") released under the [GNU GPL](/Free_Software_Foundation#GPL "Free Software Foundation"). It works with both, the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and [icsdrone](/index.php?title=Icsdrone&action=edit&redlink=1 "Icsdrone (page does not exist)").

# MSCP-PAR

As subject of his Bachelor thesis, *Parallelizing a Simple Chess Program*, [Brian Greskamp](/index.php?title=Brian_Greskamp&action=edit&redlink=1 "Brian Greskamp (page does not exist)") implemented a parallel version of MSCP dubbed **MSCP-PAR**, using [principal variation splitting](/Parallel_Search#PrincipalVariationSplitting "Parallel Search") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
