The **I.G.T. 2016** (International [Gsei](/G_6 "G 6") Tournament) was planned to be held in *Norcia Ospitalia Il Capisterium*, [Norcia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Norcia), [Perugia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Province_of_Perugia), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy) on September 10-11, [2016](/Timeline#2016 "Timeline"). Due to the [earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/August_2016_Central_Italy_earthquake) which struck Norcia on August 24, 2016 [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4), the IGT was held at *Accademia Scacchistica Romana* [[5]](#cite_note-5) , [Rome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rome) [[6]](#cite_note-6) .
