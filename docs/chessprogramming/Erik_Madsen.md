Erik Madsen [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Erik Madsen**,  
an American professional programmer and application development supervisor specializing in [SharePoint](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_SharePoint), [workflow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Workflow), and [service oriented architecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Service-oriented_architecture).
As an advocate of [Visual Studio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Visual_Studio) and [C#](/C_sharp "C sharp") [.NET](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/.NET_Framework) as well as amateur chess player, Erik is author of [RumbleMinze](/RumbleMinze "RumbleMinze") and its successor [MadChess](/MadChess "MadChess") [[2]](#cite_note-2), both written in C# and conform to the [Universal Chess Interface](/UCI "UCI").
