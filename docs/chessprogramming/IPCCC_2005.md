The Fourteenth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**14th IPCCC**) took place February 23-27, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
