**MAX**,  
an early chess program from the late 60s written by [James Gillogly](/James_Gillogly "James Gillogly") in [FORTRAN IV](/Fortran "Fortran") that uses [alpha-beta](/Alpha-Beta "Alpha-Beta") look-ahead search of [selected portions](/Type_B_Strategy "Type B Strategy") of the [move tree](/Search_Tree "Search Tree"). The program has played several complete games and many incomplete ones between frequent periods of major revisions [[2]](#cite_note-2).
