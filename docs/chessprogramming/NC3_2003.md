The **2003 Australasian National Computer Chess Championship** (NC3) was held on July 23, [2003](/Timeline#2003 "Timeline"), as part of the [Australian National University](/Australian_National_University "Australian National University") Chess Festival [[1]](#cite_note-1), [Red Hill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Hill,_Australian_Capital_Territory), [Canberra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canberra), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia). Free chess server hosted by [Andrew Tridgell](/Andrew_Tridgell "Andrew Tridgell"), organized by [Shaun Press](/Shaun_Press "Shaun Press").
