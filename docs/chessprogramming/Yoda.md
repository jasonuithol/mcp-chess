Master Yoda [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Yoda**,  
a [winboard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") engine by [Daniel Anulliero](/Daniel_Anulliero "Daniel Anulliero"), written in [C](/C "C"), announced in June 2008 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
After he made first [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") experience with his engine [JARS](/index.php?title=JARS&action=edit&redlink=1 "JARS (page does not exist)"), Yoda was Daniel Anulliero's second attempt, where the final version 2.09 introduced an [hash table](/Transposition_Table "Transposition Table") bug [[3]](#cite_note-3).
Yoda 1.30 and Yoda 2.09 (bug-fixed) are available from [Ron Murawski's](/Ron_Murawski "Ron Murawski") download site [[4]](#cite_note-4).
