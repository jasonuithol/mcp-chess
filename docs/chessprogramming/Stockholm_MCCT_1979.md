The **First Stockholm Micro Computer Chess Tournament** was held in December [1979](/Timeline#1979 "Timeline"), [Stockholm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockholm), Sweden, organized by the Swedish [ICCA](/ICCA "ICCA"), forerunner of the [SSDF](/SSDF "SSDF").
