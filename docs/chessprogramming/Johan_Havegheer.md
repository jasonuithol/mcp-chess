# Johan Havegheer

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Johan_Havegheer

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Johan Havegheer**

**Johan Havegheer**,  
a Belgian computer scientist [[1]](#cite_note-1), computer chess aficionado and journalist, early [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") promoter, tester and operator of various programs, notably [Ant](/Ant "Ant") [[2]](#cite_note-2) and [Ruffian](/Ruffian "Ruffian") [[3]](#cite_note-3). He wrote articles and reports for [Computerschaak](/Computerschaak "Computerschaak") [[4]](#cite_note-4), was member of the [CEGT](/CEGT "CEGT") rating list team around [Heinz van Kempen](/Heinz_van_Kempen "Heinz van Kempen") [[5]](#cite_note-5), and helped in translating [Arena](/Arena "Arena").
