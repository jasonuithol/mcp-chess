Power Chess cover art [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Power Chess**,  
a commercial mass market chess program running under [Windows 95](/Windows "Windows") released in 1996 by [Sierra](/Sierra "Sierra"), incorporating [WChess](/WChess "WChess") by [David Kittinger](/David_Kittinger "David Kittinger") as chess engine [[2]](#cite_note-2). Later revisions of the software were released as *Power Chess '98* and *Power Chess 2.0* [[3]](#cite_note-3).
