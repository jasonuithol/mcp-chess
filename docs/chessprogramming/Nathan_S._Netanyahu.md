**Nathan S. Netanyahu**,  
an Israeli computer scientist and professor at the Department of Computer Science [[2]](#cite_note-2) [Bar-Ilan University](/Bar-Ilan_University "Bar-Ilan University") [[3]](#cite_note-3), [Ramat Gan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ramat_Gan), [Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel). Nathan S. Netanyahu was local organizer of the [12th World Computer-Chess Championship 2004](/WCCC_2004 "WCCC 2004"), the [9th Computer Olympiad](/9th_Computer_Olympiad "9th Computer Olympiad") and [4th International Conference on Computers and Games](/CG_2004 "CG 2004") at the Bar-Ilan University in Rama Gan, assisted by his Ph.D Student [Eli David](/Eli_David "Eli David") [[4]](#cite_note-4)[[5]](#cite_note-5).
