**AtlanChess**,  
a [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") compliant chess engine by [Grant Osborne](/Grant_Osborne "Grant Osborne").
The 32-bit [Windows](/Windows "Windows") version 3.3 from 2005 is available from [Ron Murawski's](/Ron_Murawski "Ron Murawski") download site [[2]](#cite_note-2).
AtlanChess 5.52 played [Olivier Deville's](/Olivier_Deville "Olivier Deville") [ChessWar](/ChessWar "ChessWar") XVII E in 2010/11 [[3]](#cite_note-3) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
