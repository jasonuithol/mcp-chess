The eleventh International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**11th IPCCC**) took place February 27 - March 3, [2002](/Timeline#2002 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
