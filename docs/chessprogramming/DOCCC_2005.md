Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2005

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2005**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2004 "DOCCC 2004") [Next >](/DOCCC_2006 "DOCCC 2006")**

The **25th Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place November 11 to 13, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline"), at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was a nine round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament).
