The **CCT11** was organized by [Peter Skinner](/Peter_Skinner "Peter Skinner"), played via the [Internet Chess Club](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") (ICC) [[1]](#cite_note-1) , March 21-22, [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline"), 9 rounds [Swiss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament), 50min 3sec increment. After Rybka's disqualification in 2011, [Fruit](/Fruit "Fruit") by [Fabien Letouzey](/Fabien_Letouzey "Fabien Letouzey") and [Bright](/Bright "Bright") by [Allard Siemelink](/Allard_Siemelink "Allard Siemelink") are co-champions [[2]](#cite_note-2).
