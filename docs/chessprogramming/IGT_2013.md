The **I.G.T. 2013** (International Gsei Tournament) organized by the [G 6](/G_6 "G 6") was held in [Omegna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Omegna), [Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola (VB)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Province_of_Verbano-Cusio-Ossola), [Piedmont](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Piedmont), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy), on the side of [Lago d’Orta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Orta), November 09-10, [2013](/Timeline#2013 "Timeline"). Only five original programs competed, hoping to attract more programmers the next editions.
