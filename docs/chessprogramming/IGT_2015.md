The **I.G.T. 2015** (International [Gsei](/G_6 "G 6") Tournament) was held in [Como](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Como), [Lombardy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lombardy), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy), September 26-27, [2015](/Timeline#2015 "Timeline"). The tournament was a seven round Swiss with a timecontrol of 30 minutes and 10 seconds increment.
