**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Shawn Benn**

**Shawn Benn**,  
an American computer scientist, software and database engineer, from 1989 until 1996 affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, [University of Maine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Maine), and since then, holding various positions in the industry [[1]](#cite_note-1). In 1992, along with [Danny Kopec](/Danny_Kopec "Danny Kopec"), he recalibrated the [Bratko-Kopec Test](/Bratko-Kopec_Test "Bratko-Kopec Test") using six commercial chess programs,
[Chessmaster 3000](/Chessmaster#3000 "Chessmaster"), [Excel 68000](/Excel "Excel"), [MChess](/MChess "MChess"), [Sargon IV](/Sargon "Sargon"), [Sargon V](/Sargon "Sargon") and [Zarkov 2.5](/Zarkov "Zarkov") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
