**[< Prev](/DOCCC_1998 "DOCCC 1998") [Next >](/DOCCC_2000 "DOCCC 2000")**

The **19th** Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, October 30-31 and November 6-7, [1999](/Timeline#1999 "Timeline").
