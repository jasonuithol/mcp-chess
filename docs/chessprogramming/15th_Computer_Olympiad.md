Shiinoki Cultural Complex [[1]](#cite_note-1)

The **Fifteenth Computer Olympiad** (JAIST Computer Olympiad) took place from September 24 to October 1, [2010](/Timeline#2010 "Timeline"), at Shiinoki Cultural Complex [[2]](#cite_note-2), [Kanazawa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa,_Ishikawa), [Japan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan), in conjunction with the [18th World Computer Chess Championship](/WCCC_2010 "WCCC 2010"), the [First World Chess Software Championship](/WCSC_2010 "WCSC 2010"), and the [7th Computer and Games](/CG_2010 "CG 2010") conference. All events were hosted by [Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST)](/JAIST "JAIST") and organized by the [ICGA](/ICGA "ICGA") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
