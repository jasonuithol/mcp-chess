The [ACM's](/ACM "ACM") **Seventh North American Computer Chess Championship** (NACCC) was held on October 19 to 21, [1976](/Timeline#1976 "Timeline") at the [Hyatt Regency Hotel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyatt_Regency_Houston) [Houston](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Houston%2C_Texas), [Texas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas), USA [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
