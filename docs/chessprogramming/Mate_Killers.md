# Mate Killers

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Mate_Killers

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Chess](/Chess "Chess") \* [Moves](/Moves "Moves") \* [Killer Move](/Killer_Move "Killer Move") \* Mate Killers**

Some programs, most notably [Rebel](/Rebel "Rebel") [[1]](#cite_note-1), distinguish between the ordinary [killer moves](/Killer_Move "Killer Move") and the **mate killers** - causing a [beta-cutoff](/Beta-Cutoff "Beta-Cutoff") with a value indicating that a player is going to deliver [checkmate](/Checkmate "Checkmate"). They are sorted higher than ordinary killer moves.
