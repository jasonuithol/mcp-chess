Tamerlane [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Tamerlane**,  
a [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") compliant chess engine by [Luigi Marco Ripamonti](/Luigi_Marco_Ripamonti "Luigi Marco Ripamonti"), written in [C++](/Cpp "Cpp").
Its development started in 2000 and Tamerlane was first released in April 2001 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
It was the author's first attempt to write "something" capable of playing chess [[3]](#cite_note-3), soon followed by [Madeleine](/Madeleine "Madeleine") in 2002 [[4]](#cite_note-4) .
