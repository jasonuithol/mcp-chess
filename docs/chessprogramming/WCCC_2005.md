The **Thirteenth World Computer Chess Championship** took place from August 13 to 21, [2005](/Timeline#2005 "Timeline"), at [Reykjavík University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reykjav%C3%ADk_University), [Reykjavík](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reykjav%C3%ADk), [Iceland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iceland). Twelve participants competed in a eleven round robin tournament. [Zappa](/Zappa "Zappa") by [Anthony Cozzie](/Anthony_Cozzie "Anthony Cozzie") was the sole winner with 10½ out of 11, followed by runner up [Fruit](/Fruit "Fruit") by [Fabien Letouzey](/Fabien_Letouzey "Fabien Letouzey"). [Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder") and [Deep Sjeng](/Deep_Sjeng "Deep Sjeng") shared the third place.
