**Peter Aloysius Harjanto**, (Peter Alloysius)  
an Indonesian computer scientist, IT professional, chess player and as computer chess programmer author of the free [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") chess engine [Petir](/Petir "Petir") [[2]](#cite_note-2) . While student at [STTS](http://id.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sekolah_Tinggi_Teknik_Surabaya) from 2000 until 2004, he represented STTS to win several chess championships in [Surabaya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Surabaya) [[3]](#cite_note-3).
