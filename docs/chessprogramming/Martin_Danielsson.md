Martin Danielsson [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Martin Danielsson**,  
a Swedish electrical engineer, M.Sc. from [Lund University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lund_University) in 1999 [[2]](#cite_note-2), and as computer chess programmer author of the [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") and [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [Marvin](/Marvin "Marvin"), originally developed in the early 2000s until 2005,
first released in May 2004 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
After ten years "out of business", Martin Danielsson restarted the development in 2015 - the completely rewritten Marvin **2.0.0** was released as [open source engine](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") under the terms of the [GNU General Public License](/Free_Software_Foundation#GPL "Free Software Foundation") (GPL3) in March 2017 [[4]](#cite_note-4).
