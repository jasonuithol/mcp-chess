**TCEC Season 7**,  
a private tournament directed and organized by [Martin Thoresen](/Martin_Thoresen "Martin Thoresen") and supported by the [Chessdom Arena](/index.php?title=Chessdom&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chessdom (page does not exist)"), started on September 19, [2014](/Timeline#2014 "Timeline"). There are 4 Stages plus the Superfinal [[2]](#cite_note-2).
All [book openings](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") until Stage 4 were selected by [Nelson Hernandez](/Nelson_Hernandez "Nelson Hernandez") alias *Cato the Younger* and [Adam Hair](/Adam_Hair "Adam Hair"), except in Stage 2 which will be run without books.
The 32 openings of the [Superfinal](#Superfinal) were selected by IM [Erik Kislik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erik_Kislik). [Komodo 1333](/Komodo "Komodo") won the Superfinal versus [Stockfish 141214](/Stockfish "Stockfish") with **33½-30½**.
