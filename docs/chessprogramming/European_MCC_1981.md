Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/European_MCC_1981

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [European Microcomputer Chess Championship](/European_Microcomputer_Chess_Championship "European Microcomputer Chess Championship") \* 2nd European MCC 1981**

**[< Prev](/WMCCC_1980 "WMCCC 1980") [Next >](/European_MCC_1982 "European MCC 1982")**

The inofficial **2nd European Microcomputer Chess Championship** took place September [1981](/Timeline#1981 "Timeline") in [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom) during the [Personal Computer World Show](/Personal_Computer_World "Personal Computer World"), also known as the 4th [PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship](/PCW_Microcomputer_Chess_Championship "PCW Microcomputer Chess Championship").
