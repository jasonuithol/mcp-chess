**Thomas Kolarik**,  
an American graduated physicist from [University of Maryland, Baltimore County](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Maryland,_Baltimore_County), system analyst, part time [tennis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tennis) instructor [[3]](#cite_note-3) , and author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine [Nirvanachess](/Nirvanachess "Nirvanachess") [[4]](#cite_note-4) . With some interests in playing chess, he started Nirvanachess as a side project in his Freshman year of college in about 2010, with the motivation to learn [C](/C "C")/[C++](/Cpp "Cpp") and to start a project that would keep him occupied for a while.
