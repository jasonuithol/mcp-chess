[![](/images/7/73/Stefanogemma.jpg)](https://www.g-sei.org/partite-8/#!gallery-sc1_2-8)

Stefano Gemma at [CIPS 2001](/CIPS_2001 "CIPS 2001") [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Stefano Gemma**,  
an Italian IT consultant, owner of *L'Informatica di Gemma Stefano* [[2]](#cite_note-2), and as computer chess programmer,
founding member of the [Gruppo Scacchi e Informatica](/G_6 "G 6"), the Italian Computer Chess Association, and author of the chess programs [Drago](/Drago "Drago"), [Raffaela](/Raffaela "Raffaela") [[3]](#cite_note-3),
[Freccia](/Freccia "Freccia"), [Satana](/Satana "Satana") [[4]](#cite_note-4),
and [Sabrina](/Sabrina "Sabrina") [[5]](#cite_note-5).
