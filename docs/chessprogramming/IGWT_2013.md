Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/IGWT_2013

**[Next >](/IGWT_2014 "IGWT 2014")**

The **I.G.W.T.** (International Gsei Web Tournament) is an international competition between chess engines relayed on [gsei site](/G_6 "G 6") [[1]](#cite_note-1) . In the first [2013](/Timeline#2013 "Timeline") edition, the best 8 Italian engines, selected through a series of double round robin, versus 8 non Italian engines, selected through a one double round robin (10 participants). After that, this 16 engines played a [knockout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single-elimination_tournament), the final step, with [Chiron](/Chiron "Chiron") as winner [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
