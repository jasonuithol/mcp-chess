**The first Chess Programmers Tournament**,  
was initiated and organized in June [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"), by [Richard Pijl](/Richard_Pijl "Richard Pijl"), author of the [engines](/Engines "Engines") [The Baron](/The_Baron "The Baron") and [Crashtest Dummy](/Crashtest_Dummy "Crashtest Dummy") (CTD). It took place in [De Bouwhoeve](http://www.debouwhoeve.be/home.htm), [Hoogstraten-Wortel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoogstraten), [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium). Nine participants, six round swiss, time-control was 90 minutes per side and game.
