James Quon [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**James (Jim, Jimmy) Quon**, (1968 - June 20, 2010 [[2]](#cite_note-2))  
was an American computer scientist, chess master and professional chess coach. As chess advisor, he was involved in the research and publication on evolutionary computer chess concerning the chess program [Blondie25](/Blondie25 "Blondie25") [[3]](#cite_note-3).
