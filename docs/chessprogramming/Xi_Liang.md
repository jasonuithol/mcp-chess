**Xi Liang** (梁熙),  
a Taiwanese computer scientist affiliated with the Department of Computer Science at [National Chiao Tung University](/National_Chiao_Tung_University "National Chiao Tung University"), [Hsinchu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hsinchu). In 2015, he defended his master's degree in CS on the topic of solving [Hex](/Hex "Hex") openings using Job-level [UCT](/UCT "UCT") (JL-UCT) [[1]](#cite_note-1), further published along with [Ting-Han Wei](/Ting-Han_Wei "Ting-Han Wei") and [I-Chen Wu](/I-Chen_Wu "I-Chen Wu") in the [ICGA Journal](/ICGA_Journal#38_3 "ICGA Journal") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
