**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Luigi Marco Ripamonti**

**Luigi Marco Ripamonti**,  
an Italian software engineer holding a [Laurea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laurea) degree in computer science from [Polytechnic University of Milan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polytechnic_University_of_Milan) in 1999 [[1]](#cite_note-1).
As computer chess programmer, he is author of the chess engines [Tamerlane](/Tamerlane "Tamerlane"), [Madeleine](/Madeleine "Madeleine") and [Shield](/Shield "Shield") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
