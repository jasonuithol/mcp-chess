# Stephen Chen

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Stephen_Chen

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Stephen Chen**

**Stephen Chen**, [[1]](#cite_note-1)  
a [Hong Kongese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_people) early microcomputer chess programmer, in the late 70s and early 80s affiliated with [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong) manufacturer *Conic* [[2]](#cite_note-2) as author of their [dedicated chess computer](/Dedicated_Chess_Computers "Dedicated Chess Computers") programs. On the [ICGA](/ICGA "ICGA") tournament site, he is mentioned as author of [Conic Experimental](/Conic_X "Conic X") which played the [WMCCC 1981](/WMCCC_1981 "WMCCC 1981") in [Travemünde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travem%C3%BCnde), apparently the same program as the commercial *Conic 7013* endorsed by [Victor Korchnoi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viktor_Korchnoi) soon released after the WMCCC [[3]](#cite_note-3).
