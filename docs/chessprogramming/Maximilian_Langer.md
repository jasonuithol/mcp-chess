**Maximilian Langer**,  
a German computer scientist and student at [TU Darmstadt](/Darmstadt_University_of_Technology "Darmstadt University of Technology").
In his 2021 B.Sc. thesis, he elaborates on [supervised](/Supervised_Learning "Supervised Learning") [deep learning](/Deep_Learning "Deep Learning") methods along with [Monte-Carlo Tree Search](/Monte-Carlo_Tree_Search "Monte-Carlo Tree Search"), and gives a detailed description of the [chess variant](/Chess#Variants "Chess") engine [CrazyAra](/CrazyAra "CrazyAra") by co-advisor and primary author [Johannes Czech](/Johannes_Czech "Johannes Czech"), and its instrumentalization to play [Chinese Chess](/Chinese_Chess "Chinese Chess") (Xiangqi) [[1]](#cite_note-1).
