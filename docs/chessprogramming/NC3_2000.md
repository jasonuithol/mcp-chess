The **2000 Australasian National Computer Chess Championship** (NC3) was held from July 15 to 16, [2000](/Timeline#2000 "Timeline"), as part of the [Australian National University](/Australian_National_University "Australian National University") Chess Festival [[1]](#cite_note-1) , [Red Hill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Hill,_Australian_Capital_Territory), [Canberra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canberra), [Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia). Free chess server hosted by [Andrew Tridgell](/Andrew_Tridgell "Andrew Tridgell"), organized by [Shaun Press](/Shaun_Press "Shaun Press").
