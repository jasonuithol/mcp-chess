**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship](/ACCA_World_Computer_Rapid_Chess_Championship "ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship") \* WCRCC 2008**

**[< Prev](/WCRCC_2007 "WCRCC 2007") [Next >](/WCRCC_2009 "WCRCC 2009")**

The **Second Annual ACCA World Computer Rapid Chess Championship** took place June 21-22, [2008](/Timeline#2008 "Timeline"). The format was [Rapid Chess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_chess) (G/25+4), played on-line over the internet ([ICC](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)")).
