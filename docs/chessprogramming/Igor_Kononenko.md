Igor Kononenko [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Igor Kononenko**,  
a Slovenian computer scientist and professor at Faculty of Computer and Information Science as well as head of the Laboratory for Cognitive Modeling [[2]](#cite_note-2) at [University of Ljubljana](/University_of_Ljubljana "University of Ljubljana").
His research interests covers [artificial intelligence](/Artificial_Intelligence "Artificial Intelligence"), [machine learning](/Learning "Learning"), [neural networks](/Neural_Networks "Neural Networks") and [cognitive](/Cognition "Cognition") modeling.
