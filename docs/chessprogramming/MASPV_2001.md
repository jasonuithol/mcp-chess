The **Magyar Sakkprogramok Versenye** (MASPV) **2001** was held from July 27 to 29, [2001](/Timeline#2001 "Timeline"), hosted by the [University of Sopron](/University_of_Sopron "University of Sopron"), [Sopron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sopron), [Hungary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary).
