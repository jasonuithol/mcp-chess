# Mridul Muralidharan

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Mridul_Muralidharan

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [People](/People "People") \* Mridul Muralidharan**

**Mridul Muralidharan**,  
an Indian computer scientist, senior research engineer and member of the [Apache Spark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apache_Spark) team at [Hortonworks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hortonworks) [[1]](#cite_note-1), and computer chess programmer.
He holds a [Bachelor of Technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bachelor_of_Technology) from [National Institute of Technology Calicut](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Institute_of_Technology_Calicut) in 2001.
