**Arturo Ochoa M.**,  
a Venezuelan computer chess expert, early [WinBoard](/WinBoard "WinBoard") promoter [[1]](#cite_note-1), and [opening book author](/Category:Opening_Book_Author "Category:Opening Book Author"), who cooperated with many chess programmers and cooked [opening books](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") for their engines as used in tournaments or released with the engine.
