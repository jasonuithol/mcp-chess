**TCEC Season 11**,  
the Top Chess Engine Championship (TCEC) Season 11 started on January 03, [2018](/Timeline#2018 "Timeline").
From this Season 11 TCEC is upgrading its format to a league system with promotions and relegations [[2]](#cite_note-2) .
The [Superfinal](#Superfinal) between [Stockfish 260318](/Stockfish "Stockfish") and [Houdini 6.03](/Houdini "Houdini") was won by Stockfish with **59-41**.
