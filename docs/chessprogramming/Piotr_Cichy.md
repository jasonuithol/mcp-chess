**Piotr Cichy**,  
a Polish software developer, computer chess programmer, and author of two very small chess programs: [nanoSzachy](/NanoSzachy "NanoSzachy") [[1]](#cite_note-1) and **pikoSzachy**
[[2]](#cite_note-2).
nanoSzachy won the [8th PCCC 2009](/PCCC_2009 "PCCC 2009").
