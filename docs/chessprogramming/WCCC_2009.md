The **Seventeenth World Computer Chess Championship** took place from May 10 to 18, [2009](/Timeline#2009 "Timeline"), at Palacio del Condestable, [Pamplona](https://en.wikipedia/wiki/Pamplona), [Navarre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Navarre), [Spain](https://en.wikipedia/wiki/Spain), in conjunction with the [14th Computer Olympiad](/14th_Computer_Olympiad "14th Computer Olympiad"), also covering [chess](/14th_Computer_Olympiad#Chess "14th Computer Olympiad"), and the [Advances in Computer Games 12](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_12 "Advances in Computer Games 12") conference.

After the [disqualification](/World_Computer_Chess_Championship#RybkaDisqualification "World Computer Chess Championship") of [Rybka](/Rybka "Rybka") in June [2011](/Timeline#2011 "Timeline"), [Junior](/Junior "Junior"), [Shredder](/Shredder "Shredder") and [Deep Sjeng](/Deep_Sjeng "Deep Sjeng") are World Computer Chess Champions 2009
