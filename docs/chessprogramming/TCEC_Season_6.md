**TCEC Season 6**,  
a private tournament directed and organized by [Martin Thoresen](/Martin_Thoresen "Martin Thoresen") and supported by the [Chessdom Arena](/index.php?title=Chessdom&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chessdom (page does not exist)"), started on February 07, [2014](/Timeline#2014 "Timeline") with a newly designed web based [GUI](/GUI "GUI") interfacing with [pgn4web](/index.php?title=Pgn4web&action=edit&redlink=1 "Pgn4web (page does not exist)") and [cutechess-cli](/Cutechess-cli "Cutechess-cli") [[2]](#cite_note-2) . [SMP](/SMP "SMP") capable engines with a minimum of supporting 8 cores were choosen to participate. [Openings](/Opening_Book "Opening Book") are from recent human high rated tournaments with a length of 8 moves selected by [Nelson Hernandez](/Nelson_Hernandez "Nelson Hernandez") alias *Cato the Younger*. [Time control](/Time_Management "Time Management") is 120 minutes for the whole game plus 30 seconds increment per move. [Stockfish 170514](/Stockfish "Stockfish") won conveniently versus [Komodo 7](/Komodo "Komodo") with +13=45-6 and **35½-28½**.

The Season 6 Superfinal between Stage 4 top two engines Stockfish and Komodo was played 24/7 from May 19 until May 30, 2014. Both engines were updated with improved versions, the upcoming Stockfish 5, officially Stockfish 170514, and Komodo 7x, just released at the start of the superfinal [[6]](#cite_note-6), and like Stockfish before, now supporting [Syzygy Bases](/Syzygy_Bases "Syzygy Bases"). After playing 64 games with 32 (+1?) 8-move openings played with reversed colors.
