**TCEC Season 1**,  
the first TCEC season directed and organized by [Martin Thoresen](/Martin_Thoresen "Martin Thoresen"), held from December 04, 2010 until February 06, 2011.
[Houdini 1.5a](/Houdini "Houdini") won the [nTCEC Season 1 Superfinal](#Superfinal) with a margin of **23ﾂｽ-16ﾂｽ** versus [Rybka 4.0](/Rybka "Rybka").
