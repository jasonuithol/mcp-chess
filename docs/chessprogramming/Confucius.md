**Confucius**,  
a chess program by [Martin Anders](/index.php?title=Martin_Anders&action=edit&redlink=1 "Martin Anders (page does not exist)") and [Hinrich Büker](/index.php?title=Hinrich_B%C3%BCker&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hinrich Büker (page does not exist)") [[2]](#cite_note-2),
developed in 1996 when the authors were graduate students at [Hamburg University of Applied Sciences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamburg_University_of_Applied_Sciences).
Confucius played the [IPCCC 1997](/IPCCC_1997 "IPCCC 1997"), running on a [Sun Ultrasparc](/SPARC "SPARC") [[3]](#cite_note-3).

# Etymology

In 1994, both authors spent half a year in [Shanghai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shanghai) as [exchange students](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Student_exchange_program),
which gave the inspiration to name the engine after the famous [Chinese philosopher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_philosophy) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
