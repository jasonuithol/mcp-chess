**Elektro**,  
an [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant chess engine by [Roberto Munter](/Roberto_Munter "Roberto Munter") alias Dr. Otto Schmidt, derived from [Igorrit](/Igorrit "Igorrit") of the [Ippolit](/Ippolit "Ippolit") series of programs, written for mere personal entertainment [[2]](#cite_note-2).
The Elektro Chess Engine site [[3]](#cite_note-3) introduces the engine as a parody of the pseudo [Soviet propaganda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propaganda_in_the_Soviet_Union) of the pseudonym Ippolit authors and [comrades](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comrade) [[4]](#cite_note-4).
