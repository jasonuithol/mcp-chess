Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/DOCCC_2002

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship](/Dutch_Open_Computer_Chess_Championship "Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship") \* DOCCC 2002**

**[< Prev](/DOCCC_2001 "DOCCC 2001") [Next >](/DOCCC_2003 "DOCCC 2003")**

The **22nd Dutch Open Computer Chess Championship** took place at [Denksportcentrum](http://www.denksportcentrumleiden.nl/) [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden). It was an eleven round [swiss tournament](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swiss-system_tournament) at two consecutive week ends, October 19-20 and 26-27, [2002](/Timeline#2002 "Timeline"). [Chess Tiger's](/Chess_Tiger "Chess Tiger") third title in a row, operated by book author [Jeroen Noomen](/Jeroen_Noomen "Jeroen Noomen") and [Hans van der Zijden](/Hans_van_der_Zijden "Hans van der Zijden").
