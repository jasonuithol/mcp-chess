**Gary Calnek**,  
a Canadian computer scientist and early computer chess programmer when he was undergraduate student at [University of Waterloo](/University_of_Waterloo "University of Waterloo") in the mid 70s. Along with [Ron Hansen](/Ron_Hansen "Ron Hansen"), [Russell Crook](/Russell_Crook "Russell Crook"), and [Jim Parry](/Jim_Parry "Jim Parry"), he was co-author of the successful mainframe programs [Ribbit](/Ribbit "Ribbit") [[1]](#cite_note-1) and its successor [Treefrog](/Treefrog "Treefrog") [[2]](#cite_note-2).
