Snellius Building [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**18th Computer Olympiad 2015**,  
from June 29, to July 05, [2015](/Timeline#2015 "Timeline"), Snellius Building [[2]](#cite_note-2) [[3]](#cite_note-3), [Leiden University](/Leiden_University "Leiden University"), [Leiden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leiden), [The Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands), in conjunction with the [WCCC 2015](/WCCC_2015 "WCCC 2015"), the [WCSC 2015](/WCSC_2015 "WCSC 2015"), and the [Advances in Computer Games 14](/Advances_in_Computer_Games_14 "Advances in Computer Games 14") conference [[4]](#cite_note-4) . The prize giving took place on July 01 and 02 after the official reception and excursion in [Museum Boerhaave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Museum_Boerhaave) [[5]](#cite_note-5) in central Leiden, and in the playing rooms of the Snellius Building on the other days. Additional medals for all prize winners were created by [Ingo Althöfer](/Ingo_Alth%C3%B6fer "Ingo Althöfer") with [Lego bricks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lego), his socks and his washing machine [[6]](#cite_note-6) .
