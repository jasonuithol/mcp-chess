# IPCCC 1991

Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/IPCCC_1991

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [IPCCC](/IPCCC "IPCCC") \* 1991**

**[Next >](/IPCCC_1992 "IPCCC 1992")**

The First International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**1st IPCCC**) took place from October 3-6, [1991](/Timeline#1991 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany).
