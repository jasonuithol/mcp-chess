**Pulsar**,  
a [Chess Engine Communication Protocol](/Chess_Engine_Communication_Protocol "Chess Engine Communication Protocol") compatible chess engine by [Mike Adams](/index.php?title=Mike_Adams&action=edit&redlink=1 "Mike Adams (page does not exist)"), written in [C](/C "C").
The development started in October 1998, and with help from people on [ICC's](/index.php?title=Internet_Chess_Club&action=edit&redlink=1 "Internet Chess Club (page does not exist)") channel 64, most notably [Kiwipete](/Peter_McKenzie "Peter McKenzie") and [Diepeveen](/Vincent_Diepeveen "Vincent Diepeveen"), Pulsar started its life in December and January of 1998/99 [[2]](#cite_note-2).
In June 2003 Pulsar has been released for the first time. In the meantime, Pulsar is available for [x86](/X86 "X86") and [x86-64](/X86-64 "X86-64") under [Windows](/Windows "Windows"), [Linux](/Linux "Linux") or [Mac OS](/Mac_OS "Mac OS"), as well as for [Mobiles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile_phone) and [Tablets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tablet_computer) under [iOS](/index.php?title=IOS&action=edit&redlink=1 "IOS (page does not exist)") and [Android](/Android "Android").
Pulsar plays orthodox chess, as well as various chess variants.
