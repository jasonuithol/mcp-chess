Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/IPCCC_1997

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Tournaments](/Tournaments_and_Matches "Tournaments and Matches") \* [IPCCC](/IPCCC "IPCCC") \* 1997**

**[< Prev](/IPCCC_1995 "IPCCC 1995") [Next >](/IPCCC_1998 "IPCCC 1998")**

The sixth International Paderborn Computer Chess Championship (**6th IPCCC**) took place from February 19-23, [1997](/Timeline#1997 "Timeline"), hosted by the [Paderborn University](/Paderborn_University "Paderborn University"), [Paderborn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paderborn), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany). The 1996 gap was caused by the change of schedule from October to February.
