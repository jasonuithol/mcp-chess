Jay Honnold [[1]](#cite_note-1)

**Jay Honnold**,  
an American computer scientist and software development engineer (SDE1) at [Amazon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amazon_(company)) [[2]](#cite_note-2).
He is author of the [UCI](/UCI "UCI") compliant [open source](/Category:Open_Source "Category:Open Source") chess engine [Berserk](/Berserk "Berserk"), first released in February 2021 [[3]](#cite_note-3).
