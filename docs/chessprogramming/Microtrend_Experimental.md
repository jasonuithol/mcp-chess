Source: https://www.chessprogramming.org/Microtrend_Experimental

**[Home](/Main_Page "Main Page") \* [Engines](/Engines "Engines") \* Microtrend Experimental**

**Microtrend Experimental**,  
an experimental chess program written by [Wim Rens](/Wim_Rens "Wim Rens"), who was associated with [Microtrend](/Microtrend "Microtrend"), to merchandize his [Gambiet 80](/Gambiet "Gambiet") program.
Microtrend Exp played the [European MCC 1981](/European_MCC_1981 "European MCC 1981") along with its stable mate *Gambiet 81*, and both programs achieved 2½ out of 5 [[1]](#cite_note-1).
